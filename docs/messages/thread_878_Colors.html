I love colorful stamps also! I have a set of Le Plume AND two sets of
12 color mini stamps made by Color Box. They come 12 colors in one
box, but each color pops out individually and you can get small refill
bottles for each color, so if your pads get dirty, you can clean them
and reink them. I think Y thought Kenna and I were crazy when we all
went hiking together and we pulled out a carload of colors! I get my
pads and inks and even the mini cats eye pads from Clearsnap.com.
Moab, of course, has nothing in it, so I've become a good online
shopper of this stuff!!!!

Hate to break it to you Es, but when I was living in Europe I never found
any pie except for the pizza variety. I hope you don't go into withdrawal. I
remember that the home that I was working for in Spain had a housekeeper,
and she saw me making an apple pie and got all excited. She told me about
how the previous person that had lived there (also Americans) once made a
dessert like that out of pumpkin. She sat there shaking her head, saying
"can you imagine? pumpkin! Who would think of using pumpkin?" She loved the
taste of it and couldn't believe their creativity to make something like
that out of pumpkin. I had to laugh a little at her amazement at what was
probably the family's Thanksgiving meal and so traditional to us.
Cadenza
