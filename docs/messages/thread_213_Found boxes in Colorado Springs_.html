Found boxes in Colorado Springs!

From: Rachel (rachelincolorado@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-01 23:02:17 UTC
Hello all!

Just wanted to say that we had an excellent day boxing in Colorado
Springs on Saturday - May 29th! Albeit we got a late start, AND we
went for some with long hikes, it was truly a great day! Luckily we
missed the rain!

Found:

"Chantress Letterbox" - Excellent rock formations!
"SoC#2: Hairstreak Butterfly" - loved the 'make like a troll' clue
"SoC#3: Lark Bunting" - First to find! Apparently we took a
shortcut, it turned out a quicker route with a storm looming upon us!
"Pulpit Rock" - EXCELLENT! If we had not eaten too much for lunch,
we would have gone further! I'll have to share a photo with you off-
list. I'm sure you'd approve - ha!

All of these are in great shape!

I'd also like to mention that JelyBean has EXCELLENT clues! We
really enjoyed figuring them out before finding the box! Keep up
the great work that you do with your stamps and clues! Kudos!

We're not done with power caching in the Springs! We WILL be back!
Say, I wonder if any of you CO. Springs boxers ever head to Denver
for boxing???? Would love to see your names in our logbooks!

Cheers!
Pumpkin Princess
(Higlands Ranch)
~~~If you plant them, we will come!~~~
