Got out to a few this weekend, tried to find hairstreak butterfly (but I never recheck
clues before I go out and realize its not posted anymore---was it pulled or missing?)
Obviously I didnt find it. I had fun looking tho-knew I must have been in the right area ..just gotta love those vague clues of yours JelyBean. But did find Maggie from there anyway. What a beautiful hike and area. And the stamp!!!

a flashlight Dixie!! That was a great hike...we came down on our butts-it was quicker and easier-sort of fun too. Just gotta love letterboxing...I would have NEVER known about that place and have passed it dozens of times. I camp 3 miles from there several times each summer.
I noticed that it was place on July 4 2004, JelyBean, did you go to the Manitou 4th of July
music and craft festival the day you placed it? I was there...had 2 booths.

I think I already mentioned that I finally went to the Onion about 10 days ago.

Now to pick my little brain for that Big Brain Box....
