Just a couple of reminders from your friendly list owner/moderator:

1) Attachments dont come through to the group at all. If you have a
file to attach, stick it in the files section, or cut and paste the
text into your post. This is to protect all the list members from
attached viruses.

2) Forwards are like attachments. Best to not hit "forward" but cut
and paste the text into your post or email to the group.

3) Snipping is a personal pet peeve. When you respond to someone's
post, please snip out any unnecessary parts of the email you're
responding to. It's not necessary to include that person's entire
message, and the one before that, and the one before that. :)
Subscribers who are getting the digest will have to weed through a
villion posts that start with >>> >>> >>> before actually being able
to read the next message. So, this is just courteous to your list-
mates.

4) Thanks to the moderators, we instituted a "first post is
moderated" system for this board. This means, the first time you put
a message to the group - we get it first and give it the okie dokie.
Why bother? Oh ... to reduce the number of instances where that
message invites you to invest in some stock or lengthen the size of
your member. Now ... if they were advertising pie? ;)

Thanks for helping to keep this list working so smoothly! And may
there always be a box at the end of your trail!

Autumn
RMLetterboxing List Owner
