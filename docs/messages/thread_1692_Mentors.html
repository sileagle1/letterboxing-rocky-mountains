Mentors

From: Aljan Sundance (AljanSundance@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-05-02 15:45:42 UTC-06:00
So, while I was chatting with the lovely Tai Chi at the workshop, she told
about a woman that she considered to be a mentor in her life. I love stuff
like that and it got me thinking. It's a bit off topic, but tell me about
YOUR mentor, not neccessarily in realtion to letterboxing, but someone who
inspires you.

What would you think about doing a box or stamp in that persons honor?
Maybe I will put together a box that is a bit like a HH hostel but with the
thought that stamps, and a story about the person that inspired them, may
just come and visit for while, and when you come to stamp in, who knows who
you might find there.

I am totally inspired by the tattoo artist, Kat Von D. I so want to be her
when I grow up. I not only love her artwork and am completely envious of
her ridiculous talents but I want what she has. Not the cars and the party
life and all that whatever, but the passion that she has, the zest and the
obvious live for the moment and love life attitude that she has. I watch
her on LA Ink and just think, that, right there, is something that I want.

I have an oh so cool stamp design forming in my brain. What do you all
think? Who wants to play?

Aljan
