Here are four more clues. Sadly, I think they muddy the waters rather
than clear anything up.

7. Kroeger's Ace Hardware is under it. ( ??? name of the local
hardware store)

8.There's always something happening and it's usually quite loud.
(Maybe another Hospital reference, or possibly the bandshell at the
park by the underpass)

9.Pink elephants on parade. (????what the????)

10.The Albuquerque Fox. (possibly referring to Kasa Fox Channel 2
from Albuquerque that we get around here)

I see a trend in the clues referring to something up in the air.

might be an antenna or something.

I might get up early and go snoop around the hatchery/hospital area.

dollars to spend at Kat's Giant Burgers and Frozen Custard, my
favorite new place in town. And I'm perfectly willing to share.
