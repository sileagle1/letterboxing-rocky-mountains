Think Spring Gathering
Eagle, Idaho

Hosted by Capperpillar

Jan 17, 2009 1:30 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
Eagle Public Library (Community Room)

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1065

Yes, it seems like winter is just getting started, but it's time
to "Think Spring" for our local boxes. Do you have boxes that need
some stamp and/or logbook maintenance done before spring? Or maybe an
idea for a new box or series? Others attending this event may even be
willing to let you help with some maintenance on their boxes. This is
a time to get together and work on getting those stamps carved and
logbooks made so our local boxes are in great shape for the spring!

This will also be a chance to meet (and exchange with) other boxers
in the area that you may not have met yet or haven't seen for a
while. Even if you haven't carved a stamp yet, this would be a great
opportunity to come and try it and maybe even get some hints from
other carvers. :)

Bring along any supplies and tools that you already have and would
like to use. It would be preferable if you already have your images
transferred or ready to transfer for carving. I can bring an iron,
acetone, and paper cutter if necessary.

This meeting time was determined by the availability of the Community
Room at the Eagle Public Library. If attendees are interested in
getting together someplace else before 1:30 for lunch and exchanges,
I'm open to that as a possibility.

Please RSVP so I know who all to contact in case of any changes (e.g.
the lunch thing, changes due to weather, etc.). AQ-mail Capperpillar
with any questions/comments/ideas for this event. Thanks!
