I "think" what you're describing is a virtual box. You follow clues
(same as a regular box, or in the case of a geocache, follow the
coordinates) and then take a digital photograph of the location. Then
you post the photo on the computer, or email the photo to
the "planter". This way, there is no box left in the area, and you
still get credit for a 'find'.

No, not as fun as a letterbox, but likely more environmentally
friendly.
