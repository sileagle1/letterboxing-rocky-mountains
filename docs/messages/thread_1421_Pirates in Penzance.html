From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-10-09 22:01:15 UTC
There is a very special fun in being on the other side of the world
and uncovering a familiar looking four by four inch container to find
a most excellent treasure left by someone you know. So, Bean, thanks
again for the outstanding morning the three Buccaneers spent poking
around the breathtaking area where Goddess in Scotland resides.
Woo!! There are a lot of stamps from you all in there! Did you send
it around before you left, Bean, or did you all meet up and stamp?
It was like running into old friends.

I also tucked a Nessie into a nook on the shore of Loch Ness. I'm
pretty sure I saw Nessie in the water, too. Pirates love Nessie.
Because. Come on. Water and castles and ships and monsters!

Pre.. I would hide that for you in a heartbeat but I'm leaving town
for Oregon soon. And won't be back til December. If you haven't
gotten it replaced by then I'll be happy to do it when I'm back!
I've had a missing report on Grizzly Creek and really wanted to get
up to check before I left but it's not looking like I will. I've got
to get the Mt. Doom boxes placed before I head out.

The gathering looks awesome! I wanna go next time!

I just read that article and it was great! Good job, Utah boxers!
That is certainly one of the better boxing articles I've seen! And,
speaking of Utah boxers, see you all soon on my cross country trek!
ARRRRR!!!

Es, unpacking and packing, jet lagged and needing pie

Es, If I recall correctly, (and I just turned 42 so I don't always
recall correctly) before I went I asked people if they wanted to send a
stamp or a HH along or something. Kinda like a care package from US
letterboxers to Scotland letterboxers. A few folks took me up on it
and sent signature stamps or HHs and stuff, and I sprinkled them around
a few boxes we found while there and of course the one I planted. So,
if any of you others get a chance to wander around the misty rolling
hills of Scotland, you might find a little Colorado here and there.
And now, it sounds like maybe even a little more than a little.
