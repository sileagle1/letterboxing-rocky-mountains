From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2006-03-07 02:19:31 UTC
Hello RMLetterboxers!
I just wanted to let you know of a minor change to our board that will
not affect any of you, but will affect any new members that join us.
Due to spam coming through the Yahoo system, we've moved to "First Post
Moderation".

What this means is that when a new member joins us and creates their
first post, it will be moderated. If it is appropriate (ie: to do with
letterboxing, pirates, or pie) it will post. If it isn't appropriate,
it will be deleted, as will (likely) the author.

So, please feel free to invite your local boxing friends to our list -
but tell them not to worry if their first couple of posts dont show up
for awhile!

Cheers, happy trails, and lots of pirate pie ...

Autumn
list owner, moderator, and all around nice chick *giggles*
