Stamp Held For Ransome!

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2007-09-26 21:21:43 UTC
Ok, here's a new one! This morning I went up to Corona Arch to do maintenance on the
"Layers" 4-box series (my favorites). In box #1, the stamp was gone and someone left the
following message:

" I have found your stamp and it is safe with me. I will return it next year possibly. To
whomever may find this note I am a traveller who is running out of time. Every year that
passes I fall deeper into debt and soon will I will no longer be able to travel / This note will
bring good fortune and health to you and everybody you know! If you could help me My
name is Joe. 847-971-6703

At the bottom he wrote his phone number so I can "help him". How totally bizarre is
that!!!!
It is written on a notepad from a Kinko's in Illinois. I would say to that thief, get a job, pal!

Box #2 was totally gone and will have to be recarved and rehidden in a new spot. Box #3
was still there, but hidden about 20 feet away from where it should be!

I also did maintenance at the "Moabman" letterbox and someone has taken one of the
stamps out of that box also.

I also checked on the "BMAO in Moab" box by Pre. Pre, your box is fine and in great shape
with about 1/2 the journal still empty!

Lulu & Ticaboo boxes were still in their spots, but that canyon flashflooded so hard last
weekend that it is unrecognizable and the clues will have to be rewritten. Lulu box had
been underwater, but was wedged in the crack so well with rocks that she survived the
flood! Thanks for rehiding well H-A's, you were the last to stamp in.

Whew, what a morning!!!
Kat
