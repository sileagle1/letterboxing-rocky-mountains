Now that you have found the location you can find the location
address in the phone book or on the web. Once you are at the location
you will head to the cross walk to the east of the building. You
will cross the street heading at 120 degrees towards a body of
water. Continue at 120 degrees (south east) and walk down the hill
on the grass towards a large group of rocks near the waters edge that
conceal a drainage spout. Watch your footing as some of the rocks
are loose. The box is hidden inside the triangle opening between the
boulders under a couple of river rocks. The cute little shop that is
there on the corner is open Monday thru Saturday 10 a.m. 7 p.m.
They are closed on Sundays. They are unaware of the box. This can
be a busy intersection depending on the time of day so please be
discrete and hide well.
