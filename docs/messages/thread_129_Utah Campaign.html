From: Ed Webb (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-25 16:13:59 UTC
Hi Folks-
This past weekend my kids and I took a letterboxing tour through
southwest Utah. Not many boxes, but a good excuse to get out and
travel through some wonderful country. I was amazed at how excited we
were when we approached the letterboxes. It seems like such a silly
pastime that we are involved in, and it is hard to explain to my
friends how much fun it is. Blowing a whole Sunday to drive around
looking for treasures that strangers have planted. Seeing the stamps
of other boxers that have now become familiar. And the stamps! I am
always so amazed at the clever creations of the hiders and the
seekers. What a great day.
Just some appreciative musings...
Durango Ed
P.S. Autumn, I have added some logistical info to the end of the
Engineer Mountain Box Clues. In case you want to copy them to your
archives.
Peace.
