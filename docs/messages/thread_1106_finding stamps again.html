artTrekker here (lurking in California)--

You should check the archives of the NorCal group, or get in touch
with Princess Lea, Amanda from Seattle or Green Tortuga, who went
to Dartmoor recently. I, too, had held it up as a holy grail (and
I do think that would be a cool part of the world to visit), but
have *gone off* the idea a bit since hearing some of their reports.
I guess the Brits' idea of letterboxing is a little different than
ours, and for someone who must have a hand-carved stamp (no matter
how crude, really), the pickins' are a little lean there. I love
the creativity that goes into most boxes here and would find myself
very disappointed indeed to be surrounded by thousands of boxes
all with sad commercial stamps in them. Water, water everywhere...

My Lb vacation wish list is mostly domestic: San Luis Obispo, CA;
upstate NY; Portland, OR, to name a few. And for you RM boxers, how
about a side trip to Lake Tahoe? Sure is lovely this time of year!
