Thanks for mentioning my Golden Hitchhiker Hostel. The "Big Bison
Hitchhiker Hostel" is an easy drive-by off I-70 at the Genesee Park
exit. As of today, there are three HHs in the hostel (two are NEW),
waiting for visitors to take them away to new destinations.

Bring a camera and pretend to be taking a photo of the wonderful view
as you nab the box. There are picnic areas in Genesee Park about 1
mile west of the overlook away that are perfect for stamping-in.
Please rehide it well.

I hope to visit your new Colorado Springs HHH next weekend and drop
off a few HHs from the Denver area.
