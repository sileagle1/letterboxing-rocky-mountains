CO - boxing report

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-28 04:54:04 UTC
Skeetch and I just did a road trip. Had a wonderful time and found
some wonderful boxes.

Box Canyon - Such a beautiful location. Safe and dry.

Wandering Aengus - Possibly the most beautiful box I've found to
date. Box, stamp, book, clues...the whole deal.

Andrews Lake - Had a hard time finding it, but finally grabbed it. A
bit wet, but safe.

Engineer Mountain - If you are ever in Durango, don't let the time
involved in this one keep you from it. All I can say about this hike
is.. wow! The wildflowers are beyond anything I've ever seen.. and
I've lived here many years. We saw a mountain goat, too!

The Three Keys - Safe and sound and way fun. My brain hurts.

Animas Overlook - Safe and dry and in a spectacular location.

Campus in the Sky - Safe and dry and lots of fun.

To Hell You Ride - We're 99.9% sure we were in the right place, but
were unable to find it. :(

We also found one that we can't discuss on the internet that was
really fun...and another that you have to discover for yourselves!

It was a great trip. I just want to add a note about how much we
enjoyed y-nought's boxes. So much time and thought..and great riddles
and clues!

Esmerelda, unpacking from a great trip
