Innocently Missing Out

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2004-12-17 16:21:04 UTC

Sooo, there I was yesterday, innocently making Christmas candy and
mailing packages (ok, so they don't make it in time, I know, I know,
just waiting until the last minute), when I felt the cosmic airwaves
whirring around. Little did I know that it was just my boxing
buddies online having a great day of chatting and now here I am,
starting the morning out with a great round of laughter at
yesterday's posts. Thank you everyone for such fun, even when we
aren't actually out there doing "our thing"!
Sooo, yesterday I gave a hiking buddy her Christmas present, which
was a journal, exacto pen, compass, and some ink dots, and you
should have seen her face. Such fun. I have dragged her out on many
a hike, now she is dragging me out to find boxes, starting last
night, we made her stamp, and she dragged me out to find one of my
boxes in the dark, (night hikes, always and adventure). And thus....
a new addict is born. She has been wanting to be involved for
months, but didn't really know where to begin. While fooing around
practicing with the knife, she ended up making a really fun stamp,
which will appear on a trail near us soon!!

Kat (putting an end to her morning babble session)
