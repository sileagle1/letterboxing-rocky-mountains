From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2005-04-07 15:47:36 UTC

Ok, here are some more directions in addition to the map, just in
case! Downtown is small, and mostly on a grid, so it's easy to find
anything, and anyone will help with directions!

Remember that the Colorado River is North of town, so if you are
heading into town driving South down Main St., the easiest way is to
turn Left (east)(towards the mountains) on 200 South and head to 400
East. 200 South will deadend on 400 East, then turn right and look for
Dave's Corner Market. Turn Left again, this is Millcreek, and Rotary
Park is just up this street on the right side, across from Redrock
Elementary School. You will be following the signs for the Slickrock
Bike Trail, which is a few miles farther up this road!

As of now, there is a winter storm coming on Saturday, with cold temps
and lots of wind. Sunday is supposed to get warmer and clear up. Now
keep in mind that our weather reports come from Salt Lake City, which
is 4 hours away, and they are not very accurate with our weather. It
could miss Moab altogether, cross fingers and let's give collective
happy weather thoughts into the airwaves!!!

Kat
