I sadly need to report that Alpine Pal appears to be missing just
weeks after Blackvelvet's sister planted it. It was a beautiful day
today, so Cappy and I decided to do some boxing. This one is not
too far from us. We were so disappointed because we know we found
the stack of rocks where it was hidden, it matched the clues to a
tee and the rocks were too stacked to be coincidental. It's a
heavily used trail and we wondered if somebody spotted it and took
it. We searched all around and even looked for possible people that
may be boxers that were stamping in, but no luck. So, until it can
be confirmed by Blackvelvet's sister that it is indeed missing, I
want to caution anyone else from making the trek. And, it is a bit
of trek uphill. We were glad we brought our hiking sticks...steep
and alot of loose rocks to negotiate.

Blackvelvet, let me know what your sister says!

Hope you all had a Happy Holiday! It's been beautiful here....this
is why I will never move from gorgeous state!

Wish you all a wonderful New Year full of memorable boxing!

Hugs around! Dixie Peach and Cappy
