Cody/Yellowstone vacationers

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-07-23 16:01:29 UTC
Because of bad weather in Denver, we were stuck in Cody for 5 hours
yesterday and had lots of time to chat with the locals at the
airport. I learned something that I thought I'd pass along, just in
case one of you planning to travel there through hasn't heard it yet.

I was told that the entrance to the park near Cody is closed and will
probably not reopen this year because of a mudslide. She said that
they had been blasting avalanches there last winter and something
about a possible explosive that hadn't detonated being buried in the
mud and making the clean up very slow.

I suspect y'all already know everything you need to know, but I
thought, just in case!

Esmerelda, hoping you all have a wonderful time
