Taco Soup for Triple T & SHH Mini Meet - May 8th, 2008

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-04-23 19:18:12 UTC
In honor of Triple T and SHH (and a car full of kids) ditching school
in Washington for a whirl wind letterboxing fieldtrip we are turning
on the crock pots and adding the spices to have a Taco Soup Mini Meet.

We will share signature stamps, personal travelers, plain sight boxes
and as always hitchhikers are welcome. Triple T and SHH like to
trade Letterbox Trading Cards so if you have a few whipped up bring
them along to trade. In addition Triple T has shared that she has
carved a special exchange stamp for the trip.

Kids are welcome and if time allows we may just try to help them hit
a few of our locally planted boxes in Eagle before returning to their
hotel in Nampa for the evening. They are on a tight time schedule
leaving town in the morning on their way to visit our fellow boxers
in Utah.

Thursday, May 8th, 2008
7:00 p.m. - ???
Eagle Road & Chinden, Bristol Heights Subdivision, Boise, Idaho
If you have not been to my house before e-mail me and I will send you
the address and directions.

Blackvelvetrav
