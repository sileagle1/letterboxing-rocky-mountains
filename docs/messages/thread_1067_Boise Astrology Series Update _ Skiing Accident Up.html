Last time out there were two Astrology boxes missing. Box # 10 Virgo
& Box # 11 Sagittarius. Today (4/9/06) I replaced box # 10 Virgo with

He may be back to work again as soon as Tuesday or Wednesday at the
latest. I really appreciate all the support!
