NM Road Trip Report

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-10-06 16:02:09 UTC
I took a road trip to New Mexico this past weekend. On the way down,
I got the Fox Run Park box in Colorado Springs - beautiful park,
worth the drive. My friends in NM are hooked on the sport after two
finds and one attempt. I carved them stamps as gifties and they
can't wait to go again.

Disappointed not to have more time to box while in NM. Balloon
Fiesta events prevented us from getting one box. We did find the
Domingo Baca box, and the Embudo Canyon box - but the Los Vaqueros
box is indeed (as Silver Eagle reported) missing - or just really,
really hard to find, eh?

Sorry SE - we didn't have time to head up to your Sandia Crest box,
but I will get that one on my next trip. I came home early because I
was very sick while I was there. On my way home, I attempted
Funhog's Rancho de Estrellas box, but could not find it.

Cheers,

Autumn
P10 F33 X24 V29
