I think Y said it all! What an exciting day! And to think, some are
out there boxing again today!

Thank you sooooo much, everyone who put in the miles to come play
with us! I think fun was had by all, and we tried hard to stuff
Elros and DSOTM with as much pie as was possible.

And to those who brought along non-boxing spouses and friends, thank
them for their patience during our frenzy of stamp exchanges. My
head is still reeling from that experience! LOL!

And.... thank you, Y, for your beautiful event stamp, clap clap
clap, well done! It was so good to finally meet you and the boys in
person!

And....... oh boy, new stamps being planted. How sooo cool is that!
I can't wait to go find the one at Little Wild Horse Canyon! It's
ALWAYS good to have a reason to play in that canyon!

And..........It's good to realize that there are people out there
that possibly have a deeper addiction to this than myself! Wow! LOL!

Thanks everyone for sooo much fun and your beautiful stamps!
