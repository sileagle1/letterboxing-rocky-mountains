From: HER (fauxsum@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-04-14 03:16:39 UTC
A big giant yipeee to FRH and allemeyn for being our hostesses with
the "mostest-iness" for putting togther this event. Thanks also to
them, Tai Chi and Chai Tea and leviathan for placing boxes for us all
to find. It pleases me to no end that so many folks have an interest
in coming Denver proper! It was great to meet some new friends and
visit with some old ones.

I've been going to Doors Open Denver every year since it started and
starting two years ago attempted to try and get boxers interested in
some of the fascinating history, culture, architecture that Denver and
this event have to offer. Two years ago there were 3 of us wandering
the streets (and one of them was my non-boxing friend). Last year
about 15 boxers showed up, and in the neighborhood of double that this
year :-)

A toast and some pie to you ALL!! May this become an annual
tradition. The DOD location list this year was particularly well-put-
together by the Mayor's Office of Cultural Affairs.

Does anyone want to share highlights from the weekend?

For me it was *finally* touring the Molly Brown house after all these
years of passing by it sometimes twice a day (and having a letterbox
there). Our guide was the world's fastest talker. Also seeing the
progress being made at the old Elitch Theatre. The first time I toured
it was in the dark while dodging leaky ceiling areas and fox poop left
by the only residents at that time. It's going to be amazing when that
place re-opens to the public.

Thanks for a great weekend everybody!
