Wow - Es really outdid herself! I hate to increase the "regret
factor" for anyone who could not attend, but it was a really great
time. Es's house was decked out in banners and her yard was covered
in Lord of the Rings letterboxes. She'd made each of us a special
logbook, a book of clues, a map of Mt. Doom and a nametag with the
event stamp design on it. We had a great time having dinner (and of
course pie!) and stamping ourselves silly. It was a great evening of
seeing old friends and meeting new ones.

Mt. Doom was amazing today - there were SO many amazing boxes and, of
course, more decorations and fun on the mountain. I had a great day
of inky fun with the Boxtops. I hit F250 somewhere along the way, but
to be honest I'm not sure exactly where. I kinda lost count.

Thanks so much, Es, for sharing your talents and your hospitality. It
was a spectacular event!!
