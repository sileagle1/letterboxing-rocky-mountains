Help Request - Updating LBNA and Atlasquest

From: kpmboxers (kminfo99@earthlink.net) | Date: 2007-09-03 21:43:21 UTC
I'm finding that keeping both these sites updated, especially after
getting over 50 stamps at the Golden Treasures Gathering, to rather
tedious and very time consuming.

Do any of you have any tips on easy ways or shortcuts to updating
these sites? Basically I am doing searches on AQ for each individual
stamp, recording the find, then going over to LBNA, repeating the
search and recording the find there, if it exists. As far as I can
tell there's no way to determine whether an LB'er has posted to AQ,
LBNA, or both.

LBNA, IMHO, is more tedious. Their search engine isn't as user
friendly as AQ and I just find the site harder to use in general.

I thought of just dropping the update of LBNA, but I'm sure there are
many LB'ers who use it exclusively.

Any hints or help would be appreciated!

Kip
kpmboxers
