Early on this baseball season, I created a letterbox in honor of the late and great announcer of the Philadelphia Phillies, Harry Kalas. This box was available to letterboxers at all the home games I attended this past season. With this year's season coming to an unfortunate close for Phillies fans, I am now ready to send Harry on a postal journey to reach Phillies fans near and far that weren't able to get to any of the games I was at. If you have an interest in getting Harry as a postal letterbox please send me your name and address in a message on Atlasquest or to my email: fishthis@hotmail.com. The message will end up in my junkmail box so be sure to put HARRY THE K in the subject line.

Thanks!
