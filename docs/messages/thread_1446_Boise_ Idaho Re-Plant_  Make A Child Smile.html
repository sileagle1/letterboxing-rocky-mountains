11/17/2007 Box Re-Planted. Totally New Location.

"It's not how much we do...but how much love we put into doing it!"

Make a Child Smile Organization: www.makeachildsmile.org

The stories of courageous kids that you'll read on this website will
touch your heart. Here, kids with life threatening illnesses are
featured monthly.

We hope that each person or family that finds this box will log on to
the site and mail a card to the Featured Kids (3) of the Month. You
can also check out the Previous Kids section and send cards to kids,
who have been featured in the past, they also look forward to
receiving mail. Featured children are not the only ones who benefit
from the mail. It touches the whole family! Even the children's young
siblings end up receiving their own mail! Some kids are too young to
really understand where the cards and gifts are coming from, but it
makes their parents happy to know that strangers they never met, care
about their child and are sending their love and support.

Do not wait too long to send your cards or postcards because time is
of the essence in these children's lives. Many of these children
become angels at a very young age and cards may also be sent to help
comfort their families.

Directions: Locate the Park and Ride in Eagle that is at the
intersection of State Street, River Side Drive and State HWY 44.

Clues: Enter the Park and Ride at the east entrance and park as
close to the east as possiable. To the east of your parking spot you
will note a grass area with a waterfall of river rocks and a few
larger boulders. Three small steps from the sidewalk you will see a
large flat boulder. This boulder is to the north not the one to the
south. Hidden under this boulder consealed by river rocks you will
find your hidden treasure. Please re-hide well as this can be a busy
corner.

Thank you for making a child smile! Should you note the box missing
