Thanks murmur, but awww shucks. Really, all I did was put out the
welcome mat on a weekend when a small group of us woulda been out
exploring anyway.

Wow oh wow though - did we get a better turn out than I could have
ever dreamed! AND the most incredible weather possible (3 for 3
years - yay!) I think I got a sunburn...can summer be far behind?

Thanks to EVERYONE for coming to Denver this weekend. It thrills me
to no end that there was this much interest in the Mile High City.

Hope you all had a blast exchanging and 'boxing and exploring.

Who knows? Maybe we've just started an annual tradition?

Someone else will hopefully have pictures to post, 'cause I sure
don't have any....

So anybody have some D.O.D. building exploring they'd especially
like to share with us? For me it was going to the top of the Daniels
& Fisher Clocktower building. This was the first year it was open
for DOD - a 360 view on such a clear day - wow! Unicorn Girl and
Trainboy liked the elevator ride.
