possibility that it is there, but out of the container and pushed back
farther than an arm can reach. So. if you go in search of this box, you may

I don't make it to that area more than 2-3 times per year. However, I am
HOPING I can check it out when we go downtown on May 6 to see Les Mis.



Thanks!
