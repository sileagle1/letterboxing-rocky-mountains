Tornado in Colorado...Is Everyone Okay?

From: (scott_katytalbot@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-05-23 23:12:53 UTC
Thank you all for your concern. I was very worried yesterday during the tornado because I could not get a hold of my family but everyone is safe. We were without power until last night, which is great compared to the rest of town. Comcast recently came back up around noon today but we still don't have any phone service. The only damage to our home is due to hail but is minimal compared to what the folks on the other end of town. If you have never been to Windsor, it's not a huge town and is only about 15 blocks wide. The tornado took out a lot of homes between 1st and 7th. Luckily we sit around 11th and the house is till standing. I drove to the other end ot town this morning and can't describe how bad things look.

For a nice letterboxing clue, the tornado headed straight over the Stanley Manor Murder Mystery box #1 and headed northwest over the location of "The Locomotion". The Locomotion was pulled at the time, however I do not have a status of the Stanley Manor box. The homes that were most effected by the tornado are those standing between those two locations.

Ramdelt
