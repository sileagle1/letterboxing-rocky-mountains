I'll be at the faire gates when they open at ten. But it does seem
like a shame to come back out already at noon.

The only reason I thought we should be in the park at all was to give
us a chance to have pie. (I'm not sure, but I don't think they would
let us bring any in). If we don't worry about pie we could share
eclaires inside the realm and people could come and go as they please.

The other idea would be to meet at the park later, say two o'clock or
two-thirty, after the reveling inside the gates. This would be good
in case there be gatherers who don't want to go inside the gates with
all the crazies.

What say ye?
