From: Beth (baliddle@sbcglobal.net) | Date: 2007-12-08 19:30:11 UTC
Hi there! I'm about to make my annual trek to the Denver area. Last
year was my first visit there as an official 'boxer.... and you had
something like 85 feet of snow! I did manage to find one box between
blizzards. I'm hoping this year will be much more "typical" and have
good boxing conditions for me while I'm there.

I printed out a ton of clues last year which I still have. I just
verified them to make sure they're still active/accurate and I
printed out a bunch of new clues. There are way more boxes than I
can possibly hope to find! Does anyone have suggestions for "must
find" boxes within maybe a 1/2 hour drive? I'll be in Littleton (Ken
Caryl & 470 area). Feel free to email me directly if you don't want
to post here (baliddle @ sbcglobal . net) <~~ remove spaces

Also, I was wondering if anyone would like to meet up for some
exchanges and general BSings, maybe drinks or dinner? (I have LTCs,
too, if anyone trades those) I'm thinking sometime between the 27th-
29th? I'm open to suggestions, but I thought that time frame might
be best, let the hub-bub die down a little. Let me know if you're
interested and if you have any suggestions for times and places.

Thanks,
BNL BaliGrl
