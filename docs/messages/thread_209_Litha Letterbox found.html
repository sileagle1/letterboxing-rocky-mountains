From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2004-05-31 21:14:09 UTC-06:00
I haven't had a chance to get up to Dakota Ridge yet this season. The Litha
box was reported missing TWICE, but it turns out that it was simply stolen,
and moved.
Thankfully some WONDERFUL geocachers replaced the box, and provided the
clues you'll see below. If anyone goes up after this box, or the Flying
Cloud (on the same trail) - please let me know if you find the box alive and
well.

Thanks -
Autumn

> We went out GeoCaching today and on our way down from a find we stumbled
across your letterbox. We thought it was another GoeCache, so we went to
take a look. After signing the book and moving on, we came home to look it
up. We discovered that we found it in another location then the one you have
listed. It notes on there that it was stolen. We hid it better then when we
found it and we also took GPS readings as to where it was. Park at the
Stagosaurus lot off of State Road 26 south of I-70. Take the trail from the
south end of the lots going up. At the top of the ridge you will see
shooting range warnings on the left side. There is a gate on the left side
when the trail levels out. The east side of the trail is a low rock wall,
the letterbox is hidden at the southend of the first bush under some rocks.
>
> GPS coordinates:
> N 39 41.667 W 105 12.004
> Elevation: 6639 feet
