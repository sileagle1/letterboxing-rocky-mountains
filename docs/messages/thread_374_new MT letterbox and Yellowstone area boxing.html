found the box (thanks for the compliments, by the way. I am very

A good note is that if you are headed up this way, to remember that
Yellowstone covers parts of MT, ID, and WY. Don't forget to look at
other states. For instance, the Chief Joseph box you asked about is
only about 10 minutes from West Yellowstone, MT, but is located in
Eastern Idaho. If you go just a few miles into the park (the other
direction) you are in Wyoming. There are quite a few boxes outside
of the parks in various states, if you just look in all the spots.

Hope you enjoyed your trip!
