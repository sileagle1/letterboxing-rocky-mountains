Skunked Twice!

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-01 03:17:53 UTC
My sons and I went after two boxes this weekend and came back
with nothing. One box had only been planted for a few weeks but all
we found were scattered remains. The other we couldn't find because,
well, we just couldn't find it. We did have fun, though.
Sometimes I wish I was a little better at finding boxes. And
then I remember that it is amazing this works as well as it does. To
have a box hidden so no one else will find it, but can be found with
a couple of paragraphs (or less) of directions. Sometimes it turns
out the directions aren't enough (for me anyway). And sometimes the
box is too easily found and it turns up missing. It isn't that easy
to get it just right and it makes me wonder how some of my boxes will
do in the long run.
y-nought
