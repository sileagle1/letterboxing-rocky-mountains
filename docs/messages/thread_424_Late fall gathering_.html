Late fall gathering?

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2004-11-12 07:58:11 UTC

I'm astounded at the growth of letterboxing in our Rocky Mountain
region, and particularly this list. I can remember being excited
when we had a whopping twelve folks here. I can remember also our
first little gathering at Roxborough Park at the Fountain Valley
box. What a little group we were back then! (photos still in the
photos section on the board)

It's exciting to see new boxes being planted, and hitch hikers from
across the country making their way as well. It also got me to
thinking about the fact that we haven't had a gathering in quite
awhile. Didn't manage to line one up this summer.

Would anyone be willing to suggest a location with a box or two,
where we could meet and exchange stamps and get a box together as a
group?

Autumn

From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2004-11-15 18:18:39 UTC

We would love to make it to a fall gathering. We missed the last
one, and were oh-so-bummed!

I like castlewood canyon, however, in case of inclement weather, we
may want to consider somewhere we could flee to the indoors in case
we picked a day that ended up very cold.

Thanks to the Treasure Finders, there are several very nice boxes in
the Littleton/Golden area, and I'm sure we could find a nice meeting
spot.

I don't know about anyone else, but our group also doesn't have ANY
boxes from the Ft. Collins area - that could be a nice change of
scenery. Anyone from up in that general direction have any
suggestions?
