From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2004-10-14 13:40:21 UTC

Hey Staci!

I wrote an email to the State parks of Colorado to see what I needed
if I wanted to put a letterbox somewhere. This is what they said:

Thank you for your inquiry into Colorado state parks' policy
concerning
geocaching and letterboxing. As far as I am aware, there are no
regulations with respect to these hobbies. However, I am certain that
these
activities would be allowed in our parks provided that the animals and
environment would not experience a disturbance.

If you have additional questions, or if I can be of any more
assistance,
please feel free to contact me. Thank you.

Cherylynne Gerton
Administrative Assistant III
Colorado State Parks

I would imagine you could get info from other states the same way.
Good luck with that!

~RavenWolf~
