Re: [RMLetterboxing] Re: Replacement Speedball Tips

From: Jana J. Riska (jjcadenza@gmail.com) | Date: 2005-04-19 11:43:45 UTC-06:00
I don't know the Denver area, but I've never gone in a Michaels that didn't
have some of what I needed. You just have to know where to look and trust
that the people working there won't have a clue what you are looking for. I
agree with A.S. Have you looked in the fine arts section by brushes,
pencils, and such? If you ask for brayers for rolling ink, they are a bit
more recognizable and are often stored by carving materials. Although, that
is one item that does sometimes get places by stamps too.
Good luck!
Cadenza
