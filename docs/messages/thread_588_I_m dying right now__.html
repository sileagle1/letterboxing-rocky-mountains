Okay, I just had a realization that is killing me. I had looked at my
calendar for the 10th and 11th to make sure that I wasn't missing

realized that I have a symphony concert I am performing in Saturday
night! I can't believe this!

Now, be it known. At this point there is little short of death that
could stop me from going to the gathering. I will not miss it, but it
is about an eight hour drive from here. I was so looking forward to
going after my earlier committment on Sat morning and boxing on the
way down along with camping that night. I don't think I would do well
with trying to do it all on Sunday morning. I think I will have to
head out on Saturday night after my concert is over--I will skip the
usual shmoozing we do afterwords with the patrons. Then I will have
to stay in Salt Lake and get up early in the morning to finish the
drive.

This is going to be a bit of a pain and it totally puts kinks in my
plans for driving and boxing. Aryn, it looks like you may want to go
down earlier after all and meet up with others. I'm so sad! I may
have to just bring my stuff along and see if anyone has room for one
more tent at their site when I get to the gathering.

Aahhh!
Cadenza
