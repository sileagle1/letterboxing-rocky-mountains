There were these Two Guys......

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2005-03-20 20:46:46 UTC

There were these two guys all right, with 2 dogs, and they were
camped out on the log next to box #2 of the "There Were These Two
Guys.... Letterbox" all afternoon. I love this new series by
Esmerelda. I found box #1, passed by box #2 "temporarily" because
there were so many people frisbee golfing nearby. Found #3, went
back to #2, only to find that two guys were setting up lunch and
fishing stuff on the log. Dohhhhh..... so sad I am, still not
knowing the whole story of "These Two Guys"! I won't get to find out
the whole story for another few weeks, when I'll try it again!
Thanks Es, another FUN box to be had by all!

Kat
