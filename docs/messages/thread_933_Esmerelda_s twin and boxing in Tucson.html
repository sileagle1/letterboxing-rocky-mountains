From: Renee and Dave (haipnsuch@juno.com) | Date: 2005-11-21 06:54:07 UTC-07:00
Such a small world! So, now I know that if I meet someone that looks
like Es, but seems confused by my friendly waving ... it's probably her
sister. Ramdelt, just wait until you meet Skeetch ... he's another
really nice guy. Those buccaneers sure choose well!

Tucson boxing is a lot of fun. We were down there last Christmas and
again in the spring. We saw Ramdelt's stamp here and there from their
last Thanksgiving trip. While we don't have family down there, I was
at the U of A for nearly a decade and still have some good friends in
that territory. I moved back here to marry another really nice guy ...
Yoque.

To anyone who gets a chance to box in Tucson ... Everything that Kristal
and Ron and AzRoadie put out is worth looking for ... ditto for Funhog
who has a few of them there. Ramdelt, did you get Funhog's Happy
Javelina yet? It's a great stamp, but the crosscountry hiking through
cholla was pretty treacherous with kids.

Have a great Thanksgiving everyone!

~Silent Wings of the Catamount Carvers, dreaming about towering saguaros
