I made two books today!

From: aeckert1 (aeckert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-09-20 23:26:52 UTC
Last weekend the Catamount Carvers encouraged me to try the book
binding tutorial on Atlas Quest. I spent this past week collecting
supplies and courage and today I tried it. The results look a lot like
logbooks! I still have room for improvement, to be sure, but I was
pleasantly surprised by how the books turned out.

Count me among those eagerly awaiting the workshop so I can learn some
more and improve on what I've done!! And thanks for the encouragement,
Catamounts!!

If any of you want to check it out, it's here:

http://www.atlasquest.com/tutorials/logbooks/codex/

It takes some time (especially the first time) but if I can do it,
anyone can! The instructions are really clear and the pictures are
helpful.

allemeyn

From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2008-09-21 06:40:35 UTC
YAY you!!

I'll have you know that I used that exact same tutorial to make two of
my logbooks for Mt. Doom. =3

It really isn't hard. Time consuming - a little. Especially that first
time. But certainly not hard! The hardest part is coming up with the
idea for how the finished product is going to work itself out. I start
with something simple, and then my ideas take on lives of their own and
I end up with saudered wire spider webs! =3

I am always more than happy to answer questions about any of the books
that I've made. Glad you had fun with it! I am anxious to see some of
your new art out there in tupperware land.

~RW
