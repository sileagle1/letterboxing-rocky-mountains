Winter Sports

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-05 19:48:08 UTC

Hello folks-

I was able to find a few letterboxes this weekend even though they
were covered with snow. Doc Holiday is alive and well at his
gravesite and Grizzly Creek is doing fine too. I also stamped into
the new journal for Big Fish (very nice!).

It was challenging to find these because, well, it's hard to see the
trail much less the landmarks. Luckily there was nobody at these
boxes but me, so I could not only snoop around discreetly, but also
sweep the snow back over my tracks when I was done.

I had a great time and, as always, really enjoyed the clues, stamps
and journals in these Spledid Buccaneer boxes. I also think this
qualifies me as a Maniac Sick-in-the-Head Letterboxer which has been
my goal all year.

Box On!
y-nought
