Hello --

I'm still doing a happy little skipping thing now and then because I

night. Ravenwolf, Samurai Stamper, Little Wolf, Sparkle Fairy and
Brother Raven are just delightful and seem to be having a great time

Pirates!

From: Paige (Paigelinda@aol.com) | Date: 2005-06-14 19:47:31 UTC
Hi Y! (Hey I made a rhyme, gimme a dime...) We had a ball talking
with you too! I see Ravenwolf has posted our finds and whew! what a
load! It was amazing. Every box had a beautiful view or interesting
story behind it. It was also loads of fun to read the log books and
see who else had been there before us. We are already planning some
trips around our hometown to both find and plant.

Brother Raven is working on his carving skills so the "plants" will
have handcarved stamps in them. Don't get me wrong, there is nothing
wrong with store bought (some of them are so kewl!) but the
handcarved add that little extra something. I will be making all the
log books we plant as that is one of my areas for creative release.
Will post them as soon as we get them planted. Try not to get too
excited just yet for their release into the wild as we have a lot of
yard work to do first but its coming along. :0)

Thanks to All who plant boxes! What a wonderful hobby! I'm so glad
we found each other.

sparkle Fairy
