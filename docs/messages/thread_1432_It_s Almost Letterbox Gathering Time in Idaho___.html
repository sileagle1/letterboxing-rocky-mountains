Mini Idaho Gathering this Sunday, October 28th at 12:30 p.m.

I have been as busy as a beaver carving some fun Nightmare Before
Christmas images and a few other surprises along the way.

We will be having Taco Soup, Cornbread and some Halloween sweets for
lunch. I do have ice tea on hand but if you have a favorite beverage
(hot or cold) please bring it along.

the wilds with special clues for the boxes that I have created. I
will also be printing clues to any of the boxes that may already exist
near the newly planted boxes. I will be posting the Nightmare Before
Christmas boxes on both Atlas Quest and Letterboxing.Org. However, I
will not reveal the actual clues to the boxes to the general
letterboxing community until after our Gathering.

See you soon!!! Blackvelvetrav
