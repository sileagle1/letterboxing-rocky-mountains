y - a coupla thoughts on Cobras and Sharks:

- These are both the names of classic sports cars (Chevy Corvette C3
Shark and Ford Mustang Cobra) - so perhaps a car show?

- Cowboy boots can be made of both shark skin and snake skin and are
correspondingly called "Sharks" and "Cobras"

- Golf shops would have both Cobra clubs and items by Greg Norman,
who goes by the nickname "Shark" and whose products bear a shark logo
(smething you might find in a sporting goods store)

- Mephisto footwear for men makes hiking sandal styles called both
Cobras and Sharks (which you might also find at a sporting goods
store
