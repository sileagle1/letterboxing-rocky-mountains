Boxing Report

From: Lisa Cook (lisadcook@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-04-26 15:45:27 UTC

Hi all,
Went boxing the w/e of Apr 16-17 and found the following:
Grizzley Creek (in Glenwood Canyon)-actually I just went to the
pool for the day and had to make 1 lb stop on the way. What
a beautiful detour on the way to the pool. Saw a lot of stamps from
people returning from the Box on the rocks. As usual a beautiful stamp
and logbook from our local pirate.
On the 17th I found Hikers Boot in Roxborough. You all need to go
there-it is very lonely/only a few stamps in it. Its a beautiful hike.
Also found Super uno guy and Nocturnal Scorpion.
And I left a little surprise somewhere!

This Sat (weather permitting) Puppy Paws and I plan on a mega box day
in the Springs.
