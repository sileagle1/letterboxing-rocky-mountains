Animas Overlook and Celtic Tree

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-13 14:41:22 UTC
This is mostly for JeLyBean because he is planning a visit, but I also
thought I would let everyone else know.

I called the Forest Service about the road to Animas Overlook and they
said that the tentative opening date for that road is MAY 1! Their
website lists it as open now, but I checked last evening and it is
indeed closed. I'm in the process of being a pest and trying to nudge
them toward an earlier opening since we are having record high temps
and I'm sure the snow is melting fast. We'll just have to see.

I also finally got around to putting up revised clues for Celtic Tree
after Tie Dye Girl told me about the new parking construction. In the
process I found the container full of water (mixed with green ink).
Happily the logbook baggie worked perfectly and all was saved. (The
inkpad might be a bit weak, though).

Happy Spring Thaw Everyone,

y-nought
