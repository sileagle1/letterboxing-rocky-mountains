almost lost my sig stamp this weekend!

From: aeckert1 (aeckert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-10-20 18:30:24 UTC

I guess it serves me right for joking about my sig stamp being lost or
stolen by a giant scary eagle or the like. (But I was SO envious of
Autumn's great new creation - and I'm even more envious now that I
have the new stamp in my logbook!)

I was doing TaiChi and ChaiTea's Blue Spruce series. I stamped into
the first one but when I got to the second, my stamp was gone. I was
starting to think that I'd invited bad luck by kidding around about
something happening to it. I was also thinking that nobody would
believe it was an accident after how I went on and on about my stamp
being lost. Fortunately, I went back to the first box and found it on
the ground there - I guess it fell out of my bag.

The moral of the story, I guess, is don't invite bad stamp karma or
you may get it!

allemeyn
