Thanks for all the great paper recommendations! - Keep 'em coming!

I realized in reading them that I should have shared my own
recommendation for scoring fantastic fabric for anyone who wants to do
fabric covers. It's an online store only based in Boulder called
www.equilter.com They are the best I've found for what, in quilting
cirlces (ha) is called "novelty fabric." If you do a search on their
site with those key words you'll be amazed what they have to fit just
about any theme.
