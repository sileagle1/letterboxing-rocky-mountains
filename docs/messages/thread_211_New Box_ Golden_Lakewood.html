The stamp is fairly large 2 1/2 x 3 (ish) inches.

C
Placed by: RavenWolf
Placement date: May 9 2004
State: Colorado
County: Jefferson
Nearest city: Golden/Lakewood
Number of boxes: 1

Clues
Placed by RavenWolf, Little Wolf, and the Samurai Stamper
Carved by RavenWolf

Distance: Maybe 1/2 mile round trip
Difficulty: Easy (We did it in sandals.)
Kid and Canine friendly

Locate the Welchester Tree Grant Park. Probably the best way to get
there is to exit onto Indiana from 6th. Turn onto 7th and follow it
around (it will eventually become 8th). The park is on your Left.
Park on 8th, there are places near the stoplight, or you can park in
the neighborhood nearby.

Follow the cement path to the large wooden bench turn right.

Into the Woods.

Out of the Woods.

Down the stairs.

1/2 way between stair and fence, a sentinel looms to the right.

Sneak behind the giant, as his brothers scratch and grab trying to
prevent your passage.

But for those that are loyal and brave, C lies in wait under bark
and leaf.
