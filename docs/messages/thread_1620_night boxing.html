Okay, Pre, I'm butting in here from NC.
The CO folks are depending on you to bring nightboxing to CO!!
Could it be that you are making this project too big?

I planted the first night box in NC in 2005 with pieces of reflector
tape in an old slave cemetery that you could get through the gates at
night. It's practically a driveby.

Not much to that one but everyone LOVED it and it sparked the GA
folks to start nightboxing and take it to a whole new level.

People still enjoy that old, simple 2005 box for the setting. So I
say JUST DO IT! Just put out one so people get the idea and then it
may just take off.

I'll be taking Front Range Hiker to do some nightboxing here-- simple
ones --only one of which has reflector stuff (the others are just
accessible day or night).

Set a deadline, no excuses, someone plant ONE nightbox by Halloween.
