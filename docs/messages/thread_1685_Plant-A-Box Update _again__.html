Plant-A-Box Update (again!)

From: Autumn (autumnkat@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-04-21 14:10:49 UTC
What a success!
So far (through 4/20/09 based on what's been posted) we have 51 boxes planted by folks who have signed up for the "event" and another 17 boxes planted by folks who are probably unaware of the event, but I'm counting them anyway ... just cuz. 68 new boxes in our region, folks!

There are hitchhikers and bonus boxes out there - and incredible new series to find! I know there are more on the way, just based on what folks have been emailing me (and the fact that I have a box in my bag waiting to be planted)!

Thank you to everyone who has been planting, carving, clue-writing, logbook making, and lock 'n lock shopping!

Three events are happening -
4/25 - Ravenwolf is hosting a workshop
5/2 - a Utah gathering and a Denver gathering
Atlas Quest has the details on those things, or email me if you want the skinny.

AutumnsFolly
