A couple of years ago I did a trip where I found a few boxes in some
fairly deep snow. On the plus side....there was nobody hanging around
wondering what I was doing, and I was able to use tree boughs to sweep
the powder back in place so I wouldn't leave behind a trail to the
boxes.

On the negative side, I blew right past a "fork in the trail" because
it wasn't visible and it took me a while to deduce where one box was.
And I had to keep my ink in an inside pocket so the 5 degree cold
wouldn't harm it (I guess it had to freeze on the page anyway....).

I imagine some urban boxes might be available depending on what the
maintenance people do. Some areas get shoveled out...while some areas
are used to stack snow.

So good luck Beth, I hope you are able to find something,
