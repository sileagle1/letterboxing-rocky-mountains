From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-08-24 10:32:15 UTC-06:00
Being the anal person that I am, I have created a binder that has
letterbox clues for all of the boxes in CO. (My husband wished I could
be this orgnaized with the bills and house papers! LOL).

Anyway - I am working on labeling them yes or no based on if we can take
the kids with us. Matthew is 2 and Maggie is 3 1/2. They are pretty
good hikers. This last weekend they were able to go with us to plant
The King of All He Surveys (and we continued on this trail after
planting the box - so probably a good 3/4-1 mile total walking).. then
we went to lunch and out to hide Swimming in Opposite Directions (.67
miles each way - most of which was flat).

Soooo with all of that, I am looking for information on the following
boxes (the clues are not enough to tell me if the kids can make it):

Coyote Canter
Sod Village Pioneer Adventure
Abert's Squirrel LB & Parasite Box
Bear Creek
Chantress
SoC #1
SoC #2
Star Box
The Strangest Bird
No Use
Shining Star
Zeke's
Deer Creek
Wings of Eagles
Rainbow Trail
Onion

I think I have figured out the rest - most of which are no's (we have
found most of the yeses in Golden, Littleton & Morrison.)

Any help would be greatly appreciated! =]
