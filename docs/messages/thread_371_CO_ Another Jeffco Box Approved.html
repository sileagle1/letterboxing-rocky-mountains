CO: Another Jeffco Box Approved

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2004-10-03 19:10:55 UTC

Hi, Colorado 'boxers. I received approval yesterday from the JeffCo
Open Space people for my stegosaurus box near Morrison, so I am
officially publishing the clues:

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.asp?
boxnum=11280&boxname=SoC_#5:_Stegosaurus

They asked me if the box was in place, and I told them it was but I
was waiting to publish it until I got approval. They wanted to go
out and look at the spot to approve it. I then asked them, "if it
hadn't been in place, and I didn't have a specific spot picked out
yet, how would you know whether to approve it?"

Their answer: "I don't know, we were thinking you might be the first
one like that..."

Happy 'Boxing
JeLyBean

From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-10-03 14:36:06 UTC-06:00
Last weekend (or was it the weekend before?) We went out and pulled
Swimming In Opposite Directions. While we were there, we found a new
place to hide it, but because of the rules, we took the box with us. I
submited the appliation and got an email a few days later that they
thought they were in the right place, but the box wasn't there. I
emailed back explaining that the box wasn't planted because we were told
we needed approval first. The ranger was quite confused.
