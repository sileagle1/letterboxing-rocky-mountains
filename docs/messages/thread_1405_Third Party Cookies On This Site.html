Third Party Cookies On This Site

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2007-09-08 18:14:42 UTC
Hey everyone, I have noticed over the last week or so I get a delay
when navigating to or clicking on links on this RML talk list site.
During these delays, in the status bar at the bottom of my browswer,
I see this message:

Waiting for
http://view.atdmt.com/DEN/rview/yhxxxcsa0040000169den/direct/01/?
ti....

I also get a "privacy" icon on the bottom of my browser, and when I
click on it, I see that my browser is blocking cookies from this
atdmt.com site. When I read the privacy summary, I see a reference
to a web site called Atlas Solutions, that provides "marketing
solutions" by collecting information from the users of a web site.
Among the kinds of information it says it is collecting are the
following:

"Unique identifiers issued by a web site or service for the purpose
of identifying an individual over time.

"Information generated by the purchase of a product or service,
including information about the method of payment."

"Information about the computer system the individual is using to
access the Internet, such as the IP number, domain name, browser type
or operating system."

"Information generated by browsing the web site such as which pages
are visited, and how long an individual stays on each page."

"Information generated from or reflecting explicit interactions with
the web site, such as queries to a search engine or logs of account
activity."

"Mechanisms, such as HTTP cookies, for maintaining an active
connection with an individual or for automatically identifying an
individual who has visited a particular site or previously accessed
particular content."

"Data about an individual's likes and dislikes, such as favorite
color or musical tastes."

Now all of this sounds pretty invasive to me. Does anyone know what
this is about, and is anyone else getting delays from this site or
noticing messages about atdmt.com in their browser status bar?

JeLyBean
