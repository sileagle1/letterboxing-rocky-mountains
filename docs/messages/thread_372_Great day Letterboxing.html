Great day Letterboxing

From: Lisa Cook (lisadcook@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-10-04 04:44:58 UTC

I was on my way up to Buena Vista area today and just had to stop
in Bailey for a few boxes. Well, 4 hours and 4 boxes later and
it was too late to go there. In all I found 6 boxes including 2
Hitchhikers!! Woo hoo!
Found near bailey Dragons Lair/Alberts Squirrel and Parasite
box/Payne Gulch BKC (where I found one the first HH). Then decided
on the way back home to nab RC Cola and a Moon Pie Letterboxes at
Meyer Ranch Open Space (where I found the 2nd HH). The last hike for
RC Cola was the most beautiful one with yellow aspen leaves all over
the ground and the trees next to me. It was awe inspiring. I didnt
place either of the HH yet...look for them in a LB near you soon!
TDG
