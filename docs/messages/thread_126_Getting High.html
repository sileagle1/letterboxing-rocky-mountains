From: Ed Webb (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-11-14 20:55:19 UTC
My Engineer Mountain Box is pretty high at 11,300' but that is
where you start for the Yule Box.
I thought about placing one on a 14,000 + foot peak, but last
time I was on top of Handies Peak there was about fifty people up
there. I don't think one would last very long with that kind of
traffic. Plus, when people climb those peaks the first thing they do
is start poking around for a register to sign into.
Placing one on a more obscure high peak would have the opposite
problem, no visitors.
That is a fun question and it makes me wonder about Pikes Peak or
Mount Evans. Or California. Alaska?
Durango Ed
