Well I just got back from my 2 week vacation through
Colorado and Wyoming and thought I would post a quick
note of thanks to all of you that placed boxes in
these beautiful states. I managed to find several
by different placers and all of them were in great
places with awesome stamps. You are some of the most
talented carvers I have ever seen!
Also, special thanks to Y-Nought for setting up the
meeting at Castle Rock with Es, Sasha and wee-noughts,
and to the Letterboxing Gods for allowing me to "run into"
Aljan. I enjoyed meeting all of you and wish I could
have come to your Gathering for more fun. I hope to
meet more of you next time and get to know those that
I met a little better. Thanks again for your hospitality!

Silver Eagle

3 in WY and 1 in UT, so enjoy.
