Re: But What About Scotland?

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2005-04-13 15:32:52 UTC

Hey, settle down there, y. I never heard of those places, to be Frank
& el evation issues prevented us from scaling any mountains, or muros
as they are called there. We did get to see where the Declaration of
Independence was signed (the Scottish one, that is, the document our
US Declaration was modeled upon) at Arbroath Abbey on the east coast
of Scotland and of course got to see lots of castles & standing stones
and cool stuff like that.

We didn't get to meet up with Laura or any other 'boxers over there
but did find one of her letterboxes with a really nice stamp. And of
course, we left a letterbox there, too, at an undisclosed location,
but if you know that secret, let's please keep it between us.

JeLyBean

PS, kilts do get cold in the rain, but all those great single-malts
take the chill off.
