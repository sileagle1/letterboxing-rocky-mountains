Little Raven and a mystery box

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-09-29 20:46:58 UTC
It took forever, but I finally got the clues up for the new Little
Raven box. Little Raven, too. It's a brand spankin' new box with a
new stamp, new journal, new first finder in the same area but
different spot. The stamp turned out to be one of my personal
favorites.

And...my new mystery box, in honor of our own Bean. After spending
hours and hours listening and scribbling and alienating half of my
family and friends and tricking countless innocent people into
looking/listening to the clues of The Big Brain Box, I thought, "I
wonder if Bean would mind if I did a very silly spoof of this?". And
he didn't. So... I give you, 'The Itty Bitty Brain Box'.

It's very silly.
Don't hurt yourself trying to get there.
Take LOTS of ink.

Es, singing about pie
