From: Lisa Cook (lisadcook@comcast.net) | Date: 2004-08-31 19:39:17 UTC
Hi everyone,
I sent a post to the Scotland boxer (Laura) thru another list I am on
telling her that everyone here was thrilled to have a stamp from
Scotland in their boxes. Im not sure which particular dual box she
is talking about so hopefully the placer is on this list. I am
forwarding her response below.....
TDG/Lisa



Well Lisa, you made my day!! How fun it was to look through the
journals at all the entries. We particularly liked the sort of duel
boxes--a micro box followed by a proper box (with journal, etc). A
theme connected the two, on both occasions, and the cleverness
factor made it all the more enjoyable (not to mention superb
carving). So if you know who that was, please pass it along (and
keep the day-making going!). If you are so inclined, could you also
let all of them know that they have helped inspire and keep the
candle burning toward getting a letterboxing scene/site and
hopefully eventually community going here in Scotland.

We had intended on e-mailing placers, but were so caught up in
catching up with folks and doing the holiday thing, we just didn't
get 'round to it.

Thanks again for letting me know. It's so nice to feel a part of
something so positive.

Laura in Scotland
