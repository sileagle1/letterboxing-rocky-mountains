The Final Tally ... 120 Boxes!

From: Kat (Autumn) (autumnkat@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-05-05 17:13:52 UTC-06:00
Rocky Mountain Plant-a-Box month was a success! Okay ... so maybe choosing
a slightly less precipitous month will work better in the future, I'm still
thrilled with all the wonderful participation!

Here's the down and dirty:
* For Colorado, Utah, New Mexico, Wyoming, and Idaho - we have a total of
120 new boxes!
* There are at least three new hitchhikers floating around out there, and
several new personal travellers have made their debut!
* We had 18 sign ups for RMPAB Month, and several folks who weren't signed
up, but who contributed boxes to the region.
* For one boxer, their plant for the month was their first plant ever! Yay
for new boxers planting!
* We had folks from outside the region contribute boxes to our fair
states! Thank you!
* We have several new "series" box sets planted, which always makes hiking
a trail a thrill!
* The "month" was also responsible for 2 mini-meets and one workshop! Holy
cow for 20 folks meeting up in Utah! (that's not such a "mini" meet!)

Here's the new box breakdown (and of course, no way for me to count those
mysterious mystery boxes that may have appeared unbeknownst to us). I may
be off by a few boxes here or there, so dont send your CPA after me.

Colorado: 69 new boxes on AQ with another 1 on LBNA
Utah: 32 new boxes on AQ with another 2 on LBNA
Idaho: 2 new boxes on AQ
New Mexico: 8 new boxes on AQ with another 3 found on LBNA
Wyoming: 2 new boxes on AQ and another 1 on LBNA

And what's cooler still? Just in the first week of May, we've already had
more plants! Whoop!
Thank you all for participating in my whim of an idea. Anyone up for
May-ntenance month?

Cheers,
Autumnsfolly
