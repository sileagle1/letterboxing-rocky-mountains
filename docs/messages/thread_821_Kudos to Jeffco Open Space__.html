Kudos to Jeffco Open Space!!

From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2005-08-08 23:35:09 UTC
Hey all, I was surfing through my email when I received this:

"Hello,

My name is Eric Holzman, I work with the Jefferson County Open Space
Rangers. I'm writing you regarding your letterbox that was placed on
Welchester Park. One of our Rangers confronted some kids in the park
regarding an ordinance violation, and found your letterbox in their
posession. After he completed his business with the kids he showd me
the
box, buthe did not know where they got it. We looked for a probable
location but could not find one. We decided it would be better to
bring
it in and contact you, rather than guess and place it in a hiding spot
you may not be able to find.

We will hold the box here at the office for awhile and wait to hear
from you. Our phone number is 303-271-5938. Our location is 700
Jefferson County Parkway, suite 200, Golden, Co. You can pick up the
box
here during regular business hours during the week, or call and
arrange
a time to meet a Ranger here during the weekend.

Thanks,
Eric Holzman

PS: Loved your stamp!"

So I headed on down there, and the box is there - complete and in
perfect condition!! I have to completely re-hide it in a new spot,
but thanks to Eric the Ranger, I get that opportunity!

~RavenWolf~
