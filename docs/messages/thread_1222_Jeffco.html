In the interest of being encouraging and to provide an example
where The Powers That Be were "convinced" that boxing/caching ARE in
keeping with their reason(s) for exisiting (usually aka Mission
Statement-ish)

....Ladies and Gentleman I submit: The policy of Jefferson County,
Colorado re: boxing/caching.

http://www.co.jefferson.co.us/openspace/openspace_T56_R120.htm
(note only 6 guidelines on the list!)

Endearingly called Jeffco, these very box friendly folks USED to
require letterboxing permits, but have (so I'm told - no idea how long
it took as that PREdates me...) decided that 'boxing encourages folks
to visit their properties. They've even issued little stickers to
their rangers/volunteers. I consider it a Badge of Honor (and
encouragement) when I check on my boxes located w/in their
jurisdiction to see that I Got One! - A sticker from Jeffco and NO
email telling me to remove/relocate one of my boxes! Not ONE yet!
Wahoooooooo!

Heck, I've half thought I should carve them a stamp or so - so I
can see something more interesting than one of their stickers in my
(and others') boxes when the letterboxing Rangers come to visit. (Oh,
and I HAVE GOT to wonder...do they have their own logbooks while out
hunting 'boxes while at work? I sure HOPE so!)

And...well ....to carve some of them a stamp would SO amuse the
crap out of me at least, and maybe them as well.

Just 'cause it IS so, doesn't mean doesn't mean it has to STAY
that way!

Any ideas for a stamp image to carve for the Jeffco loverlies? I
think they are WAY overdue for a HollyDay PREsent!

;-) preboxed (aka Holly)
Denver
