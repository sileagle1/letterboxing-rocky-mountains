The fatter markers I use by Uchida are called Marvy Brush Markers. I
find they don't take as long to ink up the stamps and so I usually
don't have to huff on them because they don't dry out as fast. The
down-side to them is that it's hard to color very small sections with
the wider tip. The colors you mentioned wanting to buy extra are #5
yellow (bright) and #16 pale orange (flesh). I'm looking to get
another "flesh" that has a bit more pink to it.

I got 2 sets at Michael's (primary set and victorian set) which gave
me pretty good variety. My Michael's has a very limited selection of
markers you can buy individually, so I went online to buy a few
singles. I can't remember the company now, but will do another search
to see if I find one that rings a bell. Whoever it was, they had a
pretty good selection (but not ALL the colors Marvy makes) and they
were fast filling the order. I think the price was less than the $1.99
at Michael's, but then there's the shipping charges. One problem with
ordering online is that the color swatches are not always true to the
marker, so I didn't guess right on all the colors I ordered.
