From: Kathy Castleberry (kathycastleberry@msn.com) | Date: 2004-10-25 10:06:19 UTC-06:00
I just talked with a ranger at Roxborough State Park regarding letterboxes.
I have two up there and was told state parks were OK. She said they do not
like boxes because people tramp all over the place - I quickly told her how
this should not happen with good clues, etc. She said next January or
February they will have a new Ranger at Rox and he/she will be making a
final decision. I am hoping they will let our boxes stay - told them about
how Jeffco handles it. Even offered to find each box and give a good
description of where they were and if they were on a good spot. This is
about all I can do

From: JODIE KERN (jodiemkern@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-26 07:56:28 UTC-07:00
100% dried dragonfruit... It looks really cool, round with black seeds and it is a bright magenta color dried... at first bite it tastes really good, kinda like strawberries but then I dunno if it is the seeds (there are a lot of them) or what but then it is actually pretty nasty. Oh well, gotta try it.

Got whalloped by the same storm twice. LA was a total mess, got about 5 inches of rain just the first night. Then got hit by the same storm again in snow form when I got back to CO. *shrugs* whatcha gonna do? ...hide and warm by the fire!
