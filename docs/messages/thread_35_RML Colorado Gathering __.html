Well - I looked at the poll today, and most folks are asking for a
Saturday gathering. August 23 or Aug 30 seem to be the dates that
are top requested.

I'd love to arrange a picnic gathering one of those weekends.
Potluck lunch, with some boxing as well! If anyone would like to
demonstrate stamp carving tips, or anything - that would be lovely.
We can meet to exchange stamps, hike for a box ... perhaps plant
another box?

I'm actually considering making a personal traveler for the event,
but I haven't decided what you'd have to "do" to earn the stamp. Tee
Hee! *rubs hands together wickedly*

Places suggested so far include Roxborough State Park (yes, there's a
$6 fee if you don't have an annual pass) and also Deer Creek Park.

Who can make it?
Preferences on date?
Location?

I want to meet more of you that box in the state ... and maybe even
coax Cadenza out of Idaho? :)

Autumn
