Hello Cluemasters-
We are getting down to the end of this treasure hunt and no one
has found it yet. Yesterday there was a small altercation when the
owner of El Rancho Bar on Main Street above Kroegers' Hardware heard
noises on his roof. The two men up there had a list of Siver Bullet
Clues and were sure that they were on the right track.

More clues:

18. The Gateway to Sleep. (Gateway was the former name of Santa Rita
Park).

19. The FCC won't allow us to give out this clue.

20. Next Years theme is Snowdown Disco Do It.

21. Truth, Justice and the European Way.

22. Look. Up in the sky. Down. Pull.

23. Misdirection, metaphor. Don't climb up on buildings anymore.

24.No key is required. No one is gaurding it. (Last year the clues
led to a hide-a-key under a table at a Chinese restaurant that opened
a mailbox with the Bullet. In previous years it was in a Police car
trunk. And in a Fireman's pocket).

I'm afraid I have less of an idea now than I had before. The
last regular clue will come tonight but I might miss it because I
will be at the parade. If no one finds it by tomorrow afternoon they
will announce it's location on the radio. And I'm not really into
that kind of "solving". If you have any more ideas until then I would
be happy to check them out. And of course I'll let you know where it
was and who found it.

Thanks for playing this with me,

y-nought
