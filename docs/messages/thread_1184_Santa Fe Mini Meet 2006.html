Santa Fe Mini Meet 2006

Santa Fe Letterboxers are hosting a Dinner Mini Meet on Saturday,
November 11th at 6:00 pm. The Meet will be at a local restaurant
specializing in New Mexican cuisine.

Maria's New Mexican Kitchen
555 W Cordova Rd
Santa Fe, NM

We are planning for a very casual dinner event, with plenty of time
for exchanges, story telling and sharing of ideas. We hope this is
an opportunity to meet letterboxers from surrounding areas. No
structured events are planned, although there are plenty of boxes in
the nearby area to keep you busy for a few days.

We hope to see you there!

The event has been posted on Atlas Quest. Please sign up if you plan
to attend. We need to know how many people will be there so we can
make reservations.

Thanks,
lionsmane63 and Astro D
