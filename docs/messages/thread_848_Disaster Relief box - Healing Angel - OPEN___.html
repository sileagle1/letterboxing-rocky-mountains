Disaster Relief box - Healing Angel - OPEN!!!

From: Aljan Sundance (aljansundance@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-03 14:46:43 UTC-07:00
This is just a reminder that the Healing Angel postal letterbox is now open for sign ups! To get on the list to receive this box, email me reciept of donation to a disater relief organization of your choice... I am not concrete on wanting a copy of the reciept. I trust that the fine folks in this group will make a donation if they say that they have or will. Need ideas on where to donate? Check FEMA's website or the Red Cross site.

The stamp isn't quite done - the design is done, it's a beauty! I hope to get it carved up tomorrow. I meant to get it done tonight but left my carving block at home. *rolls eyes* Can't keep my stuff together. As soon as it is done I will get it off the the first person on the list.

Tell EVERYONE that you know about this box and let's make this list a long one!!!!

Thanks!

Aljan
