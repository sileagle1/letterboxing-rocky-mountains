Boxing Day

From: HER (fauxsum@hotmail.com) | Date: 2005-12-28 03:48:23 UTC
I for one have spent time in the Commonwealth. 12/26 is such places
is known as BOXING Day....hehe....With this awesome weather on the
Front Range.....OK I Did Some.

Proud to announce I've PREsurpassed F60...can 100 be far behind?

Anyway, DABD....Day After Boxing Day...I'm pleased to announced I've
placed A Road-Side Incident ...on LbNA...my P3.

...in Boulder with a First Finder ...smile....and a >>>PRErequest to
that person!<<<

I was up that way doing errands.......one late in the day was
finding Fador Glava.... what an inspiring box!

and ran out of daylight finding a place to plant my Boulder-Centric
box. I am blonde and dork. I wasn't sure on what date I'd actually
be able to place this box...so I left a space blank on my first page
in this box! Which, of course! I forgot to fill in once I'd found a
place to put it!

Would the first finder mind entering in to my space provided (on the
first page along with stamp sample) the date "12/27/2005"?

Thanks!

Oh, and I'm 80+% of the way done with getting ready to place
GME. ..Another Denver Mystery(ish) box some time VERY soon. If I
possibly can before my paid break from work is done.....Look for it
soon, too. I Hope...

Cheers,
preboxed aka
HER/Denver
