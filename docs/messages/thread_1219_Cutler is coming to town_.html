ES! You need those handy, and oh so warm, hand/foot warmers that

down the field! WOOT! We had a blast! And, those warmer jobbies

nice "Jack" frosty touch! But, last night wasn't windy which helped

us...and the stadium lights seemed to be trying too. The camera man
to the left of us attached a big 24" square room fan under his
camera to keep his lense clear! It was so funny to see! I was so
tempted to just run over and breath a bit of pirate air on it!
AAaaarrrr!!!!

Alas, it wasn't a pretty game to watch, but, the warm welcome the
fans gave to Cutler was awesome, AND, his first touchdown pass was
right in front of us! COOL! When Al Wilson went down, it seemed
surreal with the whole stadium shouting, "WILSON, WILSON, WILSON"!
He scared the heck out of us, but sounds like he'll be okay. We
love that guy!

down with a Santa Hat on??? Ha!
