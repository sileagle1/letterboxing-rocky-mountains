Okay all you Rocky Mountain letterboxers!

I am making a call for Lord of the Rings letterboxes! As some of you
know we have our own Mt. Doom here in Western Colorado high atop
Grand Mesa. There are currently 7 lovely boxes there done by
assorted lovely boxers.

We would love (we being me) to add more boxes! Aside from the fact
that, hey, come on, Lord of the Rings letterboxes, sweet!, we would
like to be able to fill in the 'holes' between the existing boxes
making them all easier to find. Not to mention more tempting.

It's a very safe location, far off the beaten trail. No chance of
them being found accidentally. I will watch out for them and do
maintanence. It will be your creation, your plant. I'll find it a
perfect home and send you the directions so you can post the clue.

We have:

The Preciousss (Golum)
Goldberry
Tom Bombadil
Screaming Orc
Ringwraith
Faramir, Prince of Ithalian
Fellowship of the Marmots

Just think of how many other subjects you can imagine!

So get out your carving tools and start carving! Come on! It'll be
fun!

Esmerelda, currently waiting for a box from LndCrzr..ARRRR!
