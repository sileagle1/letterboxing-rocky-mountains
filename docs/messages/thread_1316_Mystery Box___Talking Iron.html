"TALKING IRON"
A Mystery Letterbox

FIRST OF A SERIES OF LETTERBOXES FOR OUR OWN ROCKY MTN. PIRATE,
Esmerelda...our favorite ducky! It's an "Ace in the Hole"!

Fer all ya cowboys and cowgirls out thar, I jest wanted to bring yer
attention to the fact that it's a Series! Yep, more than one. How
many, ya ask...shucks, I'm not gonna tell ya and ruin the fun fer the
Posse! Now being a mystery and all, ya'll have to find the first box
to get yerself the clues for the next and so on. The other clues
can't be found out any other way. Unless ya'll willing to bribe the
Posse with pie! Har...jest a little joke thar!

Now seriously, the Posse done asked me to let ya'll know that since
thar are more than one of these hidden around and about the state and
the fact that ya'll might not be able to wander all over the state
like them cowboys did, that we propose that if ya want the clues to a
box that may be hidden in an area that ya'll live or will be
traveling to AND you don't intend to find the whole series in order,
that you jest feel free Pony Express me and ask me fer the clues to
the box in yer interested location!! Phew...Clear as mud??? My head
is spinning...need to get some shut eye! But, one catch...if the box
hasn't been found yet, we reserve the right to wait until it's been
found the proper way, by doing the series in order...after that,
ya'll can hunt till yer hearts content! That's all I got to say,
except....

Yeehaw! Giddy-up for a rootin' tootin' time!

Allers...Jest Peach
