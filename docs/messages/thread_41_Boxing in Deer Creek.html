Well, I got my fat butt out into the mountains yesterday and found my
first 2 boxes!! Yippee!! We found the Deer Creek box and the Soar
Feet box. Both boxes were in good condition, but haven't seen very
many visitors. Trinity Jah at 31/2 was a trooper if I have ver seen
one. She hiked 4 miles and only pitched a fit once!! WHOOHOO!! I
am very sore today as I haven't done anything of this magnitude in
about 6 years, but am totally excited to do more!! I will tell ya'all
that the hike up deer creek is a little bit difficult only because
most of the way up is rocky and kinda steep.

See ya'all soon!!
