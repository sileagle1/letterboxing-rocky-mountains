Cowabunga!

From: HER (fauxsum@hotmail.com) | Date: 2006-07-28 03:47:03 UTC
A moo-ey fun event is currently happening in downtown Denver which
delights and amooooses me, so I made a temporary box for it. And
right here is the only place to get the cluuuuuuuuues.

This box will disappear on October 19th and probably find a new
life ...perhaps as a traveler, a postal box, cootie, or to a new
hiding spot, since I know only a handful of you will be able to
mooooove yourselves to Denver for this wonderfully creative urban art
project (oh yeah and a letterbox!)

After I pull the box on 10/19 but before its next In Cow Nation ...if
you should happen to meat me on the trail, and I have this box on
me ...say Cowabunga! for this hand carved stamp...one of my faves, so
far. (Log handmade too - no ink or pen in box.)


***

http://denver.cowparade.com

Find Aspen Eyes. 2 cement squares towards the closest street from the
back right leg. Look under, on the side by the drain.

Wrapped in the same color as the pole and the dark space for
camouflage not waterproofness. Please note how it's wrapped before
opening and note that the contents are really jammed into this
oversized altoid-like box. Please - be discreet and rehide sooper,
dooper well or I'll come to your house "Cow Patty" your lawn and
shubbery...

This is a pretty low key place to hang out to wait for your snagging
moment at all times of day....Number of people around will vary,
however.

Parking not great in this area, but remember the 16th Street Mall
Shuttle is FREE...and at the shuttle stop kiosks you can also pick up
a Grazing Guide.

Please hit the "send email" button from this talk list to report
finds, status updates and other Bovine News.

preboxed
