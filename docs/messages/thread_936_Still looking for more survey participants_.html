Still looking for more survey participants!

From: marisa1of9 (marisafink@bsu.edu) | Date: 2005-11-25 03:50:22 UTC

To date, 245 people have responded to my invitation to participate in
the first descriptive research study on letterboxing and letterboxers.
I am looking for another 250 or more to tell me about themselves and
their letterboxing experiences so I can compile the data and share the
results here and on several other letterboxing sites. We are an
interesting bunch surely there are many out there who are just as
curious about our culture as I am. Here's your chance to become part
of this historical research: Any visitor 18 years of age and older may
complete the survey, and all responses are anonymous. To voluntarily
participate, go to http://portal.bsu.edu/inqsit/inqsit.cgi/fink and
select `letterboxing' and 'continue'. Use your trail name or a
pseudonym to sign in. For more information about this research
project, please contact the Primary Investigator - Marisa Fink, Ball
State University, AC 414, 2000 W. University Avenue, Muncie, IN 47306,
765.285.1962, marisafink@bsu.edu. Thank you for your participation.
