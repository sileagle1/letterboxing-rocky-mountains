A great day for LBing

From: Kristina Sandoval (krislsandoval@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-04 14:13:58 UTC-07:00
Well, we finally got a chance to go Lbing today. it was the first time for hubby and dog! Dog had a great time.. not sure about hubby! LOL We wanted to do three but after the second one hubby was ready to call it quits. So tomorrow the kids, dog and I may head back out.
We were able to get a couple hitchhikers planted. Ran into some geocachers that thought we were too. So we explained how ours worked and they showed us theirs.

I did see some familiar stamps and names in the logs. Es, ravenwolf, Eoghan, JeLybean, CW sunseeker to name a few. It is much more fun looking through the logs when you recognize other's stamps.

Enjoy the nice weather this weekend and get outside!
Kris and family!
