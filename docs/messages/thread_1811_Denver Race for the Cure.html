Boxing friends-

I've been participating in the Race for the Cure for several years now since a colleague of mine was first diagnosed with breast cancer. This past year, of course, two of our letterboxing friends have also battled breast cancer. In recognition of this, I decided to create a special team for this year's race called "Stamp out Cancer!":

http://www.komendenver.org/site/TR?pg=team&fr_id=1100&team_id=49757

This year's race is October 3. I hope that some of you will be able to join me at the Race for the Cure. Since three miles *is* a long way to walk without the prospect of a stamp, I'd be open to the idea of a post-race mini-meet if anyone else is game.
