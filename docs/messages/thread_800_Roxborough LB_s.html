Roxborough LB's

From: Kathy Castleberry (kathycastleberry@msn.com) | Date: 2005-07-28 17:13:17 UTC
Both of my letterboxes (Puma Prints and Hiker's Boot) at Roxborough
Park are now gone!!! They have a new manager at the park and he will
be looking into letterboxing at Roxborough. I did get back the
logbook on Hiker's Boot, at least, and they gave me the other box
that had been there so long -- Fountain Valley. I will mail this
logbook to the owner if he wants it or if anyone wants to adopt this
one, let me know...the stamp needs repair or recarving. They tell me
that letterboxing probably will be allowed at Roxborough later and
that I may be able to put one inside the Visitor's Center. I am
trying my best to be patient with these people -- I have asked them
repeatedly to let me know if they don't allow them and I would remove
them - but apparently they forgot. I actually gave them the
instructions on where they were located - and they found them!!! I
will now just remove these two from the LB website. I absolutely love
this park and I hope it's future is secure. If you have not been
there, you are missing a real treat - even without letterboxes.
(wiping a small tear from my cheek) Puppypaws
