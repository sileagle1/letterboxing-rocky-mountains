Red Letterbox ... heads up

From: Renee and Dave (haipnsuch@juno.com) | Date: 2006-01-11 18:43:28 UTC-07:00
It was Cirrus' (aka Kapu) 10th birthday today and we thought it would be
fun for him to pick up a "first finder" on a newly planted box not too
far away. However, when we finally found it, it had actually been placed
on Dec. 21 (not Jan. 9) with the FF getting picked up on Christmas Day.
It's an interesting box and we like the stamp, but Yoque will be
contacting the owner to try and get him to clarify his clues a bit. Of
all the letterboxes that we've found, we think that this is the creepiest
site and think that it's critical to have good clues for this particular
area. We personally came across the carcasses of two elk and a deer on
our search for this box and were serenaded all along by gun fire. If any
of you were planning to head out there, we suggest you hold tight until
the owner works on the clues a bit. It's not too difficult to get to ...
it's just the type of area that warrants very clear directions. You
don't want to travel ANY FARTHER than you have to on this road. And yes,
we read the warning at the bottom of the clues, but it all just wasn't
very clear to us.
~Catamount Carvers

Re: Red Letterbox ... heads up

From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2006-01-12 15:58:39 UTC
Oh Silent Wings, I'm so sorry to hear all this. This box was
planted by my son and his girlfriend and it's their first plant,
first hand carved stamped, first everything! They actually
surprised me and Cappy wtih it for a Christmas gift knowing how much
we love this hobby. That is why it was planted on Dec 21st and we
found it on Christmas Day! He put the date he posted the box rather
than the date he planted it, not realizing the difference. I spoke
to him about us being the "First Finder" with the obvious advantage
we had! We want to make it right to Cirrus, and I've emailed you
offlist about it.

I don't know where exactly the clues led you astray, we found it
right away, but we did warn him to warn everyone. The serenade that
Silent Wings refers to is a shooting range that we understand is
down the road and over the hill, but you can hear it clearly. I
haven't read the clues he's posted yet, I will do it today. Since
it is so close to the road we thought you could park, grab it and
leave without a problem. I will let him know to clarify the clues
right away and let you all know when he's done that. I hope it
won't keep people from adding it to their Finds, he worked so hard
on it.

Hugs, Dixie Peach
