From: sileagle1 (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2003-08-12 12:36:57 UTC
Howdy, Rocky Mountainers! I got an invitation to join this
list yesterday so decided to try it now that I have boxes
in Colorado and New Mexico (not sure how many states this
list encompasses). Just wanted to let you know that I
published a Letterbox Report (not SITREP) on the main list
(post #31558) from my recent travels through your area
and beyond. My family and I love your part of the country
and welcomed the break from the Texas heat. I did not
have time to find all the Colorado boxes but I went for
as many as I could. Though I have been to Colorado many
times (I've even climbed 7 fourteeners) I was amazed at
the new places that your boxes took me to; places I had
never heard of but were great! As you have probably
noticed, I also planted a box in Grand Lake near Rocky
Mountain National Park, one of my favorite places. I made
sure the box is not on National Park land so hopefully it
will not be confiscated. Hope you will look for it next
time you are up that way (probably only accessibly in summer).
Thanks again for the great finds!

Silver Eagle (P54F184X5)
