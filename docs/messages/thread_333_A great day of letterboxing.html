A great day of letterboxing

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-06 04:11:59 UTC
We didn't get to very many, but we had a wonderful day. Was the
weather perfect today or what?!

I took two of the miniature buccaneers and my sister out today and
we all got Altitude Sickness. :D (wonderful stamp!)

We went to Larkspur to plant a new box outside the fence of the
renaissance faire property, but after several attempts just didn't
feel good about any of them and brought it back home with us. I
haven't decided if I'll try again, just wait til the faire opens
again next year, or plant it at another faire in another state. I
may even just take it to the faire with me and make it a one or two
day box that you have to get from me! I just need to ponder on it
for awhile. :)

Hope you all had great days!

Esmerelda
