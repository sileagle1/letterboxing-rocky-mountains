I *finally* was able to make the drive up to Meeker, Colorado today
and what an incredible time of the year for it! The colors are
just.... wow....and the day was made for a drive. :)

I also got brave enough to ask and the 'That'll Do, Dan' letterbox
is now safely tucked into the White River Museum in Meeker.
Although that limits the time you can find it, it was just too
wonderful of a place to pass up. Any of you who wander up there
will enjoy the museum... I was shocked at how much
incredible 'stuff' they have in there! The lady who was working
today knew absolutely everything there was to know about everything
in there plus had endless knowledge of the area.. and was happy to
chat and answer questions.

I hope everyone is having a very stampy-riffic weekend!
