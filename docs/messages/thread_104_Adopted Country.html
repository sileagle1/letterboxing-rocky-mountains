Actually, the hike is fairly casual, mostly level until the very
end. Gorgeous country, lots of different mushrooms along the trail.
And the waterfalls are pretty cool. I was alone and cruising, so it
only took me about 2 1/2 hours. I would suggest spending more time if
you can just to soak it all in.
I did send the Drew Clan a note. They were wondering if that box
had been swept away by nature.
I found the Rocky Mountains micro last weekend near RMNP. Neat.
Is it appropriate to stamp into a micro box pad? I didn't know so I
squeezed mine in. I hope it is alright.
