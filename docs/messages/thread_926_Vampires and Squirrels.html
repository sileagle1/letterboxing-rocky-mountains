From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2005-11-10 14:57:51 UTC
Welcome back, Aljan!

I need to tell everyone that this box is a letterboxers MUST HAVE! It
is AWESOME! Aljan, all of your boxes are very special, but, this one
is very, very cool! And yes, we were up there at night, with those
second finders....my son and his girlfriend...Robothouse and Miss
Chickie...my son lives in Longmont and we met them there for their
first box...and I told them too that they are spoiled to have such a
wonderful FIRST box, and BTW, we gave them them the FIRST
FINDERS...anyway, I digress....

We had flashlights, think flashlights and cemetery and purported
vampires and then have a squirrel JUMP out of the hiding place at you
unexpectedly! It nearly ran up Cappy's arm! We all jumped and laughed
too hard! We wondered if modern vampires take on the look of
squirrels instead of bats! Ha! What a crazy, fun, night!

Okay, all you boxers, you MUST HAVE this box before the squirrel takes
it to another hiding spot! Have I ever lead you astray??? JUST DO
IT, me hearties!

Hugs, Dread Peach
P.S. Thanks again, Aljan for the adventure and reward!!! Silent
Wings, we were looking out for ye...we expected YOU to jump out from
the bushes, but not a squirrel to jump out of the tree! Ha! LOL
