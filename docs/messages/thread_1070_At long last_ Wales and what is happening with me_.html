At long last, Wales and what is happening with me! (OT)

From: cadenza74 (jjcadenza@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-04-12 21:47:56 UTC
Okay, so I was mean and dropped tantalizing hints without telling
anything specific. I am back from the UK now and thought I would take a
moment to update you, my good friends, on why it was I went out to
Wales.

I'm sad to say that I am going to be leaving the group this fall. I
hope to still participate, but I am going to be moving to Great
Britain--Cardiff, Wales to be specific. I am not at all sad to say the
reason. I am engaged to marry a man who lives in Cardiff!

He and I dated 13 years ago when we were in college, but the timing
wasn't right and I broke things off. Neither of us has married since
and this year we reconnected. It has been very right this time and
things have come together perfectly. I am so happy! The only thing
that makes me sad is the fact that I will be saying goodbye to so many
of my good friends that I have come to care about. I am excited to go
there though. We will marry here in Idaho, but will be moving in to his
flat that he owns there in Cardiff. Luckily I really liked it there.
Cardiff was a great city with lots of fun things to do. It will be an
adjustment for me after living in a town that is much smaller. I am
also going to miss my mountains so much, but there are some nice
national parks close.

So that's my news. That is why I was a bit evasive in answering
questions about my trip. We are planning on marrying in mid-October. I
am just so very happy and wanted you all to be able to share in my
excitement.

-Cadenza [:D]
