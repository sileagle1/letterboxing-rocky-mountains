From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-09-14 15:30:25 UTC-06:00
I just met up with Ranger Eric at Deer Creek to help him locate
Rattlesnake Gulch. What a nice man! I asked him if he had his own
stamp yet, he said no, but his wife is VERY interested =]

Anyway - he is the same ranger that located my Swimming in Opposite
Directions box... he told me that box will have to be moved. So, I plan
to make a large note on the clues page that says the box is unavailable
(it will still be there, I just don't want new people going to look for
it). Tomorrow, I will hopefully be able to get out there and find a new
location for it. Then I will have to apply for the new spot... in other
words, it may take a few weeks for the box to get a new home.

If you haven't found it, and want to find it - I suggest pulling the
clues ASAP. Unless I have to - I will leave the box in its current spot
till I get approval for the new location.

PS - I left a temporaty hiker at Rattlesnake while I was there. =] Of
course, I was in a hurry, and no prepared to be out boxing, so I didn't
stamp the hiker into the letterbox.... oops.
