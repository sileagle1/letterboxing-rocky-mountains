Just sending another call out for FOUR events happening in the Rocky
Mountain area over the next few weeks!

The first is Rocky Mountain Plant-A-Box month, which only halfway through
April is already (in my not so humble opinion) a huge success! Boxes are
popping up all over the place! Whoop! You can participate by simply
planting a box, releasing a hitchhiker, or just reactivating an old box.
Sign up and get your boxes counted in the final tally here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1129

And if that weren't enough already ... I'm excited to say that three
gatherings will be happening in conjunction with RMPAB Month:

The Rocky Mountain Smilin' Moose Mini-meet is THIS SATURDAY, 4/18 at 10am
for the Denver area Colorado folks (and some from further away!). If you're
coming into Denver for the Doors Open Denver activities, please join us!
Front Range Hiker thought of the venue, and I'm rounding up the troops.
Event details are here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1208

Local carving mistress Ravenwolf is conducting a logbook making workshop
(space very limited and may already be full) in Centennial on Saturday, 4/25
at 1pm, hosted by the lovely Tai Chi and Chai Tea. Event details are here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1210

And the folks on the other side of the hill are celebrating the conclusion
of RMPAB Month with the Rocky Mountain Gardner Villiage Mini-meet on
Saturday, May 2 at 11:30am in West Jordan, UT. Thank you CW Sunseeker for
coordinating! Event details are here:
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1222

Thanks to everyone for getting excited and involved with this little idea of
mine. :) I'll post a final tally of how many boxes were released by those
who signed up for RMPAB sometime in early May.

Box the Rockies!
Autumn
