Stubby the Toothslayer update

From: (scott_katytalbot@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-04-18 15:21:29 UTC
I have some good news and some bad news for all of you. First with the good news....I spoke to Stubby this morning and he said that he and his daughter went out letterboxing this weekend and found one of my boxes as well as one by CW Sunseeker. Thus, I think he is hooked. His daughter is even thinking about carving up her own stamp! On the down side, the name "Stubby" may stick. The doctor still seemed somewhat optimistic last week, however this past weekend Tom thought that his finger was starting to separate. With a nice goo coming out of it. Almost like his body was rejecting the finger tip. He goes back in to the doctor this Wednesday and will have more news at that time but is not optimistic.

Ramdelt
