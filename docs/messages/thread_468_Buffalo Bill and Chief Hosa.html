Buffalo Bill and Chief Hosa

From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2004-12-31 23:40:46 UTC

Hi all! It's been ages since I posted in, but we finally did some
New Year's Eve letterboxing and we want to report to the Splendid
Buccaneers that both of these two letterboxes are doing fine! What
a beautiful day today! I was so excited to see both of these
stamps! WOW!!!! For those of you who haven't found these, you MUST
go! The stamps are absolutely SPLENDID! And the home made log books
you must see to appreciate!

Jodi, I too have a a new grandbaby, well, 10 months old now! He's a
sweetie! We wanted to plant our first box but have never carved
before and now with all the wonderful stamps we're finding, we're a
bit intimidated! We have some ideas but can't seem to get the them
to transfer easily to the rubber....can anyone help me???

Happy NEW year to all! Have a fruitful letterbox year in 2005!
Hopefully, we will get out there to find more of your boxes this
year!

Thanks again to ES and the Splendid Buccaneers for the FUN boxes!

I think everyone does it differently... I draw my design out with pencil and then just flip it over onto my carving medium (drawing side down then) and then rub, draw... how do I say it... blacken the whole drawing area on the backside and when I lift it up the design has transfered.
