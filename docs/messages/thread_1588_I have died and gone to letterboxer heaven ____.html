I have died and gone to letterboxer heaven ....

From: Autumn (autumnkat@gmail.com) | Date: 2008-08-29 00:27:41 UTC
I am hopping up and down - making that funny "squeeeee" noise that my
boyfriend associates with anything I find super exciting.

I just opened my mail. *hop hop hop*

Okay - let me back up a bit in my story. While the Fellowship of the
Two was making the Ascent of Mount Doom, a serious tragedy occurred.
My old signature stamp fell down into the pit, consumed by the Eye of
Sauron. It melted away just like the One Ring (only maybe not so
dramatically - I mean - like no mountains exploded)..

Now, as any of you who have seen my boxes can attest, I'm a pretty
craptastic carver. So just imagine how ecstatic I was to get an
email from one of our MASTER carvers - Ravenwolf - offering to carve
ME a new siggy stamp? I seriously about fell over in my office
chair. Seriously, Me?! Whoop!

So Ravenwolf and Aljan Sundance pooled their artistic heads, and drew
and carved me the most spectacular new Signature Stamp. I just
opened my mail and I swear my neighbors came out to see if I was
okay. These two have created something I could never have dreamed
up. I'm beyond thrilled. Aljan's art and Ravenwolf's carving? It
is the most beautiful thing I've ever seen on pink stuff. *swoons*
So all ya'll that have my old sig stamp as an exchange? Um hmmm ...
we need to hook up and swap again, because this stamp is just
amazing!!

Thank you, thank you, a million times thank you to Ravenwolf and
Aljan for my new siggy stamp which is soooo cool I'm seriously
hopping and squee-ing with glee! *hop hop hop*

Autumn
(who is totally gushing)

...and who will be cross posting this gushing thanks to AQ as well!
