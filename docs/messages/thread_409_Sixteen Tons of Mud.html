From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-28 05:17:11 UTC

I can't believe you were here today, Kat!! I almost went up to
check on 16-ton.. but decided it was too muddy!! LOL That has to be
quite a climb in the rain! I was down by the river on a nice mostly
dry trail sitting comfortably on a log while you all were valiantly
doing the mud tango.

And, y'all need to know that NO one rehides a box like Kat does.
She told me that she'd put some extra rocks on 'Big Fish' last time
she was here because it wasn't very well covered.. when I walked up
I just wooted out loud.. there was a boulder the size of a cocker
spaniel on the top of the cairn! I grinned all day thinking about
Kat lugging that over there to make sure that box was well hidden!
Now THAT is a considerate and thoughtful boxer! A grateful pirate
hug comin' your way, Kat.

Y, you're taking the whole onion deal better than I am. It just
makes me make the scary face thinking about evil space ranger
tossing that away. What.. it was taking up too much room in the
office?! It was tiny!! And you've been calling them ever since
they dug it up! Maybe we need to leave messages to the rangers on
the boxes.

Esmerelda, who can't stop singing '16 ton' now.
