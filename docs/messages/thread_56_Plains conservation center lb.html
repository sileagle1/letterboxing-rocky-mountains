Hi everyone,
Just an FYI. Plains conservation center is only open on Saturdays UNTIL Labor day.
Then more or less closed to the public except special occasions like full moon walks and first Sat of month (see below).
So go check it out before Labor Day.  They are mainly open in the winter season for school field trips.  They are open the first Sat of each month in the winter for a special program and I think you must sign up in advance.  If you want to just letterbox you can on that day with an advance request.  They also may allow it for a group set up in advance during other times...if it doesnt interfere with the school programs.
Ive never been there but always wanted to go...my kids went in 4th grade and I couldnt make the field trip.  Its only recent that they have the open Saturdays (maybe this year).
Im also new to letterboxing and have many questions...but that will be another post.
