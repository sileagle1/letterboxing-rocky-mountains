Well - she didn't sign her name. But Scarab of the Doubtful Guests (the
guy who planted the box) carved her a stamp that was the letters N-P-R and
a little microphone stamp. Those were in the book. I believe that was
Linda's "sign in".

Scarab sent me an email regarding it - and I was pretty twitterpated to
have found that box, since I really could have done any of them while
there -but we picked that one because it was an Urban Box that we thought
we could do quickly.

We didn't know anything about the NPR spot at the time. Go figure!
