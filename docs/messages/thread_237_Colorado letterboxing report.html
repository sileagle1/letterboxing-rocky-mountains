Colorado letterboxing report

From: Silent Doug (silentdoug@letterboxing.info) | Date: 2004-06-25 09:04:41 UTC-04:00
Jennifer and I were in western Colorado last week for a wedding (visiting
from Pennsylvania) and we took some time to go letterboxing (of course). We
were able to find the following boxes, all in good shape and all with
really terrific stamps and clues. We were pleased to pick up the first
finder certificates for two of the Zebediah Gump series, too!

Thanks to Esmerelda and all the other CO letterboxers for some great boxes!

C, June 11, 2004
The Miner, June 11, 2004
The Peninsula, June 11, 2004
Zebediah Gump - The Crystal, June 13, 2004 (First Finder)
Zebediah Gump - Doc Holliday, June 14, 2004 (First Finder)
Zebediah Gump - Grizzly Creek, June 14, 2004
Spouting Rock, June 14, 2004
Continental Divide, June 15, 2004

Doug
