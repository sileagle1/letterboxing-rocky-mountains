Hiya Rick from Boca! Welcome to the list and to SLC!

This is probably a good time to introduce myself to the group also.
I recently moved to SLC from the Chicago area! We love it here! I haven't had a chance to go boxing yet, as almost everything I own is still packed up in boxes and I can't find some of my Letterboxing stuff :-(
I'm definitely going to try to get up to the Twin Falls gathering in July, so I hope to meet some of you there!

I was happy to see that I recognize some of your names from the Postal group!

AND, I am SERIOUSLY BUMMED that we live so far from a real ren faire! We lived 5 minutes from the Bristol faire back in the midwest, so this year we are going through faire withdrawel. We are going to try to hit some of the smaller faires within driving distance though... it looks like Grand Junction has one in the fall that we are going to try to make.

Happy letterboxing,
Howling Coyote
