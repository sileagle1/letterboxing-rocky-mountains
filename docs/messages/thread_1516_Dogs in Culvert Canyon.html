We went up Culvert Canyon about a mile and a half. When we stopped we
were a little farther than Gold Bar Tower which was across the canyon
from us. That was SUCH a sweet hike!

Linus is a little too smart for his own good and gets bored waiting for
us slow people to stamp in and read logbooks. We need to work on his
little disappearing act. He is also driving our four cats crazy trying
to herd them all into one room. (He doesn't close the door, though).

A little side note about the cats.......when we got back from Moab we
looked around for Garfield and finally heard him whimper from one of
the dresser drawers. He must have crawled in when we were packing and
got closed up in there for a day and a half (yikes!) Linus swears he
didn't do it!
