From: rvn_wolf (treikya@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-06-23 17:40:28 UTC
Weeeellll, the Wink-Winkers (being new, and really not knowing - making this whole thing even better) placed a LB inside a pie shop.

Now, we RMLBers (Rocky Mountain Letterboxers)are of the pirate variety, and it has been long established here in CO by Esmerelda, the Pirate Queen, that the pie is a pirate's preferred pastry.

So recently, amidst some discussion of unplundered booty at our local museum, the topic of said letterbox came up. The parley went something like this:

"PIE SHOP!!"
"And Letterbox..."
"In the pie shop?"
"IN the pie shop."
"...Mini-meet?"
"MINI-MEET!!"

And then there was some discussion of a certain letterboxer in CO who does amazing work, but we never see her, so she is obviously a figment of the imagination of another letterboxers who self admittedly stalks the first one. (Catch all of that?) And when we may be able to see her.

She then committed to coming down on July 21. And the Pie Plunder mini-meet was born.

Now, I just need to get off my duff and go meet the lovely people at the pie shop, and see how many LBers they can handle descending on their lovely little establishment. When that happens, I shall post something here and on AQ.

Clear as mud, right? :D

~RW
