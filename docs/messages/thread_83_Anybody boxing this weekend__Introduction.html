Hi all,

I've been lurking for about a month and thought I should introduce
myself. My name is Holly (aka Zenroxie) in Northern Utah. I've been
letterboxing for 1 1/2 years, and I'm so excited about the growth of
Letterboxing in the Rockies! When I planted my first box it was Utah's
3rd, and now there are almost 20.

I'm amazed and inspired by A-Bear and Cadenza's work in Idaho. Keep it
up! Just think what could be accomplished in another year!

This weekend I found A-Bear's "Rings" box and Heather's 2 "Teddy's
Waterhole" boxes in Utah. They are all alive and well with frequent
visits. I also planted a box near Bear Lake. (The clues will be up as
soon as my film is developed.)

In addition to letterboxing I'm involved in The Carving Consortium
(http://theccforum.proboards9.com/) and Found-Art (found-art.com). If
you enjoy carving and want to imporve your skills, the Carving Consortium
is a wonderful, fun, and supportive place to do it.

The hardest part about planting boxes for me is writing the clues. What
kind of clues do you like to write? And what clues are your favorite to
decipher?

I hope you all had a lovely weekend.

Holly
