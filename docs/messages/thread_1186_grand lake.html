Hiya Chasers:

Didn't want you to feel ignored amongst all this pirate talk and at
the risk of being PREsumptuous speaking for other placers, here's what
I know about boxes placed for the GL Gathering (aka GLG) for your
Monday trip to that lovely town so you have a chance to print clues
and PREpare for your upcoming adventure:

I was the person who brought 4 clues to the GLG (2 of mine and 2 of
Front Ranger Hiker's.) My clues are all up and ready for your GL
boxing pleasure, if you haven't checked LbNA recently. I know that as
of late today, Smoked Salmon (FRH's box) now has clues up on LbNA.

And finally, re: Boxer Lover's Face the Sun box (based on a status
change on one my boxes far from GL I wonder if she may be travelling
again? - lucky "dog" - woof, woof!). It's my understanding that there
are two listings for this box...one for when the box was placed
specifically for the GLG with the notation GLG, and a second after it
was placed in its permanent home after the GLG was over -this would be
the clues you want to print.

Aljan has some wonderful! boxes up in GL you should definitely check
out... not placed for the GLG, however some were pulled around the
event for various reasons, but I think? most/all? have now been
replaced?

So.......happy clue printing and have a GRAND time!
