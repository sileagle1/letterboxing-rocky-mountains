First of all, we want to send a HUG and special thanks to Kat,
Kennamtn, Rivergeek, and Y-Nought for such a FANTATICALLY FUN time!
You all are the absolute letterboxing dream team! Thank you, thank
you, thank you! We added 19 "F" boxes and a HH to our total! We
loved all the trails, the drive-by's, the stamps, the journals, the
views, the rocks and meeting all you letterboxers! And Kat, the
banana cream pie was to die for! We love you guys!

The craziest story was running into A-Bear, CW Sunseeker, and Black
Velvet on Dead Horse Point planting a box! That is a FIRST for us!
How do you record Finding the Planter???? FP=1 for us! What a
great story!!! We're still laughing over that!

Okay, I have some pictures for you all. Before I post them, I guess
I should ask you all for your permission??? If anyone wants them
emailed to their own email address, just let me know....can do!

Hugs to all!

Dixie Peach and Cappy too!

that there is some cosmic force pushing weather systems away from us. Maybe
it's like a biblical plague. Didn't they usually go in increments of seven?
