Cu approved!

From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2004-10-11 00:54:29 UTC

We finally got the approval for the Cu letterbox - so it is alive and
well and back out in the field.

I spoke with one of the people from JeffCo, and asked if it was
easier for them if we put out a box, then let them find it and
approve it, then publish clues - OR was it easier to find a spot, ask
for approval, obtain it, then put the box out. She said that it
didn't matter either way. She said that if you have a good spot,
sometimes it is easier if the box is already there, so they can get a
good look at its location before approving it. So, the good news is
that you can place a box and then ask for permission, but if they
tell you that its location is no good, you have to go back out and
move it, but if they approve it - it's already there and you don't
have to worry about it.

Also went and got the Castlewood Canyon box (the one that the
Castlewood Canyon Park put out) very cool little hike! We had never
been there before and were pleasantly surprised at the location -
very scenic!
