From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-13 19:58:07 UTC
Well it took me about a week after Esmerelda reported this box missing
before I remembered to edit the clues. That was the best mistake I
made that day because Echo and Jessi had already printed those clues
and went looking for it. And when they saw that it was gone they put
one of their own stamps and a logbook together and hid it in the same
place. So this letterbox has found new life in the kindness of it's
visitors.

Since I didn't originally place this box I was not planning on
replacing it. But I couldn't be happier that it is alive
again, "owned" by Jessi, Echo, Floating Feather, and myself, (but
really having a life of it's own).

A big thanks to Jessi and Echo,
Viva Box Canyon!
