Missing boxes

From: Kathy Castleberry (kathycastleberry@msn.com) | Date: 2005-06-20 21:53:37 UTC
I am sorry to hear more boxes are disappearing. Interesting: The
geocache box that I looked at this morning at Roxborough was full of
little trinkets and even a water gun. The Rangers really do not like
this type of stuff and questioned me about my Letterboxes that are
there. I told them that we only used stamps and logbooks and did not
put in trinkets and I think this makes it more favorable for us. They
also did not like that the box was so big. I am thinking of moving
Puma Prints into the Visitor's Center - and they liked that idea and
will get back to me on this. This way, they could monitor the box a
little closer and maybe we can win their hearts and minds!!! Even
tho I am a volunteer ranger at another park - I can "feel" the tension
when I talk about LB's - so, I don't.
