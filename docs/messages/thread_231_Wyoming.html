From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2004-06-17 16:51:02 UTC
I would suggest picking up a Speedball linoleum cutting set - if you
haven't already.

At Hobby Lobby, you can find it over by the art supplies (markers,
colored pencils, etc.) There are several different cutting edges you
can get. That is how I do all of my stamps.

Also, any images I like, I either draw directly in a program like
paint, or Adobe on the computer, or I draw my picture on paper, then
scan it into the computer. I can then print it, and using nail polish
remover (acetone), you can transfer the image onto the pink stuff.
That gives you a better idea of where and how you want to cut out
your stamp.

Hope that helps!

~RavenWolf~
