From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2007-04-23 02:02:03 UTC
Oh no, Dreadful news for Dread Peach! (how's that for a headline?)

I will check on Snappy some time this week for sure! We have had some
amazing flashfloods this year, bigger than anyone alive in this area
recalls ever seeing, so it is definitely possible. He was placed
close to that wash, which may have flashed and taken him for a ride
down to Lake Powell!!!! The river never went very high this season,
so I'm sure that wasn't the problem, but the sand may have changed
and put him in a puddle. If I find him, I'll bring him home for some
TLC if necessary. Poor Aspen has had her first box washed out twice
this year! We replaced it once, and then after another storm we found
it 50 feet down the rocks and into a wash, lid ripped off, but the
bags kept everything safe!

On a happier note, there are four new boxes planted in Moab and
THAT'S SOOOOOO EXCITING!!!! Kenna and I are getting out the boxing
stuff after a boxing drought, yay!

Kat
