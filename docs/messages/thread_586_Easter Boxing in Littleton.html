Easter Boxing in Littleton

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2005-03-28 03:33:48 UTC

What a beautiful day, after so much cold and ugliness! Spent the
afternoon in Littleton and found Oh Dear 2, Fido, and then headed up
to Deer Creek and traversed a very muddy slippery trail to get the
Rattlesnake Gulch box.

The best part of the day was arriving at Oh Dear, only to meet up
with Kim (Phoenix Ascending), Tim, and little Connor who were
stamping into T-I-Double G - Rrrrr! So the four of us did the Oh
Dear and Fido boxes together. What a pleasant surprize to get
exchanges in addition to my three finds for the day.

The light got away from me (and my muddy boots took time to clean
before getting in my car) so I wasn't able to get Swimming in
Opposite Directions, but that's next on my list, I think!

Hope you all had a lovely holiday - and got some boxing in on this
beautiful day!

Autumn
