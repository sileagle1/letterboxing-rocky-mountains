From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-10-10 20:49:54 UTC
I received an e-mail from 2PUP this week and it looks like some of my
newer boxes have already gone missing. I ran out today to confirm
and sure enough we have some missing boxes in Kuna, Idaho:

Sidewinder: Confirmed MIA

Language of Purple:
Box # 1 Purple Prose Alive and Well (somehow moved to the spot that
box # 2 was in. The clues have been updated)
Box # 2 Purple Cow Confirmed MIA

Cowboy Angel: Alive and Well

Last night Lizman and I pulled the 3 remaining boxes from the
Astrology series. In total I was able to save 7 of the original 12
boxes. But I am amazed that any of them were still alive. Just like
the night before the 3 that we found were not covered up at all.
They were just tossed back on top of their original hiding spots. No
rocks, sticks or leaves for camouflage. I am not sure why I am
finding my boxes so carelessly re-hidden if you can even call it
that. Yes, that is frustration you hear in my post. It only takes a
couple of minutes to properly re-hide a box for the next seekers to
find in place alive and well. Why have people become so sloppy here
in Idaho with putting them back?

Blackvelvetrav
