Hi ho Sillllverrrrr and awayyyy........

Howdy folks-

The Durango Snowdown festival has begun and there are a LOT of cool
prizes for whomever finds the hidden Silver Bullet. Those of you who
remember last year's event will recall that the clues, especially
the early ones, are very vague which keeps people glued to the radio
all week. So here are the first four:

1. North of Tuba City to Fair Dealing.

2. South of 240. (This is a county road number and it makes sense.)

3. Montrose east to Bernalillo.

4. West of 234. (Also a county road number, this clue basically
means that it is in Durango.....duh!)

So there you go, there will be five clues a day until Saturday
afternoon, when the last clue basically tells where it is if it
hasn't been found yet.

Very appreciatively,

y-nought
