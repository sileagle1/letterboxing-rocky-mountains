Pretty quiet on the list. Here's a story: I went up to the high
country for a hike and persuaded my companion to check on my Engineer
Mountain Box with me. The trail was 90 percent still covered in snow.
The three foot tall stumps in the clues are still completely buried.
I think it will be a while before that trail is ready for much
action.

This means that the creeks will be full, the flowers big, and the
bears will have something to eat this year. Much rejoicing around
here after many dry years!
