Roots & Shoots June BOM Planted

From: Kelly (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-06-10 03:51:51 UTC
Tonight I was able to plant Roots and Shoots Redmond's "Wonderstruck" June 2012 Box of the Month. Because the logbook is gigantic I opted to plant the enormous stamp with index cards. Please stamp on one side of the index cards only and I will cut and paste the images into the logbook. As a reminder this image is very large. Please bring an 8 x 11 sheet of paper or cardstock to capture the image. A big juicy ink pad and plan on taking the box to your car do you can stamp on a flat surface. You may need several attempts to stamp a clean image so multiple sheets of paper or cardstock are recommended.

I Love a Good Book BOM by Roots and Shoots Redmond - http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=193704

Happy Hunting!
