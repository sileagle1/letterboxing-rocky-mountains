From: Aljan Sundance (aljansundance@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-05-10 10:21:16 UTC-07:00
OK gang. With much generous help from my friends, I am in the final stages of getting the patches manufactured and ordered.

They will be 5 inches of fabulousness. I have yet to see the final digitizing from the patch company, but I am confident that they're going to be great.

They will be $5.00 each.

They should be ready in about three weeks. I will start taking orders in two and a half weeks or so, and you will be able to get your patches by sending me a check and a self addressed, stamped envelope.

Can someone tell me if there is a spot, either on AQ or wherever, where I should also advertise them?

Thanks to everyone for your interest in and support of this project!

I can't wait to see these patches around the boxing grounds.

~Aljan
