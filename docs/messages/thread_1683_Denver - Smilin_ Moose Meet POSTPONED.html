The Rocky Mountain Smilin' Moose Mini Meet has officially been postponed.
For those that wish to come to Denver for the Doors Open Denver event, you
should remember that it goes both the 18th and 19th, so consider that if it
was on your agenda at all.

NEW MEET DETAILS:
We're moving the meet to Saturday May 2nd from 10am to Noon. Same place,
same time ... new date. Let's pray for more hospitable weather. I've
updated the details on AQ
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1208

Cheers,
