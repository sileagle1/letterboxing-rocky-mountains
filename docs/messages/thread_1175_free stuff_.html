OK here's the deal:

I work closely with our office high-production print shop. Most of
my logbooks and box (paper) contents stuff is made from materials
that would usually be tossed/recycled. Even with me scamming stuff
every week, there's still SO much left and I thought some of you
might be interested. :-)

Please contact me offline with details of what you want and please,
BE SPECIFIC with the size and quantities of what you'd like. I'll
pick up the postage for up to 2 lbs of "stuff" each mailing - don't
worry about the postage - it also costs me nada. Be sure to
also include an address to which I am to send.

If emails are anything like when I had a couple hundred Altoid boxes
to give away....basically, if I have to take the time to reply to
your email asking for more info on what you want, I'm not really
interested in proceeding...

Having said that, I have some cool FREE stuff - COMPLETELY devoid
of "guilt" or the need to "payback". Honestly, this stuff would
just go in the bin if you don't want it! I'm only posting this here
on the RM list so things don't get outta control, so please don't
link/distribute online. As long as I'm at this job (end of Jan 2007
is my goal to be out the door), this is a standing offer anytime you
need/want more goodies down the road as the weather gets cooler and
the nights get longer for being creative.....

1) I can get my hands on reams and reams and reams of thick paper
(about like index cards) with NO LINES. Aprox 60-65 lb. weight in
all white only. Perfect for log book pages because the ink does not
bleed through. Not acid free, if that matters to ya. This paper
starts off at 8 1/2 x 11. If you just want it at this size, just
estimate how tall a stack you want in inches and I'll send it off.

BUT, (this is the really cool part): Because I also have acess to a
heavy duty power cutter I can literally cut up to the thickness of a
ream of paper or less in ANY size you need in MINUTES. Send EXACT
measurements (W X D X H (H = aka how tall a stack at EACH WXD
dimension I am to cut for you). Multi WxD (as long as you specify
H) orders NO PROBLEM! You'd be nuts not to want this offer!

2) At the Grand Lake Gathering we had a discussion on backings for
stamps. Ravenwolf really like foam core. The other day I saw a
huge piece of the MUY nice stuff in the trash, so I grabbed it.
This stuff is SCHWEET! The normal stuff is all white but you can
sorta put your fingernail into the outer layer ..over time anyway,

However, THIS stuff is stiffer than the normal stuff, but still has
some flex (unlike wood) (total width is 3/16th). Side #1 has the
graphics for which it was produced, then tossed at the end of each
month. Side #2 is all black...so would be good neutral color for
the "showing" side for the back of your stamps. Both sides are
stiffer as well slicker than normal foam core, but the overall
thickness is a bit thinner than the normal stuff. If you use some
good glue and/or sand or otherwise rough up the surface, I think
you'll like the results. I sure did.

I snagged a 3 FOOT piece which is more than I could use in a hundred
years....And I can get a huge piece this size every month! I can't
precise cut this stuff for you (doesn't fit in power cutter - as
above) so I'll get out the utility knife and rough cut it at least
1" larger than the WxD dimensions you provide.

And finally..I can't get as much of this stuff as I used to, but
still have a decent supply if you don't want it in more than a few
pieces at a time...and/or can wait a bit until my our supply stacks
up. I'll send in 8 1/2 X 11" size only, you have to cut to size:

3a) Chipboard cardboard. Great for logbook covers! NOT the
cardboard box type stuff (with the "wavy bit" between the "covers"),
but rather the thin stuff...like thick cardstock really.

3b) Regular paper weight (20lb) COLORED paper in standard Xerox
colors (nothing "groovy" but if you like grey, red, green, violet,
pink, blue and such), speak up and I'll send ya a "grab bag" when I
have some to get rid of.... Full 8 1/2 x 11 size only, you cut to
size. If you can live with one side being printed on rather than
both sides blank, but edges dinged (and therefore won't run through
photocopiers)...please let me know - I can get my hands on more of
one-sided stuff.

Reuse, recycle (p)rebox on!

The Order Desk is now open! I promise to mail w/in 7-10 work days
(probally WAY less!) from when your offline email hits my inbox.
