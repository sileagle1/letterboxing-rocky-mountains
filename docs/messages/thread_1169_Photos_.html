From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2006-09-04 19:24:50 UTC
Following is the first part of a new series of short stories meant
to while away the hours for a special Y guy who was unable to attend
the "largest yet" gathering of the Rocky Mtn. Letterboxers of
America (hey, where's our patch? But, that's another story...)

So, dedicated to you, Y Guy! We missed you and all the
other "crazed" boxers who were unable to attend! Here it goes!

It was a dark and stormy nighterrrdark night! The lone fireman
added more wood to the fire in his fire pit hoping to chase away any
unseen hobgoblins and protect his invited guests from the local
moose. Everyone new of the crazed moose that struck down the poor
town elder in Main street in the center of townhe didn't want this
for his guests in his own back yard!

Back to the fireit was a huge pit that the fireman and his
beautiful lady, Aljan had put in recently to warm the nights and add
the ambiance for tales, such as this! The wonderful deck off the
back of the house added views into the thick pine forest, and, kept
the moose at bay!

But suddenly, from the quiet of the night, a sound was heard
munching! On the edge of the forest, not far from the fire, was the
munching Moose and friend! YOW! They were formidable and not to be
messed with! Cappy shone his light for all to see the awesome power
of the crazed beings! The fireman was of a fearsome heritage and
lead a party into the brush to check on his opponent. There,
nestled down (can crazed moose nestle?) amongst the grass and
needles of the forest floor, settling in for the night. For, even
the crazed moose knew of the warmth that the fireman brought to them
on special nights and the wafting of somemores was like sugarplums
dancing in their heads!

Somemores! YUM! The kids then turned crazed! It was too much fun
poking those marshmallows into the fire and then with Aljan's
special touch of graham crackers and Peach's favchocolatethey
yummed down their tasty treats!

As the moon shone thru the pines, and the crazed moose snored away,
a few of the grand, grand, gatherers went their wayfor tomorrow was
the day for all to meet and exchange stamps and stories!

Stay tuned, for part 2 of Peach's story!
