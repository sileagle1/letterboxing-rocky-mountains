A month or so ago I took Raisin down to NW Pets to have her picture
taken for the Pet Friendly Magazine Dog Cover Contest. Raisin was a
total wild child at the photo shoot and I swore that her pictures
would not turn out. I was able to look at the proofs on the web
about a week later and even though one of her pictures turned out I
honestly thought it was not one of her better shots. She just looked
so sad in it and it was not a very "crisp" shot either. My copy of
the picture arrived in the mail about a week later and I stuck it up
on the frig not giving it a second thought. That was until Saturday
when I received a call from Pet Friendly Magazine.

Apparently Raisin's picture has been chosen out of about 130 pictures
as one of 8 finalists to be featured at the Family Pet Expo April
28th & 29th at the Idaho Fairgrounds. Visitors at the Pet Expo will
have a chance to vote on their favorite dog at the expo out of the 8
finalists. The dog with the most votes will be picked to be featured
on the Cover of Pet Friendly Magazine as the People's Choice. The
other 7 dogs that are not selected will still have their picture
inside the magazine some time during the year. So if you have a
chance to stop by and cast a vote I know Miss. Raisin would love the
support. Although she remained remarkably unfazed by her new found
celebrity when I shared the good news with her on Saturday.

Family Pet Expo
April 28-29
Idaho Fair Grounds
Saturday 10 a.m. 5 p.m.
Sunday 10 a.m. 4 p.m.
$3.00 for adults
Children 12 and under free

This annual event encourages interaction between pet owners and pet
companies. Hundreds of new products and services will be available
for guests to view, sample and share with their pets of all kinds.

Doggie Hugs and Smiles from Raisin!

P.S. Raisin has new pictures posted in her album on the RML album
page.
