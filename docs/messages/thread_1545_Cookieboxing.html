OK, it's not pie, but...

Today I went and found a letterbox that Rose Girl, my daughter, planted
for me for Father's Day. Her first plant. Sniff. They grow up so fast...

Anyway, I was surprised to find the first finder prize was cookies.
Shortbread to be exact. How awesome is that? (I was even more surprised
a bear had not eaten my Father's Day letterbox in the week or so it has
been there.) Ever found edibles in a letterbox?

Anyway, the Happy Father's Day cookie box will be a semi-private box
because there are, like, two pages in the log book. But, if you are in
CO Springs with a couple of hours to kill and are looking for a
challenging summer hike with some refreshing water fun at the end, let
me know and I'll send you the clues. You may have to bring your own log
book pages.

'Box Happy,
JeLyBean
