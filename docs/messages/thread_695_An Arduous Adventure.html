An Arduous Adventure

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2005-05-25 03:53:45 UTC
It's been two days since the final two in the fellowship finished
their trek to the top of the Morgul Stair. It was no easy feat, since
the fair elvish princess Aspenita needed help in the cooler morning
hours down in the fair city near the Anduin River. That meant that the
travelers must bear the midafternoon temperatures starting at 107* and
the full waterskins they started with were soon emptied. Heat
shimmered off the rocks as they took shelter under a large boulder
that hid the first treasure of the day, Juggling the Rocks. After
checking closely for orcs, the two continued up the stairs, stopping
often as the sun beat down merciless. After taking twice the normal
time to get up there, the two triumphed over the poisonous, evil
Shelob, in her dark,webbed,lair. As they waved the fabulous journal in
the air and danced from foot to foot, they noticed in the distance,
the eagles were coming, the eagles were coming!

Thanks for a fabulous adventure, Y!
