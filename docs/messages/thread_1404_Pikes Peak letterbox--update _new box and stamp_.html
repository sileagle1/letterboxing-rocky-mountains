Pikes Peak letterbox--update (new box and stamp)

From: Ronda (TXAFFamily@aol.com) | Date: 2007-09-07 19:56:32 UTC
A couple of weeks ago, our family ventured to the Crystal Reservoir
at Pikes Peak and located this box (bag). We were quite disappointed
to find no stamp, and from the looks of the logbook entries, many
finders felt the same way upon finding this "box". When I contacted
Serena, the owner, she informed me that she is now living in CT and
is unable to maintain the box. So...she gave her blessing for me to
assume ownership of this LB. As of this morning, a "real" box was
placed in the same location including a new logbook and new
handcarved stamp (Old logbook is still there as well). I've also
included a couple of "First Finders" gifts :-) Please give this box a
second find next time you are in the area! Preboxed's "Pike's Hike"
and "Big Foot X-ing" are nearby as well :-)

This box is also now listed on Atlasquest under the author's
trailname "Sunfloweerr" for now. I'll soon be assuming ownership and
making a few minor adjustments in the clues (For instance, there is
no longer a small log in the trail), but the clues should still be
able to lead one to the box.

Happy hunting!

TX AF Family
