From: front range hiker (frhiker@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-04-12 00:04:18 UTC
I replanted my original Triceratops box today in a new location, at
the Natural History Museum in Morrison Colorado. If you never found
the original, or just want to check out a fun family place, more
information can be found on Atlas Quest or LBNA.

The museum normally charges an admission fee, but they are having a
free day on Saturday April 12th, from 10 am to 6 pm. It is close to
outlying Doors Open Denver venues if you are planning to participate
in our DOD event.
