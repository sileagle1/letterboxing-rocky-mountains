Alpine Pal Box Update - Lakewood, Colorado

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-08-07 14:28:20 UTC
My sister e-mailed me that there was a 300 acre fire on Green Mountain
Monday night in Lakewood, Colorado which is an open space just to the
south of her home. They even had to evacuate that night. All is okay
now and no homes were lost. They were able to return home on Tuesday.
My sister planted the Alpine Pal Letterbox for me on a trail on Green
Mountain and we are assuming that it was probably consumed by the
fire. For the time being the status of the box will remain unknown
until we can verify if it was destroyed or by some miracle it
survived.
