From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2008-05-06 04:32:20 UTC
--- In RMLetterboxing@yahoogroups.com, "Baqash" wrote:
>Hi!

Best Friends is a very cool place to spend some time. It is my daughter's favorite place in
the world! There is a box on the premises of Best Friends, also there is one right outside
Zion NP in the little town of Springdale. There are several great ones in St. George, but
also a couple of less than good as well. I'll have to go through my journal tomorrow and
let you know which ones I have. Cedar City has a few nice boxes, and there is a great one
in Parowan, just north of Cedar City. There are a couple of other boxes in the Zion-Kanab
area near hwy. 89 that I haven't had time to hike to yet, and there is a good box at the
Zion entrance to Kolob Canyon off I-15 south of Cedar City. There is one listed in
Hurricane, but we couldn't find it as the area had a new subdivision built there, and it was
at the end of a busy day when we hunted for it. Anyway, it's a beautiful fun area with or
without boxes!
