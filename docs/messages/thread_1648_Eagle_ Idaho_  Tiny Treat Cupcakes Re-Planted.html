am attempting a third plant by changing the box to one of my Hit and
Run Magnetic containers. I like to call them a Hit and Run box because

black magnetic boxes are small and not completely waterproof (I
discovered this past winter) they do not contain logbooks. The clues
have altered very slightly from the original clues.
