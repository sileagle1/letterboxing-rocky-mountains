From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2005-04-12 21:35:28 UTC

Okay, I figured everyone would approve, so check out the pics! I have
more, but I didn't want to use up all the allowed space. When you all
have pictures ready to post, feel free to delete any of mine to make
room for more!

Y-nought....we got 8 first finders! YOWWIE! We are excited! We were
just ahead of Ravenwolf and her group and running into ABear was so
COOL! We loved the Portal Hike and really, really, recommend Negro
Bill's Canyon to those who will be going back...it's beautiful! We
picked up a couple boxes as we left Monday including ES's Dewey
bridge, which we LOVE your stamp, ES! Right after leaving there, we
spotted a group of turkey vultures sunning on some rancher's post.
They were beautiful, if somewhat "vultury"! Then a cow was on the
road, and we felt like we were being stopped from leaving this
wonderful adventure! I snapped a picture of the cow....too funny!

Let me know what you think of the pics!

Hugs, Peach

Oh no, Ramdelt, what's a letterboxer to do??? Actually, we arrived
Friday afternoon about the same time as Ravenwolf's group did. It
seems we were just ahead of them on many of the boxes. We do feel a
bit sheepish about being first finders on so many...WHO KNEW???
Although, 4 of our first finders were because we did the Portal
Trail hike which had 4 new boxes on the same trail and that wasn't
until Saturday morning! It was quite a hike and feel we definitely
worked to earn them! 2 were at Dead Horse Point when we ran into
the placers, Saturday afternoon! GADS! So, actually, it isn't as
bad as it appears, it's not like we were racing around Moab Friday!
Our first finds were on 2 trails that we did on Saturday, when most
of you were there! So, don't be too hard on me! And, there were 11
more boxes we found that we weren't the first finders. And, many,
many more boxes that we didn't have time for. So, Ramdelt,
please,please go easy on me....how about some pie to make things
better?!
