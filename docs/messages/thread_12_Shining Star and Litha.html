Hi All! We regrouped and returned to Mt. Evans to find the Shining
Star LB. It was an absolutely beautiful hike! We also hiked to
find Litha last night. Thanks Autumn, it was such a great view! We
live just south of there. We were the second finders and we were a
bit bummed that the first finders didn't leave a stamp...did they

hidden, etc. If you had it on this one, it is now gone. I'm up to
12 FOUND, hee-hee...should I order a patch????

Re: Shining Star and Litha

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-07-13 15:36:10 UTC-06:00
*giggle*

I'm so glad to hear that you found Litha. No - whoever found it first,
didn't say ANYTHING. They didn't stamp, just left a note or something??
Funhog (from Oregon) emailed me to tell me she hunted for it and couldn't
locat it on the rock wall, so I clarified the clues a bit. Glad you found
it, I was a tad worried.

The reason that box had nothing else in it was because it was planted on the
hike. Eric and I hiked up to find Rain Cloud, but missed a clue along the
way (that tree with the woodpecker's handiwork slipped by unnoticed).
Anywhoo ... we got to the power poles, and on a whim, sat down and carved a
stamp, and hid that box. We weren't prepared for doing so, and didn't have
sheets to leave in the box like the others. Ah well!! Not bad for a spur
of the moment, eh?

Thanks for letting me know about it, and that its safe and well!

:)
Autumn
