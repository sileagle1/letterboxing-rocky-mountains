I have had a few questions about the ingredients in the Taco Soup
Recipe. In case you are not familiar with these items:

Shoepeg corn is a variety of white sweetcorn valued for its sweetness.
It is characterized by small, narrow kernels that form uneven rows on
the cob. I have always found the corn in my local Albertson's store
in the canned vegetable isle.

Rotel Tomatoes are a Brand of canned Tomatoes: http://www.ro-
tel.com/pages/index_flash.html

I am sure you could substitute items if you do not have the exact
ingredients. The beauty of this recipe is it is easy and flavorful.
