No no no... You have this all wrong.

There WE were, enjoying food on a stake, watching some entertainment, when I looked over and what did my wolfy eyes spot? A Y! In a kilt! A Y I knew even! What chance! What luck!

We rushed over and much merriment ensued! We even got to meet his lovely bride and chat for a bit. Even Elros wandered over with his lady friend in tow (although, he was rolling his eyes, and had that look that said 'oh man - it's those crazy letterboxers. We can't go anywhere without them showing up'. =3 )

Kidding, kidding. Many smiles and hugs were exchanged.

Next time we will have to put a shout out that we are going to be there. Sorry we missed you, BoxTops! We are actually planning another foray later in the year. If it pans out, we will be sure to say something.

Merry meet, Sir Y and Lady Paloma!
