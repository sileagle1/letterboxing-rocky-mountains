No comparing silver hairs... mine have somehow 'vanished'. I'll be
one of those 127 year old ladies playing bingo with elegantly coiffed
pink hair. Woot!!

If you're still searching for the location, the ren faire is between
Colorado Springs and Denver in a very small town called 'Larkspur'.
It's basically a grocery/gas store.. and a renaissance village. My
favorite kind. If you type Colorado Renaissance Festival into
google, it'll pop up the page and it has maps. I'd do a link but I'm
at work and it won't let me open a second window. I'm being
repressed.

Because of my schedule (yes, back to that again) I won't know a date
for sure until the month before. And, yes, the faire is certainly
farther from Idaho than Fort Collins is! I would completely
understand it if y'all want to meet there instead. I, myself, will
still be going to the faire since, well, that's what pirates do.
hehehe. A year just isn't a year until I have my day at the faire. :)

You're so kind about the stamp and box. I'm so lucky and grateful to
be involved with such a fun group of boxers!
