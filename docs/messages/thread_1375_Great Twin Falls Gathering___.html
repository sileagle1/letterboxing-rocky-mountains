Great Twin Falls Gathering!!!

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-30 03:59:39 UTC
Great gathering at Twin Falls! A huge thank you to DragonRider for
all his preparations, planning and watching over us as we braved the
heat at the Falls to look for all the great boxes that had been
planted. I have never drank so much lemonade in my life but I was so
thankful that the jug was full and that it was ice cold! My hats off
for spoiling us rotten while we were in town.

It was wonderful to see DragonRider, BoxerLover and Trouble Bug again
this year. Always good times when you can box with familiar faces.
And we so enjoyed meeting and boxing with Singing Wolf, Howling
Coyote, Ronnie Penguin and A Bug. We really appreciated everyone
making the effort to come to Idaho for our second gathering this
year. Now if we can just tempt everyone to Boise again some time.
So many boxes, never enough time.

It was also a lot of fun for Lizman and I to travel with our favorite
letterboxing buddy A-Bear on the way to Twin Falls. She is a true
partner in crime boxing from sun up to sun down in all kinds of
weather over the years. Gotta love those that share the addiction
and understand your need to find "just one more" before calling it a
day.

I will post some pictures on the event album but will also add them
to an album in Kodak Gallery in the next couple of days. We shot
lots of them and there are too many to add to the actual event
album. Attendees can send me a direct e-mail with their e-mail
address and I will send over the Kodak Gallery link once I have them
up on the site.

Safe travels to everyone as they head home and we hope to see
everyone out on the trail again soon! Happy Hunting!

Blackvelvetrav & Lizman
