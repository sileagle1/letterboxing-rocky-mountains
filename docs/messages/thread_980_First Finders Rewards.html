on Saturday) and need ideas for first finder rewards. I've only been
first on two boxes and was impressed with the creativity of the first
finder items.

best first finder item that you have planted or found? Are
certificates better, or would you prefer other (dare I say...) "stuff"
that relates to the theme of the box? I got a cute Dilbert doll from
the Dilbox LB that I would never call junk ;-)

they take!

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2006-01-30 18:20:22 UTC
I missed that first note also! I usually do something simple with
matted colored papers and pictures of the stamp or the location! I
like to leave something, but sometimes I run out of time before a
trip or a planting and I end up with something quick and simple. I
try to always make something to fit in a journal, because I love
finding ff's that fit in my journal. Then it is always with the
stamp! I love the charms that Es leaves in her ff's. I leave them on
the ff and they just happily bulge in my journal! One favorite ff
was a necklace of an alien crop circle and that ff I made a pocket
in my journal to keep it in.

Kat (who has a big sad lip from not finding ANY ff's for soooo long)
