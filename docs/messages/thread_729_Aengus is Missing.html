Aengus is Missing

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-10 17:52:10 UTC
I am sad to report that all that Ravenwolf found of poor Aengus was a
few shreds of a logbook and a piece of a stamp. Apparently, the marmot
mentioned in the clues developed a taste for Irish poetry over the
winter. The letterbox was made of wood and was wrapped in a gallon bag.
I checked on this box two weeks ago and there were a few teethmarks on
the outside of the box. I had no idea that he would go for the contents.

I'll be carving a new stamp and putting another box together to place
nearby. I rather liked Aengus and I really liked the fact that a few
people had visited him. Hopefully some of you will get a chance to
visit the new one too.

y-nought
