From: Jana J. Riska (jjcadenza@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-02-10 09:04:21 UTC-07:00
Don't forget that I'm itching to do one up here around
Yellowstone/Jackson/the Grand Tetons. I am looking toward doing one this
summer, but I'm working on seeing when there might be some cabin
availability. Does a Yellowstone area gathering sound interesting to
y'all? (Don't worry or flame me though, I'm not advocating leaving boxes in
the National Parks.) I think it could be a kick. There are some fun boxes
out here. You have to travel a little bit more to catch them all, but what
fun travel it is! There are boxes on either sides of the parks that are
easily accessible, and if you want to drive a bit more out of the way or if
you are coming from the east, there are a bunch going through Cody and
Wapiti. Cody is a kick of a tourist town if you are into cowboys, rodeo and
such. It was founded by Buffalo Bill Cody and flaunts it to the umph
degree.

I don't want to plan much though if you all can't make it because, well
frankly, I'm the only letterboxer in this half of my state that would be
willing to come to a gathering. I recognize that it is hard to say without
knowing a date, but in general, do you think we could have enough interest
to draw people up into this fun country?

Cadenza
