Connecticut coming to Utah-I smell a gathering

From: (cadenza74@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-11-23 23:48:20 UTC-07:00
Butterfly and I have been talking for a while now about her coming to Salt
Lake City to meet up with her kids from Hawaii. She was shocked at the
dearth of letterboxes in these parts, to which I explained that there is a
dearth of letterboxers in these parts. I think she is still a bit blown
away by the fact that letterboxing is still pretty new to the Rocky Mountain
Area.

Anyway, she is planning on bringing a bunch of carvings out from other Conn.
boxers so they can leave a collection for us to work with. She and I have
been talking about the possibilities of meeting on January 4 and 5. I
think she has at least a dozen offers to send new letterboxes at this point.

I have been excited to meet her and then the thought occurred to us that
maybe this would be a good chance to let others know about her coming and we
could arrange a gathering for any letterboxers who might like to come from
other areas. I know there are only a handful of boxers in Northern UT so I
decided to post the invite here and see if we are able to get any kind of
response.

Let me know if any of you are interested in meeting. I would love to meet
any and all boxers that I can. This should be a lot of fun! I hope some of
you might be able to make it!
