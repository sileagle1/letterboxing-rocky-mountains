Incident Date November 16, 2007 Time 8:42:00 AM Division Gold Hill
Shift I
Title Other Location 4065 N. Cheyenne Canyon
Summary Officers were dispatched to Cheyenne Canyon on a suspicious
package that was left in a picnic ground. Officers responded and found
a box looking object that was dark in color and had tape around it. The
object was sitting on the ground next to a picnic table approximately
25 feet from the road. Officers thought the object was suspicious and
called for the bomb squad. Bomb Techs, Sgt Fraiser and Sgt. Clayton
responded and x-rayed the package. It was determined that the package
was not an explosive device.
