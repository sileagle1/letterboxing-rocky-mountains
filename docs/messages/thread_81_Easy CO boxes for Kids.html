Easy CO boxes for Kids

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-09-02 04:42:42 UTC
Let's see ...
The two boxes at Chatfield (take your bug spray, though).
The Peninsula box in Frisco (just did that one myself today).
The Grand Lake box
The WoTY- Yule box
The Garden of the Gods (Little Feet) box - Colo. Springs
All three boxes at the Plains Conservation Center in Aurora - though
Coyote Canter is over a mile (guess it depends on the stamina of your
kids)
The boxes in the Bailey area would be easy enough for kids -
definitely the Dragon's Lair, though the Abert's Squirrel might be
tricky with a toddler - even with a pack. Bring another adult with
you.

I try to put in my clues if the hike and terrain are kid friendly.
When in doubt about a specific box you plan to get, just ask the
group!

And welcome to the list, Debbi!
Autumn
