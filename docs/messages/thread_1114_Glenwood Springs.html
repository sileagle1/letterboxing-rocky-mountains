Glenwood Springs

From: bsktbug2 (cswaim@bresnan.net) | Date: 2006-06-06 15:49:37 UTC
Hey Preboxed - we want to thank you for a hike up to the mine! My
little sister is in Denver on business and had called yesterday to see
if we wanted to meet halfway for supper. So, Glenwood it was.
My son printed out a couple of boxes to find, but we only had time for
one.
Yes, we did see the marmot heading up the hill, poking around in what
looked to be washing machines or some sort of trash. The kids were
pretty excited and we loved looking, but not touching, the mine.
I have to say though, my dh was pretty sure we were going to be
arrested for going on mining company property. It was pretty funny,
and he was the first one back to the truck with our 2 year old on his
shoulders!
What an adventure, we're going back for the Riverwalk box! and the No
Name box!!
Also, it was our first time being FF! And our first hitchiker!
Yeah! We are soooooo hooked!
