From: cadenza74 (cadenza74@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-12-15 07:41:46 UTC

I've just had a thought and if I post more on the big list, I may
kill some cats :0

I haven't been able to dig in for a while with my computer woes, but
I let myself break the rules a bit and use my computer at work to
check a couple of personal things, then I decided to check if I had
any mail at Atlas Quest and now here I am having spent nearly four
hours going through Atlas quest, LBNA, the main list, this one,
catching up in Mark and Sue Pepe's blog (which I love!) and reading
tributes to classic letterboxers.

Wow! I'd forgotten how much I have learned to love this community.
It doesn't seem comprehensible that it has been nearly two years
since I began letterboxing. I had no idea what I was beginning when
I clicked on a tiny reference in my email newsletter. I need to get
a new computer so I can do this in smaller increments (I say that but
it seemed to take about as long when I was checking regularly too!)I
just wanted to take a moment to express gratitude to my fellow
addicts for the quirky community we are. It is so fun to be a part
of all of you!

I better go get some sleep so I can wake up in the morning.

Happy holidays!
Cadenza
