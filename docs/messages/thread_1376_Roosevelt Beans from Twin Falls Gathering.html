Roosevelt Beans

For those that were at the Twin Falls Gathering on Saturday I though
I would send along the recipe for the Roosevelt Beans I brought
along. This was the recipe I received from the Roosevelt Lodge in
Yellowstone National Park. I made a double batch for the gathering.
I cooked the meat and onions and then tossed everything into the
crock pot instead of the oven since were on the road for the
gathering. Also I was not able to find the exact size can of beans
the recipe called for so I used the sizes that I was able to find.

1 lb. Hamburger or Sausage
lb. Bacon Diced
1 Onion Diced
1 16 oz. can Pork and Beans
1 12 oz. can Kidney Beans
1 12 oz. can Lima Beans
1 12 oz. can Butter Beans
Cup Brown Sugar
2 Tbsp. Cider Vinegar
1 Tbsp. Prepared Mustard
Cup Ketchup
Salt and Pepper to Taste

Fry meats. Drain fat. Sautee onions with meat. Stir in next 10
ingredients. For a thicker dish, drain liquid from beans. Bake at
325 for 45 minutes.

Enjoy! Blackvelvetrav & Lizman
