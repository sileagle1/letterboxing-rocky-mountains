From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-08-16 17:03:52 UTC-06:00
We got home Saturday night. What a great trip! We left Denver and
headed for Rawlins. Then on to West Yellowstone (never made it to
Cody)... then down to Jackson and on to Rock Springs. From there we

time) and then back to Denver.
