I have to agree with Y about the people we have gotten to know in this game. I didn't get
to climb Mt. Doom because of my knee, but that was alright because the company was
even more fun than the stamping!!!

I think my favorite boxes were those that had the clues woven into a story, and there are
some in our group that are masters at it. I have two favorite story boxes: one is about a
frozen dog, giant, and a witch, and the other is about aliens, ancient art, and the arboc.

Another couple of favorites were found in a couple of different canyons right after a hard,
quick thunderstorm, and the power of the waterfalls and flashflooding off the sandstone
were too wonderfully incredible to put into words.

One day we had a great time taking our 4-wheelers out to find a couple of different boxes
dealing with dinosaurs.

There are definitely places I wouldn't have gone or found without boxes, including some
caverns in VA last summer, that were so fun to wander through. I love boxes that tell
some history of certain places, and even though I have been to some spots before, I learn
things about that spot that I didn't know before that box! Eeew, starting to babble, time to
go!
