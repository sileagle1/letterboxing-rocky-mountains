Woot!

That's a great place and a great time of year. I have tentative plans
to be gone in the first part of August, but if they fall through I
would love to do an exchange.

There are letterboxes on both sides of the pass, but not so many that
you couldn't get them all. Also, for the east side you might want to
look at the Del Norte boxes. And there are some boxes on the west side
in and around Pagosa Springs. A longish hike leads to a very early
Drewclan box by some waterfalls, if you have the better part of a day
to spend on one letterbox. The wild mushrooms are awesome on that trail
if you hit the timing right.

If you get the chance to come as far as Durango you won't want to miss
the Animas Overlook letterboxes. There are six letterboxes all by
different placers on a small loop trail....very nice.

Let me know if you have any more questions about anything in this neck
of the woods ....either here or at edwebbe@yahoo.com.
