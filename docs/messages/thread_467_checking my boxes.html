checking my boxes

From: gonefromnj (gonefromnj@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-31 23:37:10 UTC

Sadly, the Lemony Letterbox is definitely gone. It appears as
though there was some grooming done on the trail in October or
November (don't know when the box was last found, check your books)
and the tree that was well surrounded by dense weeds was given a
crewcut. I am going to call South Suburban Parks and Recreation to
see if it was turned in to lost & found by their maintenence crew,
but I am not holding my breath.

Dilbox is still safe, though it was peeking from its hiding place
and could have been easily spotted by a non-boxer. I hid it better
and hopefully anyone else who finds it will hide it even better.

Found with Bite My Shiny Letter Box that the trail and tree had also
been groomed recently, but luckily someone had moved the box a bit
further from the trail and tree but in the same general area (and
put it in a Ziploc as well, for some reason). We checked out
JodieMKern's husband's stamp and it is really cool!!!

So the lesson learned: don't hide your letterboxes too close to a
South Suburban trail.
