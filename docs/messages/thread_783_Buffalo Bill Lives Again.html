Buffalo Bill Lives Again

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-16 23:28:55 UTC
Skeetch and I finally made it back up Lookout Mountain and, sadly,
Buffalo Bill was indeed gone. No sign of any part of it anywhere.. we
really searched the entire compound. We suspect that some boxers were
in a hurry to leave and didn't wait until no one was around to rehide
it and were seen. There were about 127 people 10 feet away from the
spot while we were there.. the tourists are out in force!! No
Springer Spaniels in sight, but someone did have an Irish Wolfhound.
But I digress.

I just happened to have brought along another box and it is now hidden
safely far from the madding crowd. I'll post the clues asap. It's an
entirely new box.. new journal.. new stamp of a new image. I consider
it another find for anyone who's been to the old one because, well,
it's all new and in a new spot! Woot!

Es, who never finds pie and icy sodas on the trail
