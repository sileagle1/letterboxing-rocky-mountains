From: Debbie Kotlarek (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-12-01 20:14:12 UTC-06:00
Well, I'm not in the Rocky Mountain region, but here in Wisconsin one of the
few places we can find the camo Duck Tape is Wal-Mart. It seems some stores
stock it in the paint department with other colored tapes. However in my
area it is only carried seasonally and is found in the hunting/fishing area.
I just stock up in the fall so I have enough to last most of the year.

I like the Duck Tape because it tears easily, conforms to container shapes
well and isn't too shiny. It's also relatively inexpensive.

Wisconsin Hiker
