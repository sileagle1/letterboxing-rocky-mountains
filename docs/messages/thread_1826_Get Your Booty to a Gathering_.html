Get Your Booty to a Gathering!

From: rvn_wolf (treikya@gmail.com) | Date: 2011-03-21 16:58:01 UTC
Ahoy!

Springtime is upon us. I know what you're thinking. Man I should get out there and plant some stuff for my fellow letterboxers to enjoy.

Before you do - come see us on April 9th! Lots of newbies in the area, I'm hoping to put some names to faces. And you veteran letterboxers - yeah you, reading this right now - you know who you are! Don't be shy! People want to get to know you!

I'll be doing a carving workshop. Bring your tools, something you are working on, and your questions. we'll do some transfers and talk about materials too.

TaiChi is also going to show us how to make letterbox pouches, and give us some tips on encryption.

Sign up here: http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=2069

Looking forward to seeing you!

~RW
