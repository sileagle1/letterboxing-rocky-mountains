Re: Roxborough letterbox?

From: gonefromnj (gonefromnj@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-14 14:01:42 UTC

To be honest, I've only hiked Carpenter's Peak twice. It took me 8
years after the first time to even consider doing it again. Don't
get me wrong, I think the payoff is outstanding, but I just didn't
enjoy the hike all that much either time. Maybe its just me. So,
if you were to hide a box up there, I agree that there are a lot of
hiding spots but there would be limited traffic and I probably
wouldn't be stamping in until about 2012 (my son will be driving by
then - yikes!).

My goal in placing boxes has been to try to find places that seekers
might not otherwise go. That way, they're not only finding the box,
but finding a new trail or park, as well. In that sense,
Carpenter's Peak might be a great place, but I think any place in
Roxborough that will bring people to this hidden gem of a park would
be worthwhile.

Just my opinion...
