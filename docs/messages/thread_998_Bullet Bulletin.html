Sorry Kat...I..um...missed a couple of clues and had to wait until
this evening's update to fill in the blanks. I pulled off the road
when they came on the radio...but I still missed one of them. Anyway,
here they are:

1. North of Tuba City to Fair Dealing.

2. South of 240

3. Montrose east to Bernalillo.

4. West of 234

5. The Frosty.......(I will have to wait 'till tomorrow for the rest
of this one).

6. Bayfield zip minus 1679. (Okay, Bayfield is 81122 which leaves us

looked up 247-9443 and it is a private residence...poor guy).

7. Hop right above it. (I was thinking a dance studio is above the
location?? I could use other ideas).

8. Walk in the direction that the walking man just came from. (???)

9. Remember when the Bullet was downtown? (I know it has been hidden
downtown for the last two years, maybe this means it isn't this year?)

10. Turn at the light just before the river, park at the third light
after that. (Believe me, I'm running through the possibilities, but
there are four main river crossings in Durango.)

And then there was a bonus clue announced that I had to pick up at a
local coffee shop--- When you find the Bullet please be careful. The
Bullet is enclosed in a priceless piece of homemade artwork.

These clues seem a little easier than last year so I'm thinking it
will be found sooner. Just a hunch.

Thanks for playing with me,
