From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-06-13 01:08:25 UTC
Well, we are doing are darndest to get you all over to our side of
the mountain! :)

The Splendid Buccaneers placed two more boxes today in the Zebediah
Gump story. They are:

Z Gump - Mustang
Z Gump - Sixteen Ton

I have to tell you how much fun I'm having researching all the boxes
we've placed. I've lived here for over 30 years and know basic bits
of history, but have learned so many wonderful and intersting facts
since I started doing this.
I spent a great morning at the local library this week reading a one
of a kind, hand-typed book about the history of Palisade. Aside from
the great facts I uncovered, I was highly entertained by the
editorial comments placed in the stories from the local paper that
would never be tolerated today! A great time. So, I wanted to thank
you all for getting me going... my brain is all filled with fun new
facts that my family patiently listens to. LOL

Esmerelda, patiently waiting...if you plant it..they will come!
