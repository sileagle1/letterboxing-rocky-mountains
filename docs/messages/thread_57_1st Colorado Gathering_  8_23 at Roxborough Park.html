1st Colorado Gathering! 8/23 at Roxborough Park

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-08-17 20:25:05 UTC
Okay Gang! We're going to have our very first gathering! I hope you
all can attend!

Date: 8/23/03 (That would be NEXT Saturday)
Time: 11am
Location: Roxborough Park Visitor's Center
There is a small fee to enter this Colorado State Park, unless you
have a state park pass. I believe it is $6. Sorry Min-Beagle ...
I'm pretty sure this park doesn't allow pets. :(

There are two boxes (currently) located at Roxborough. Fountain
Valley and Foot Loose. Check for clues on the LbNA website.
Potluck -- Let's bring a small dish to share!
Exchanges!
Boxing!
I even carved an event stamp for all those who attend! :)

This will be a great time to meet each other, exchange stamps, hunt
for the boxes in this park, plant some new boxes, and feast!

If anyone would like to demonstrate a microbox, stamp carving, or any
other such thing, that would be welcome!

Directions:
Take C-470 to Wadsworth and head south past Chatfield State Park.
Turn left on Waterton Road (just before the entrance to Lockheed
Martin.) Continue on Waterton Roadcrossing the South Platte River,
until it ends at North Rampart Range Road (1.6 miles) Turn right
(south) on North Rampart Range Road. Continue south past Roxborough
Village and the Foothills Water Treatment Plant. (2.3 miles) At the
intersection of North Rampart Range Road and Roxborough Park Road
(just before the entrance to Arrowhead golf course), turn left onto
Roxborough Park Road. Take the next right (about 50 yards away) to
enter the park.

So ... who's coming?
