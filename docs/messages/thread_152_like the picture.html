On a side note, let me share a personal moment that made me
smile a bit. My little sister and her husband have decided to
move out to Florida. I was talking to her about how that felt so far
away and I had to admit I was having a bit of a pity party over
feeling like I wouldn't be able to see her or my nieces much
anymore. Much as I tried to not let her hear it on the phone, I
must admit, I started to cry some. My sister (a non letterboxer
who sometimes mocks how involved I've become with this)
immediately started trying to comfort me and jumped in saying,
"don't worry Jana, we can plant lots and lots of letterboxes for you
and then you will want to come out all the time. We'll get a
letterboxing Mecca going for you!" I had to laugh that in trying to
comfort me the first thing out of her mouth is a letterboxing
comment. Now I know her enough to know she won't plant for
me, but I had to giggle a bit at her attempt to comfort me. I guess
that it's a sign that I'm hooked good. :-) Don't you love family?

Just thought I'd share with the group.
