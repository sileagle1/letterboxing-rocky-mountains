From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2003-07-31 15:23:49 UTC
Three Nevada letterboxes are missing! The Welcome Center box (we
actually saw the empty box); the One Armed Bandit (which we
confirmed with somebody else that we were in the correct spot); and
the micro box on the strip. We were so bummed! The Welcome Center
box and the Micro box were in too public of places (in our humble
opinion) and maybe a non-letterboxer decided to keep them. Who
knows! But, it was very frustrating, especially as we spent too
much time in very HOT temperatures to look for them just to discover
them missing! GADS! We did find the one in St. George and LOVED
it! Hope you all can go find it sometime! We also found a couple
in southern CA that were fun. We hope to plant some here now that
we're back from our trip.

Dixie
