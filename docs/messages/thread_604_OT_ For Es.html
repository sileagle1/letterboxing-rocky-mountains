Gaura, I have a stamp carved for my sister's wedding. It was supposed
to be planted on her wedding day at the beach in Charleston where she
was supposed to be married. I carved a stamp of the symbol on top of
her wedding invite, which just made her giggle. The beach wedding was
uprooted by a tropical storm, and it was moved indoors. I still went
out that day though, trying to find a spot near that beach to plant
the box. I finally decided that maybe it wasn't the best idea to
plant a letterbox on a beach with a hurricane coming!! I ran around
the beach and collected some shells to keep in a letterbox (I've never
been so wet!) and finally decided I could plant the box somewhere in
the Salt Lake Valley, since that is where she grew up. I will call it
the "Relocation Letterbox." It was a fun adventure just getting out
of Charleston that weekend. It certainly makes for an interesting
story about my box. I can't wait to hide it!

Cadenza
