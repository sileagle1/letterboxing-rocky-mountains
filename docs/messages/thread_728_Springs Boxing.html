So Saturday I was supposed to do a craft show/barbecue in
Johnstown...left home at 430am to get there at 6am to set up.
Waited for the rain to stop...and waited ....and waited....by 10 am
they cancelled for the day. So we go for breakfast at a cute cafe
then drive home in the rain. I get home at 1ish/take my daughter to
get her hair cut.. All the time thinking...I could go to the springs
to find some boxes... Well its not raining here...yet...or in the
springs. So I leave at 230 and see how many I can get in before it
gets dark/or rains. I lucked out...it threatened but really no more
than a few sprinkles. First went for Lark Bunting--what a great view
from this box. Then on to Chantress-what a gem of a park in a
residential area. Then to Pulpit rock. I get there at 630 or so
and JelyBean says its less than a mile so no problem to make it
before the sun sets. Longest mile Ive ever walked..... I think he
took a helicopter!! lol... It was great ......beautiful hike/and
great stamp.
I thought about watching the sunset from there but then Id probably
stumble back in the dusk. I did get back just as the sun was setting.
Gotta love that croc!!!

Did I mention I had 2 hours sleep the night before? I think we all
need intervention! Lets see this sunday Ill be done with my
committments at 1pm (trail cleaning at Lory SP)---how many boxes can
I find before sunset?

Sorry Kathy I didnt call before going to the springs you but it was
