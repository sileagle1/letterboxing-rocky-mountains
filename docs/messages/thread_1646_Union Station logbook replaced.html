Union Station logbook replaced

From: aeckert1 (aeckert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-12-30 19:49:12 UTC

I placed the fourth logbook in my little Union Station letterbox today,
so it's again ready for more visitors. I can't believe it's lasted as
long as it has, though if it were always in the condition I found it in
today, it would have been long gone. I figure it must have been
muggled, because everything was jammed inside so that the box wouldn't
close and it was replaced so that it was clearly visible to passers
by. I really doubt that any of our area letterboxers would have left
it that way.

I would have been really disappointed to lose the logbook that I took
out of there today. It had Astro D's 500th find in it, Autumn's old
stamp (the one lost on Mt Doom), some DNC finders and a host of other
stamps and notes that I would have missed out on. Needless to say, I'm
glad that I checked on it when I did.

allemeyn
