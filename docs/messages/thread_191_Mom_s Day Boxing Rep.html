From: tlrewok (teri@adminpowerinc.com) | Date: 2004-05-10 16:13:24 UTC
Hi All!! Spent Mom's day out in the world doing some LBing. Took my
Mom with us and got a new convert!! Went and snagged Tigger. This
place is extremly busy!! Went to go and get Oh Dear, but my Miss
Trinity Jah decided to run in front of a bicycle so we had to leave
when she decided to throw a tantrum. PLEASE NOTE: This place was
super busy!! People everywhere on foot, on bike, on blades. Watch
your kids!! The guy, with his kids right behind him, was going way
to fast for given conditions (I am pretty sure he was over the POSTED
15mph since he locked up his brakes), not to mention the very rude
comment of something along the lines of "Good Job Mom". I have taken
a lot of time in teaching my daughter "the rules of the road" so to
speak. But, as with most kids excitement got the best of her. The
guy had at least 100 yards of visability and certainly had plenty of
time to see that there was a little kid walking along the bike path.
SO my point is just to watch your kids and teach them the best you
can. Now back to your report!! After visiting a furniture store we
decided that we could do another box and went and visited Woody The
Beaver. He is doing great, but I worry about the moisture with no
baggy. Probably won't have much moisture but definately something to
think about. My mom has decided that we will try and do this every
weekend. What took her so long!! I've been going for about a year
and she has known about it the whole time!! Well, thanks for letting
me ramble!! Talk to ya all soon!!

Ewok
