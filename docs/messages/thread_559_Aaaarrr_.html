Aaaarrr! cried the desert moab as they swaggered down the beach near
the takeout. Aaaarrr, again they cried from the top of a large rock
overlooking "x" marking the buried treasure. And again, Aaaarrr! as
they held aloft the pirate gold booty for all to admire. Aaaarrr!
they cried with a triumphant sneer as they swaggered back to their
vessel to drink a toast to the distant visiting pirates from
eastward.

Kat (whose face might get stuck in a piratey sneer)
