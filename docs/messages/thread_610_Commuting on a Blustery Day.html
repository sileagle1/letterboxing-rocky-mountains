Commuting on a Blustery Day

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-09 22:36:51 UTC

Wow.. I'm looking at the window thinking of all of you and WILLING
this nasty wind and rain to go away!! It best be blowin' itself out
tonight so that tomorrow is a perfect letterbox day for y'all.. or
someone is walkin' the plank!

This morning was oooh so lovely and I had the very great pleasure of
a drive in the pirate-mobile out towards Utah to visit Kat's new
Commuting box. What a perfect place for it! I tell you, there is
just nothing like the feeling of slamming the car door shut,
slinging your letterboxing bag over your shoulder and heading down
the trail on another adventure. It just makes me want to sing sea
ditties. Which, surprise, I do. arrrr.

Kat, I've lived here for eleventy billion years and had never heard
that story... loved it!! And loved the picture! And the stamp!
And the journal! And the first finder certificate! Thanks for the
wonderful morning... it was like something special to keep me from
feeling so sad about not being there with y'all tomorrow.

Pirates love letterboxing.

Es, going out to shake her cutlass at the sky and demand that it
clear up by 2 tomorrow
