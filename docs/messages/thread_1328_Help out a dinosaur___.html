Help out a dinosaur...

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2007-05-06 14:48:52 UTC
Hello, Denverites. I recently got a note that the journal for SoC#:
Stegosaurus near Morrison is about full. I plan to replace it next
time I go up to the Denver area, but who knows when that will be? In
the meantime, if anyone happens to be meandering by that box and
would like to replace the log or add to it for me, I'd be most
grateful.

Remember, though, JeLyBean boxes are small, my logbooks are about
2.5" square. I usually just cut them out of copy paper, punch two
holes near the top about 1.5" apart, and bind them together between
two pieces of card stock with brass fasteners. So, you could just
unfasten the fasteners, keep the card stock covers, and replace the
paper inside, if you wanted, or just simply add a few sheets to the
end.

For these acts of kindness, I usually offer a hitchhiker, if I have
one, clues to an unpublished box, if you don't already know about
them, or a free hint for one of my harder boxes like the Cypher
Series or Big Brain.

If you could help, much thanks.

'Box Happy,
JeLyBean

JeLy....Stegasaurus is good to go for quite awhile! I made a new
journal and had intended on mailing you the original, BUT, there are
still about 25 pages left! So, Cappy and I decided to leave the
original and wrote Stegasaurus Journal #2 on the one I brought and
asked boxers to fill the first one before stamping in the second.
When you hear that it's full, we'll go up and get it and mail it to
you then. It's in great condition! We had brought up extra stuff
just in case, but, it didn't need anything! It's kinda crazy because
it has our original stamp in it back from 2004! So, we stamped in
the new journal so you'll have the newest Peach and Cappy stamp. It
seemed weird to see that old stamp of ours. Dorkiness!!! BTW, they
are closing that road to vehicles because they are adding an on ramp
at Alameda and C-470! But, if boxers follow the clues, they'll have
no problems getting it.

What a gorgeous day today! WOOT! Can Spring finally be here???
