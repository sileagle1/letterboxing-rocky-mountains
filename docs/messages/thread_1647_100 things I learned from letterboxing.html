Have you guys seen the "Things I Learned From ..." game that people
play with movies? If not, check out this link:
http://www.jokes.com/funny/whatever/things-i-learned-from-movies

It occurred to me that we could probably do something similar with
letterboxing. The things you have "learned" do not need to be true,
just funny and based on LB experiences. For example:

1. Hitchhikers are always worth slowing down for, and you should
often pick them up.
2. North is straight up.

Feel free to add your own, and Happy New Year!
