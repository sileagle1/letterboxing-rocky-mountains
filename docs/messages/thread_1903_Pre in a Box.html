Since we’ve returned from our Colorado trip, we have continued to try and decipher various mystery boxes from the area. Of course pre’s boxes are a large part of the mix. We have discovered she had a blog, called Pre in a Box, where there appears to be some clues that are embedded. Unfortunately, to get to some of the details, you have to be invited as a reader.
