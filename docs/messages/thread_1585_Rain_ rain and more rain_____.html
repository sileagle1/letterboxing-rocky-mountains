Rain, rain and more rain.....

From: meadesl (BoxTOPS@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-08-18 06:15:26 UTC
Well, after a very wet week with no let up in sight, we managed to
take advantage of a couple of hours of sunshine this afternoon to get
in some boxing in Colorado Springs.

Was not a very successful day...only one in four boxes. A couple of
the boxes were new or fairly new boxes. I suspect that the amount of
rain that has been drenching us has turned the tupperware into
flotillas. Just looking around in some of the locations you can see
that a lot of water had run through those areas.

I'm suggesting that if you've had a bunch of rain lately, as we have,
you may want to do a box check.

FYI - the one box we found was anchored in place on three sides by
large rocks with a SPOR on the 4th side. Lesson learned for me in
placing boxes in the future!
