JelyBean.. I'm so sorry about that box! What a frustrating email
that must have been to get! Is there any chance you can trace it
through a company email address?

My first thought was to wonder if the box had been poorly rehidden
by the last letterboxer. In my own opinion, that's one of the
biggest problems with some of the new boxers who don't want to play
by the rules. And, unfortunately, it only takes one. One of
the 'newbies' left my Durango Rio Grande box uncovered (and it's
right in the middle of town!!) and also left Y-nought's Campus in
the Sky a mess. I'm so lucky that Y checked my box for me and found
it before someone else did. All my clues say very clearly to rehide
the box carefully. I know that pretty much all of us put that in our
clues. After replacing the Big Fish journal and rehiding it in a
different location I went to check on it recently only to find that
the latest finders had let their 6 year old have the thrill of
rehiding it.. and it was laying uncovered again.

And then to have someone just TAKE your box. What in the world are
they going to DO with it?! Unbelievable. This is someone seriously
in need of a life.

Y ... it must have been just shocking to find Three Keys
vandalized. Now that I've found it it just blows me away that they
even stumbled upon it! I'm so glad that you remade it for us all to
enjoy.

I've decided to leave my journals the way they are for now. But I
may pull some of the clues and make them word of mouth. And, at
least at this point, I will probably make all my future boxes much
more difficult to get to and/or find. I'm not going to let a few
jerks ruin the pleasure I get from this hobby! When my daughter and
I go to Disneyland we always make a pact.... no-one no-where, no-
how, no-way will have the power to ruin our trip. We always say it
out loud, shake hands then stick to it. And it always works. We're
able to have a fabulous time despite anything anyone might toss in
our way! HA! So, I'm going to have to adopt that attitude for this
hobby that I'm so addicted to!

Esmerelda, hunkering down
