From: Ed Webb (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-01 16:42:10 UTC
I intend to go searching for Adopted Country yet this fall. I know
the trail well and it is an incredibly beautiful place. It is so
great to have an excuse to go back there.

We found the Spouting Rock letterbox in August, but no one who signed
the book has been able to find the Hanging Lake box for some time. A
shame because the stamps are so well done and perfectly matched. That
is another gorgeous place I never would have seen if it wasn't for
letterboxing.

P.S. Thanks to Silver Eagle for responding about the Andrews Lake
box. Just knowing it exists is enough to keep me searching for it.

From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2003-09-22 15:18:42 UTC
I'm sorry that I'm just responding to this, I got busy and spaced it
out...I wanted to let you know that the Hanging Lake letterbox has a
major incorrect clue! The clues tell you "after the 5th bridge and
the 1 mile marker..." when actually it's after the 7th bridge and 1
mile marker! We looked everywhere by the fifth bridge, but the
bridge wasn't by the 1 mile marker, so, we watched for the 1 mile
marker, which is near the 7th bridge and we found it easily! There
were many other boxers who found it and wrote a note in the journal
about the incorrect clue. Now, we found it last fall and I don't
know if it's still there, but it may be! It's absolutely beautiful
up there and would be worth another look???


Hugs, Dixie
