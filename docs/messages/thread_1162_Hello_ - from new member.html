Hello! - from new member

From: nursingwren (mymacksy@comcast.net) | Date: 2006-08-22 15:24:18 UTC
I recently joined the group and wanted to say hi. We were introduced
to letterboxing by a fantastic article in "Family Fun", a magazine
for
families with kids ages 2-15. It was written by an American family
living in Dartmoor. I'd heard of geocaching before, but was not
attracted to it just because using a GPS is not that interesting to
my
small children. Letterboxing is right up our alley :-)

We were hooked on the first find - the All American Series in
Greeley.

There was an article in the Denver Post yesterday about the
ecological
damage and "littering" (that would be the box) caused by geocaching.
I
was sorry to see it, because from what I can tell, letterboxers and
geocachers are more careful with their surroundings than the average
camper.

Thanks for all the work that so many of you are putting into this
hobby. It makes me believe in the goodness of humanity again :-)

-Hatsoff
