Suggestion For Fixing Counties In Wrong Regions

From: Silver Eagle (sileagle@alltel.net) | Date: 2004-05-30 04:14:37 UTC
I am currently in the process of asking the webmasters to
completely redo the Regions of Texas to a scheme that
most of my Texas peers have agreed upon, and to do this
I was sent a spreadsheet to update and send back to the
webmasters. While updating it I have noticed many errors
that never showed up because no boxes have been placed
in those counties yet, so not only am I creating new Regions,
I am correcting many errors, too. It occurred to me that
this might be a good way for you in Colorado to get your
counties fixed, even if you don't want to change the Regions.
I suggest one of you ask the webmasters for the Colorado
spreadsheet, update it and send it back. No guarantee that
anything will be done, but it is still better than asking
for one county to be moved each time you discover an error.
On another note, I hope one of you will find my Grand Lake
box soon and let me know if it survived the winter. Once
again I find myself wishing I could transport to some of my
letterboxes and enjoy the area all over again any time I want!

Silver Eagle
