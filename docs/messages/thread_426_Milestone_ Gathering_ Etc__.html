Milestone, Gathering, Etc..

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-11-15 01:11:41 UTC

Hi All--
With fresh snow on the mountains and around my feet I stamped
into Animas Coyote Letterbox today. It was my 50th find. I love that
box, really great stamp and fun clues.

Some time ago on this list I said I would trade all my finds if
I could stamp into Bard's Tale. I realized today that that just isn't
true. I have been to some great places, with my kids and alone, to
find these fifty boxes. And I have acquired some great stamp art in
my journal in that time (a little over a year).

I agree with Autumn that it has been an amazing year for this
list and for Letterboxing in the Rockies. And I would LOVE to go to a
gathering. But since I doubt that I could make it, I shouldn't have
any input as far as when and where it should be. But if there is one,
I will try like crazy to make it work. It would be neat to meet some
of the folks that have placed these boxes.

In the meantime, happy trails, and thanks for planting,
y-nought

P.S. I also brought a nice doggie back with me from the mountain
today, this time wearing a garland of flowers, (yawrrroooo), good dog.
