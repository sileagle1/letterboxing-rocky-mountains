From: arogn53628 (arognlie@msn.com) | Date: 2008-02-14 18:07:23 UTC
Hi All,

I haven't posted in awhile, but I've had an interesting LBing issue
arise. I had a guy email me that he was walking his dogs, found my box
lying in plain sight and thought it looked like a fun thing to do. I
emailed back, asking about the condition, etc, and requesting that he
put it back so I could retrieve it. He emailed back and said he was
going to carve a new stamp for it! I emailed back and asked him to
please just put it back...he emailed back and said he would put it back
after he finished carving the stamp! I'm not quite sure what to do with
it if he ever does put it back, since it won't be my stamp and possibly
not even my journal? He hasn't told me if the journal was still in it.
I'm glad the guy is interested and thinks this is fun, but apparently
he doesn't quite get the idea of it. For now, I'm just going to list
the box as missing and see what happens, I guess. It was one of my
Wildflowers boxes and had had LOTS of visitors since I placed it almost
two years ago. Sigh.
