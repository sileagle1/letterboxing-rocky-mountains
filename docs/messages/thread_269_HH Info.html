Hi all.
I found the Pony Express 80th Anniversary HH today at the Meaning of Life LB (which by the way is a beautiful hike with a spectacular view...thanks Eoghan!)
It is my first HH....woo hooo!!!!!
I didnt open it there and figured Id do my HH stamping at home and
when I placed it (possibly in the next few days).
I realized when I got it home that the original placer did not stamp
in the log where it was first placed/and the first finder didnt either (they only stamped their stamp in it.) I looked on Silent Dougs site right now to
verify the HH Etiquette and this is what is says:

Logging a Hitchhiker
Whenever you find a hitchhiker, you need to make sure to log the find in several places.

1.. Record the hitchhiker's stamp in your personal journal.

2.. Record your personal stamp in the hitchhiker's journal.

3.. Record the hitcherhiker's stamp in the host letterbox's journal (the letterbox where you leave the hitcher).

4.. Record the stamp of each host letterbox in the hitchhiker's journal.
Im quilty of #4....darn it.
So its just an fyi for us all here when placing and moving HH'ers.
I am kicking myself for not looking at the logbook when I found it (but it was 730pm and starting to get dark...I was alone...its a good excuse anyway!

Look for it in a LB near you soon!
