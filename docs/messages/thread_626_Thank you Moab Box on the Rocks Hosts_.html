Thank you Moab Box on the Rocks Hosts!

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-04-13 17:25:00 UTC

Finally a chance to wipe the sleep from my eyes and pry my feet out
of my hiking boots (yes, I drove 10 hours with them on) to take a
moment to say "Thank You" to all the great letterboxers who made the
Box on the Rocks Gathering a wonderful adventure! I appreciated all
the time and effort put together for the event and also the
preparation of the new boxes and special carvings contained in each.
Oh how A-Bear, SW Sun Seeker, Lizman and I marveled and smiled each
time we found a new box on the trail. We totally loved the
creativity and thought put into each box! And we really enjoyed
hitting the trail with all the other letterboxers that attended the
event too. What a wonderful way to share a hobby we are all so
passionate about!

As a smile back I have not wasted a moment and stayed up way too late
last night posting the simple boxes I left behind so our new Moab
friends would have a tiny reminder of their fanatic Idaho friends to
the north. You will have to let me know once you hit the trail if I
have messed up any of my clues or the boxes for that matter. Once I
landed at the computer last night I realized that I had clues
scribbled in a million places and none of them made sense imagine
that! Epically the ones little Lizman (Alex) helped with. HA! HA!
I ended up logging on to some existing boxes to assist with
directions to the places the boxes are hidden. Although other boxers
hiking with me stamped into the logbooks of the boxes I placed most
of them still contain the first finder artifact for the "real" first
finder.

Check the site for these new letterboxes:

"Fish Out of Water" (carved and placed by Lizman) Moab, Utah
"Sharp Tooth" Price, Utah
"Winged Spirit" Moab, Utah
"High Noon" Moab, Utah
"Easy Rider Kokopelli" Moab, Utah
"Kokopelli's Hole In One" Moab, Utah

With the great boxes we found during our visit I have already called
a friend who knows how to make logbooks and I may just try my hand at
a little more homemade feel to the ones I place in the future.
Thanks for the creative inspiration!

A special thank you also to A-Bear. When she first mentioned making
the trip I was like "WHAT?" Fortunately common sense was thrown out
the window in a matter of minutes and Lizman and I were on board for
the journey! We had a simply wonderful time and thank you again to
our Moab hosts!

Blackvelvetrav & Lizman
