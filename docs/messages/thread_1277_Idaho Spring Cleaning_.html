It's Spring-cleaning Letterbox style here in Idaho. A-Bear and I
have hit the trails to check on our local boxes for status updates.
I hope to eventually check all of my Idaho boxes this spring and
summer updates will be constant on both Atlasquest.com and
Letterboxing.org.

If you are already boxing in Idaho, planning on boxing in Idaho, or
just dreaming of that special Idaho boxing moment please, please,
please check and double check your clues and print right before
heading out as change will be constant over the next several months.
If you are coming to Boise we would love to have you visit!

I will also post on here the boxes I am checking and their status.
Some I may replace, some I may relocate and some I may retire
depending on what I find and the level of activity the box has seen.
