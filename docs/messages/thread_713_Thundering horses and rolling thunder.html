Thanks, Es. You are so sweet! (probably from eating pie).

I was able to check on some letterboxes this weekend that have only
recently thawed out. All five of the Animas Overlook boxes are fine and
accessible. And Wandering Aengus only needed a little bit of walking
over snow to get to. I didn't make it over to Wings of Eagles, but it
is basically the same elevation as Aengus, so it should be accessible
too.

I noticed that the outer plastic bags that covered many of these
letterboxes were shredded by critters over the winter. Some were so bad
that I removed them completely. I'm not sure they do much good if the
rodents won't leave them alone.

Happy stamping,
