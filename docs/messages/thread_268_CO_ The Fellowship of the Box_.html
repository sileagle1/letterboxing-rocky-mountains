CO: The Fellowship of the Box?

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2004-07-26 01:01:36 UTC
Fellow Coloradoans, I have a proposal that some may find a little
controversial, but this seems to be a low-flame talk list so here
goes:

I would like to gauge people's interest in combining our brainpower
to solve the elusive Bard's Tale box, and then perhaps make an
excursion together to find the box. This would in no way be sharing
the answer; we would work together on the clues from the start, and
share our insights only with each other. Once the group is formed
and we begin work, no one else would be allowed in, thus we couldn't
be accused of giving away any part of the answer.

If you don't think this violates the spirit of letterboxing and
would like to partake in this effort, email me off-list at
jelybean "at" adelphia "dot" net.

OK, fire up your flamethrowers....

JeLyBean
