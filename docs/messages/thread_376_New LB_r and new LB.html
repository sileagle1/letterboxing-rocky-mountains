New LB'r and new LB

From: michelle32855 (michelle32855@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-05 21:56:55 UTC

Hi, All

I'm new to both the hobby and the group, so I thought I'd do a little
intro. Name's Michelle and I live in Ft Collins. Surprise,
surprise...that article over the weekend lit a fire under my fanny ;)

I'd read some about LBing but hadn't pursued it. Then along came the
article and my cousin was really fired up about it. So...off we went
and yesterday found all of wilderwomn's LB's and had a great time
doing it.

We got so fired up in fact that we went and planted one just an hour
or so ago. Here's a chance for an "FF" in your siggy if you don't
have one already. I posted the clue on letterboxing.org and also on
atlasquest, and will include them here as well.

Really looking forward to learning lots and chatting with y'all!

Michelle

Clue (assuming you start in Ft Collins, of course)

Find and follow the street of equine dentition to its western
terminus. You'll need to park the car here. Proceed west on the
footpath. When it forks, stay to the left and cross the footbridge.
Make a left turn. Just past the sign of signs, you'll find a fallen
tree. One piece lies apart. Beneath it, you'll find your goal

Should be pretty easy to find. I'd be really happy if anyone who does
drops me an email and lets me know what you think.

Thanks!
