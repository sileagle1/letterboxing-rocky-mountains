Rainbow Trail near Westcliffe

From: Renee and Dave (haipnsuch@juno.com) | Date: 2005-05-31 19:28:04 UTC-06:00
We were in the Westcliffe area this past weekend and visited the Rainbow
Trail LB by Edna. The plastic shoebox was quite visible from the trail
inviting many non-letterboxers to check it out and sign the
stenographer's pad within. It appears that the original stamp went
missing long ago. It appears that only one party had stamps at all
(manufactured ones), so they left one of their stamps behind. We didn't
bother stamping in the unrelated stamp into our logbooks, nor did we
stamp into the LB logbook. We did cover it a little better. I will send
a note to Edna. Does anyone know if this is an abandoned box? There was
another LB in the area with a bigger hike and we didn't have time to do
it. Edna is also the owner of that one.
~Catamount Carvers
