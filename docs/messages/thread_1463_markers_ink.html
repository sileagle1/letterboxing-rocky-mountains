From: Amy Eckert (aeckert1@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-01-21 20:40:52 UTC-08:00
I use Sharpies for conditioning the stamps I carve
with PZKut (you may have noticed this on the Merry
Christmas stamp!). They dry too quickly to use for
regular stamping, though. In my book, Marvy markers
are top of the line. They are pricey but worth it -
especially if you have one of those Michael's coupons
from the Sunday paper.

I have found that the feather tip ones work really well.

And if you color the stamp and then puff a bit of air on them, like you're cleaning your glasses, right before you set the stamp down to print, it helps moisten up whatever may have dried while you've been coloring.
