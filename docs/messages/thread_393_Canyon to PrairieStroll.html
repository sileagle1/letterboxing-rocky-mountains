CW Sun Seeker came down today and we went on a hunt for this box. First
off - let me tell you, if you are going there from Denver - look at a
MAP! The directions take you very far south...

Some how we managed to skip one of the steps, so it took us forever to
FIND the box! DUH! But, once we realized our mistake, it was found
quickly.

If you have little kids... a stroller works great for the majority of
the trip. But, the last part is not at all stroller friendly. We had
to leave the stroller behind to finish this one.

Nice stamp - hand carved! good day. Had hoped for more boxes but my
terrible ability to follow directions and detours for road work really
stinks. Not to mention that it got really cold and started to rain - ok
sprinkle.

On the way home, we stopped so that Cathy could get Harry's Heaven and
Twenty Mile. Both were found and in good condition. We also stopped at
Sea Goat (one of my boxes) which was still in place and DRY! woohoo!
And hey - there is only ONE stamp in that book!

I am hoping we can get together again next week so that we can do more
boxes in this area or up in Ft Collins. We also talked about a day trip
to Colorado Springs! My husband thinks I have gone crazy!
