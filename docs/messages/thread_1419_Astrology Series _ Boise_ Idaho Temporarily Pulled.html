ones that were still alive I pulled this evening. I discovered they
were not hidden well or were totally exposed. I found two of them just
sitting out in the open. I need to put a bit of though into the re-
carve and re-plant plan I have for this series. I loved the location
but with the high traffic, high river and the boxes being left out in
the open to fend for themselves I need to reconsider the hiding spots
and sizes of the boxes before I put them back out again. 12 boxes are
a lot to maintain when they keep disappearing. However, I do have
every intention of re-planting these boxes.
