From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-01-06 20:40:50 UTC
Hello -

We are just a few weeks away from the big treasure hunt here in
Durango. For those of you that weren't members last winter, this is
a week-long city-wide treasure hunt with five clues a day given out
over the radio. This is a great opportunity for me to win valuable
prizes and for you to feel smug and smart because y-nought could
never win without your help. And it's a fun way to pass a week in
mid-winter.

Last year the clever tips from this group led me to actually grope
the porta-potty that the prize was hidden in...but alas, I didn't
climb up into the flower bed and pull it out of the vent chimney. I
will try to do better this year.

look, this may help to guess what they will be getting at this year.

It all starts on January 30, (I know, a long way off yet, but what
can I say, I'm excited). I'll start posting clues as I hear them on
the radio. If this bothers anybody let me know offlist and I'll find
another way to beg for help.

Thanks in advance,
