Okay, just so I can say that I still know what the topic of this forum

La Paloma and I found ONE letterbox in our travels over the break. It
was Tadpole Country in St. George, Utah. The clue sheet must have been
from Mission Impossible, because at the trailhead it vanished
completely. We tore our packs and the car apart for a half hour until
it re-materialized wedged between the seats. After hiking and stamping
it was too dark for us to try for the others we had printed clues for.
But........that letterbox is in the COOLEST place. Thanks CWSunseeker
for showing us this trail, we will definitely be going back.
