Ski Trip Mini-Meet - March 8th, Empire Colorado

From: front range hiker (frhiker@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-02-29 20:26:16 UTC
We have decided on a date, time and location to meet Milwaukee
letterboxers Wisconsin Hiker and Martini Man who are visiting Summit
county for a ski vacation.

We will be meeting for dinner on Saturday, March 8th at 6:45 pm at
Jenny's Restaurant, 4 W Park Avenue in Empire. Empire is located at I-
70 exit 232, about 30 minutes west of the Morrison exit on I-70.

Jenny's will need a count of our group, so please sign up on the Atlas
Quest event listing if you can join us.

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=666

There will be an event stamp for this occasion. And, Jenny's has
wonderful PIE

Hope you can join us.

Front Range Hiker
