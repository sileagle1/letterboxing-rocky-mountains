Dark Canyon Utah and Piracy in the Skies

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-03-24 22:48:46 UTC
Elros and I checked on this one yesterday. Still sitting there in an
awesome spot......but getting kind of lonely. The last time it was
found was in 2004...... by me. We were in the area anyway. I didn't
hike all that way just to check on the letterbox.

I should probably do something like that, though, to try to improve
on my letterboxing karma. While I hear of you'all having your meet
and greets and big hootenannies, and first plants found, and all that
fun stuff.... I'm having to start over due to a little mix up in the
friendly skies of United.

My first mistake was checking a bag on my flight through Denver to
Salt Lake City. I haven't seen that bag now for two weeks, and the
first week was living in a hotel, borrowing my neice's husband's
clothes, and doing laundry in the sink. But, hey, I can get over
losing a few clothes....what really hurts is that I had my super-
sweet leather bound journal that I bought at the faire in Larkspur. I
haven't really been letterboxing much since then, except for A
CERTAIN HALLOWEEN GATHERING IN SANTA FE!!!! Ouch! There is some
consolation in the fact that Elros and La Paloma have copies of the
stamps I found that day....but the exchanges I made there were long-
awaited and personal. I can't believe I checked a bag with that in
there!......and hope is pretty slim that it will ever be found.

I also lost my signature stamp but it was time for a new one of those
anyway.

So...All you folks that were at the Spooktacular.....I'll be needing
to meet up again for an exchange if I can get my karma running.

y-nought
