Some More Photos and a note on photo sizes...

From: Renee and Dave (haipnsuch@juno.com) | Date: 2005-09-20 22:28:54 UTC-06:00
I added a few more photos for Boxtemberfest ... the Plunder. We are
running out of room for photos on RMLetterboxing. Those of you who have
uploaded photos should delete the full size versions of your images, if
you haven't done so. This would free up space for more photos. What you
do is ... go into your album, click on the photo, click on full size
version and then hit "delete full size version only," and then hit the
confirmation when it comes up. It's simple enough and we might add a few
more photos when there is more room available.
Thanks,
Catamount Carvers
