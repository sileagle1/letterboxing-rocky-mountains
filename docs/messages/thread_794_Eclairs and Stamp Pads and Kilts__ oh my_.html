Eclairs and Stamp Pads and Kilts.. oh my!

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-25 03:46:30 UTC
Okay.. at the risk of posting way too many times in one day, I have
to say a few (or many) words about Renaipalooza.

Yesterday Sasha and I went early to the Rock so we could leave a HH
in the box...we passed a smiling gentleman and his daughter on our
way down but didn't see any letterboxing stuff so didn't stop or ask
questions. Of course, when they came back down with the HH in their
hands we wooted and were soon introducing ourselves to Silver Eagle
and his lovely daughter.. only moments before the Noughts pulled up
and joined the party. A most excellent adventure that had us smiling
the rest of the night.

Early this morning we met the Noughts outside the faire and had just
a superb morning following them around (are they still behind us?!)
and saying arrrr to strangers. At the appointed time Y and I went
to the gate to watch for boxing faces and it wasn't long before we
were all at our table stamping and chatting and sharing and eating
eclairs and taking pictures and.. oh my heck, I had such a great
time! Even though I've known y'all are just coolest of people, in
person you're surely the nicest bunch of folks I've had the honor to
be around in a very long time. And, come on, sitting around a table
in the shade eating eclairs and talking about letterboxing while
kilted men play bagpipes in the background?! I'm still smiling! Of
course, we had our very own kilted Y. Is this a great country or
what?

So.. sorry for all the notes but had to say WOOT and see you all
next time! Huzzah!

Es, who wants to adopt Dixie Peach
