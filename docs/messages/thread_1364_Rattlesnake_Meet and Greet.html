Yes, LONG TONGS and a hiking pole to retrieve that box from its rattling,
fanged and venomous keeper! OH MY, so who was to know that was a nice
summer den! Everyone ... please, please be careful at any of the lower
elevations, which may harbor rattlers.

We've only done three boxes on that Lariat Loop (on Silent Wings' b-day),
but look forward to getting back to it. I think we'll be taking it
slowly and enjoying all the cool sites! Thanks PRE and FRH for all your
work on the Loop!

As far as the Meet and Greet goes, it was good to see some old friends,
make some new friends, and meet our talented Missouri visitors, who had
really cool things to share!!!! Thanks a lot everyone for gathering.
We're glad we drove down for this! We were really waffling about the
drive down and late night, but the promise of an adventure clinched the
deal. No disappointment there!

~SW and the rest of the Catamount Carvers
