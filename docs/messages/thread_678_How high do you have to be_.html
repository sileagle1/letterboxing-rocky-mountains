I know most people think of Rocky Mountain National Park, Aspen, and
Vail when someone says "Colorado High Country". But, only as an
invitation, I'd like to point out that the San Juan Range in
southwest Colorado has 13 peaks above 14,000' and another 21 peaks
above 13,800. That's 34 of the 100 highest peaks on Colorado, far
more than any other Colorado Range.

There is a lot of high country in Colorado.
