Re: See you at The Faire

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-14 20:08:03 UTC
Sweet!

You must have done some fancy shenanigans to be able to come.

The gathering will be at Twelve Noon on Sunday, July 24. We will
meet inside the Colorado Rennaissance Festival gates, just up the
hill at a statue. They won't let us bring pie, but we can buy
eclaires and other goodies inside the castle.

If there is anyone reading this that wants to exchange stamps but
not go into the faire, PLEASE POST HERE when you will be there. I
think it is safe to say that we are all letterbox stamp fanatics,
and it really would be no big deal to get our hands stamped to join
someone outside for a meet and ink.

This is just over a week from now, so I'm starting to do a few happy
dances a day.

y-nought
