From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-18 03:07:40 UTC
Fish Storiesah yes it was a fine summer day at the Fishing for
LetterboxeR's Gathering on Saturday, July 10th in Eagle, Idaho. The
fish were jumping right on to the land and some even thought they
could hide but we knew their plan.

Lot's of great fish food and snacks in our bowls but the real prize
were the carvings if the truth be told. "Three Little Fish" were
hiding in a series by A-Bear. Along came "Frog Fishing" with a
croak and not far down the path "Dancing Fish" was dashing in his
top hat and cane! Down the river we headed after a quick
refreshment stop and my fish friends
discovered "Goldfish", "Charlie" and my tasty ode to ice
cream "Phish Food" flavored that is.

Our final cast of the evening was a cuckoo clue. A great catch in
water or on land as the fish were biting and it landed right into
our hands! Nets now put up for the week. I am sure we will all be
out fishing again soon before our logbooks are full and we are out
of colored ink!

I had fun meeting new local letterboxers. Good times. We succeeded
in getting J-Bear to make a guest appearance which was cool because
then everyone could receive his new stamp. Of course while we
letterboxed, he and our little nephew TRKR (3) passed out on/under
the picnic table. The final FISH kicker from the event, was that I
returned home to start to scrapbook all my letterbox adventures. I
hope I'm not too in over my head. =-)

The really cool part was that we were all out there hours before the
event planting more boxes. If you plant them, they will come.

Let us know when any of you are headed to Idaho and we'll definitely
plan another gathering, of course it doesn't have to be FISHY at
all. =-)

I loved all the cool carvings and all the creativity with the stamps
and food. Incredible FISHY for sure. Thanks everyone. Thanks for
asking about it y-nought. =-) Good times.
