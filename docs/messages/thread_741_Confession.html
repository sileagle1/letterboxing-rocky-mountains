Confession

From: Renee and Dave (haipnsuch@juno.com) | Date: 2005-06-17 07:29:37 UTC-06:00
The Catamount Carvers stalked into Grand Lake yesterday and with a twinge
of guilt snagged all the First Finder's for Aljan's new series. These
boxes are over the top! You've got to go check them out! You'll want to
savour all the details of these boxes, so leave a good day for them (our
opinion). With the other boxes, we think that Grand Lake is definitely
worth a good two days worth of letterboxing.

We have to admit that had you seen these first finder certificates,
there'd be a mad dash for every new Aljan box. People would call in sick
to work and descend upon this mountain hamlet. There would be pushing
and shoving, possibly resulting in yet another creepy Grand Lake incident
giving Aljan more material for another box in this series!

Our bonus was successfully contacting the local Grand Lake master carver
via her fire department connection. We all had some dinner out at the
lake and did some exchanges.

It was a letterboxing day to remember!

~Catamount Carvers
