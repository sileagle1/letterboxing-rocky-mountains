From: Renee and Dave (haipnsuch@juno.com) | Date: 2005-09-22 21:15:54 UTC-06:00
I believe that y-nought is right about the album making. I just followed
along with the Yahoo prompts and it was much simpler than I imagined!

Colored pens ... Boxer Lover had the amazing fatter tipped pens, but they
were the same brand as mine. I previously purchased a cheaper set and
they were worthless. Of course, TDG gets good results with Crayola
markers ... do I have the brand right TDG? The brand Boxer Lover and I
use is Le Plume and they are acid free, if that is important to you. I
bought mine in a set (Le Plume II), and needed to add on a black and
brown marker. I found that Boxer Lover had a nice flesh tone marker, so
I'm getting that next. No more jaundice looks for the people that show
up in stamps! I think that Boxer Lover also had a nice, brighter yellow
than mine. I'm thinking that it might be better to just buy them
individually, so you can get the colors you prefer (brighter is my
preference). Any advice on your end Boxer Lover or TDG?
