Just a reminder that it can be very helpful to cut out non-pertinent parts
of replies to posts. I have started using the daily digest now that Yahoo
finally made it easy to respond to individual posts. If I want to scroll
through the whole digest, I have to go through TONS of repeated information
that I've already read.

If your email program automatically copies replies, can you just cut out
some of the extra stuff and save the list and our email accounts a lot of
extra text?

Thank you!
Cadenza

just to give you an example, I'm including the text of a recent thread. I
don't think we realize how long they get sometimes when you are only reading
the beginning. All of this extra stuff was for a five word reply. Now, I'm
not pointing fingers because we've all done this. I still want to hear the
replies. It would just help to not have all the extra stuff with it.
