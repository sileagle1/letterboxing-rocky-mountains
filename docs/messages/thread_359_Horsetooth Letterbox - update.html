From: Cattail (MLK) (marcikoski@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-09-24 17:48:50 UTC
Hi again - so I heard back from the Larimer Parks guy who pulled the
Horsetooth Letterbox. He seems very interested in writing a
letterboxing policy for the park, and is considering replacing the
box. He will have to write and get approval for the new
letterboxing policy (I don't know the details of it), and I'm unsure
of how long it will take to do this. Meanwhile, my little box is
sitting in an office in Loveland somewhere...

Truth be told, I was a little dismayed about the possibility of an
XPlore article about letterboxing. There aren't too many
letterboxes in Fort Collins (not too long ago there were NONE), and
I would hate to see them disappear! Especially the "For Cattail"
box! I do know, however, that Miles (the reporter who made the
original inquiry to Esmerelda) is one of their best writers, and I'm
sure he would do an excellent job explaining the ins and outs of the
game IF they were explained to him. So Carrie, if you haven't
already done so, I think it would be the best thing if a seasoned
LB'er contacted him for an interview. I would be happy to
participate, but I am now in San Diego :)

Anyway, I'll keep you guys posted on the Horsetooth letterbox, and
the clues on the LBNA website have been modified to show its current
status.

Cheers!
cattail :)
