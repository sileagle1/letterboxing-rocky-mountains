Rocky Mountain Trip

From: babybearlb2002 (babybearlb2002@yahoo.com) | Date: 2003-09-09 21:21:46 UTC
My wife (Love Bunny) and I (Baby Bear) just got back from a 3 day
hiking and letterboxing tour in the upper Denver area. We
found "Grand Lake", "Shining Star", and "Wheel of the Year - Yule"
boxes, and all are alive and well. I also place 3 microboxes, and
hope to have the clues posted this week. Those boxes are:
"The Rockies" - Estes Park at Mary's Lake
"Rock Crawler" - Morrison at Red Rocks Park
"Bear in Paradise" - Idaho Springs at Echo Lake (Near Mt Evans Rd).

Had some rain, but still enjoyed being in the mountains.

PS - Grand Lake Lodge was closing that day, so will not be able to
get to parking area. You may be able to park at gate and walk in,
or hike from town up to the box. Next one there will get an extra
bonus!!!
