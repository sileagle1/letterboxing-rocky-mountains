From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2005-07-16 18:59:07 UTC
Given the amount of slavering and hyperbole over pie on this talk
list, some of you may find this story hard to believe, but I assure
you it is absolutely true.

I was taking a tiny walk this morning when I discovered a lost dog.
Springer spaniel, I think. Anyway, I decided to take the dog along
with me to see if I could find it a home. I continued my walk a
little further up the road, when, lo and behold, right there in the
woods, no lie, I discovered someone had left... (drum roll please)...
a pie! Honest! It was delicious, too, a sort of chocolately,
marmallowey, graham crackery thing. I even gave the dog some. And
even better, a little further up the trail someone had left me an icy
cold tasty soda to wash it down with! Unbelievable! What a day!
Sometimes life is good.
