Language of Silver - Silver Spoon - Boise Update

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-05-26 14:44:17 UTC
The series Language of Silver in Boise has just been updated. I thought the third box was missing and could not even locate it myself. If anyone happens to head out again here is a note from Capperpillar from yesterday - Silver Spoon -- it's still there! While we were out there today, we decided to check on it and we found it. Only one other person has found it since we did last June. There's a small trail that runs along the fence, and the box is at the base of the 5th or 6th post (depending on how you count them), which is about 20 steps down that small trail. There were several posts with rocks piled at their base, so it could seem like it was missing if you look by the wrong post.

It is totally embarrassing that I could not locate my own box. Next time I will bring my clues along. HA! HA!
