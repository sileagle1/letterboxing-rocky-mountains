Sorry, no pie here. Maybe I will add a picture of one to honor the RM list :)

A blog is like a website with a slightly different format. It works
sort of like an online journal where I can go in and make regular
posts about what is happening or areas of interest to me. So I
thought, "what better to be of interest to me than letterboxing?" If
you've gone to Mark and Sue Pepe's site, it is actually a blog, not
just a website. If you haven't gone, you need to. It is a great
site. They have also started to collaborate with Atlas Quest to show
links to each other's sites. I'm also writing Ryan to see if I might
be able to add a LB search box to my site like they have. Wouldn't
that be cool?

Here is the Pepe's site
http://markandsuepepe.blogspot.com/
this is mine (it doesn't have nearly as much info in it yet)
http://letterboxing.blogspot.com/

Does that help?
Cadenza
