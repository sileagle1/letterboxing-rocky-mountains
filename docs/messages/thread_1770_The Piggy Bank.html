C'mon Gang!

We all spend a few bucks every time we plant or find a single letterbox. Why not set aside a few of those dollars from your letterboxing budget for those horribly unfortunate people living in Haiti? Help me out and let's see how generous we can be helping those who can't help themselves.

Mercy Corps is a Pacific Northwest charity with high marks, sending 89% of their money directly to aid programs. I've primed the pump but could use your assistance:
http://www.mercycorps.org/fundraising/funhog
