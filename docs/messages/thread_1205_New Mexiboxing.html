Hello-

La Paloma and I just flew back from Taos and boy, are my brazos
cansado!

We didn't get anywhere close to all the letterboxes available. But
we did find some great ones and had some incredible hikes. We hit
Red River in the off season and it seemed like we had that whole
valley almost to ourselves. Plus, it had been a while since we had
found a letterbox, so the enchantment was double. It also was funny
because it seemed like we were following Rubberpeace and Rubber
horno across the countryside.

A big thanks to all the folks who have placed in that area, and a
recommendation to anyone who is considering a trip there.

Viva los rellenos!
