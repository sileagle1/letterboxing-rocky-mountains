BoxerLover-

You can't really go wrong with any of the boxes in the Moab area. But
I kinda like the Ticaboo and Lulu's day out series. It's a short walk
but you feel really secluded. And I like Searching... for the same
reason. And Dead Horse Point is a fun letterbox with just amazing
views.

I'm afraid that at the end of March I'll be eating dehydrated food
and only dreaming of pie. My kids and I will be backpacking near
Canyonlands in an obscure place called Hatch Wash between Moab and
Monticello. But, please, have a slice for me.

y-nought
