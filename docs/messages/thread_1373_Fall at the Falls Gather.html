Fall at the Falls Gather

From: Judy (jowagnon@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-07-30 02:51:32 UTC

Many thanks to Dragonrider for hosting this most excellent gathering in
Twin Falls ID yesterday! We had a blast! Great friends (old and new),
great food, great boxes! Cooties swarming in spite of the Cootie
Inoculation stickers. Lots of really good ideas I'm going to be
copying. I especially enjoyed the Creatures of the Canyon series and am
proud to say I have the complete set. (Had to do more than one
dog-and-pony show to distract the lookers-on, but we got that Trout!)

DR, thanks for procuring the COVERED AREA, even it you had to show up at
6:00 a.m. to get it. 8~D It was a toasty day, even in the shade!

There was an unscheduled event some of us got to see later after dinner
in town: a base jumper off that MAGNIFICENT bridge being filmed for a
National Geographic special. The cameraman was MOST patient with
A-Bear's questions while we waited for the sun and clouds to get just
right! He did pack up rather quickly though.

I arrived home just before midnight - only 2 hours late - tired, but
happy in the glow of the day and finally rehydrated. So grand to see
all you super 'boxers! Check out the photos on the AQ event page. I'll
be adding mine directly.

Boxer Lover(47)
