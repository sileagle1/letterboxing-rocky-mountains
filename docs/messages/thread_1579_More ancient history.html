I found some tidbits researching the early lb-usa list. For those of
you that are interested:

Message #834 contains some text from a person named Michele Spalding
who lived in Boulder. Her website still has a letterboxing page from
1999. She was supposedly going to plant one in 1999 but the link to her
clue never worked.

Messages #3828 and #3834 are Jay Drew and Mapsurfer talking about
Michele Spalding as well as other early letterboxers.

And message #4726 is from Brooks/Devere announcing the letterboxes he
placed in Arizona, Texas and Colorado the summer of 2000.

This might be boring to some people, but I just love reading those old
messages from when it was all brand new.
