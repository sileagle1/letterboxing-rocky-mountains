From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-11-21 04:37:56 UTC
Randy was in Colorado around the 4th of July for an orienteering
competition. I met him down in Colorado Springs and we did a few
boxes together down there.

While we were walking (since none of the ones we did could really be
called hiking) - I did pick his brain a bit. All I got out of him on
the Bard's Tale was that he thought his was the highest in
elevation. I told him there were several boxes that were really
high - and all he would confirm was that his box was "above 11k feet"

That said, he's really cryptic - so who knows. He also told me he
thought it would take someone about a year to DECIPHER the clues, and
over a day to hike for them.

*sigh* I think we're all cursing the Mapsurfer at this point. :)
Autumn
