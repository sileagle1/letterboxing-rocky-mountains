Aengus Again Ambling

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-24 03:38:40 UTC
Wow! I never thought of driving up into the mountains right after work,
but it was a great way to wind down from my day.

It was just after a light rain and the silvery peaks were shining above
the low scudding and misty clouds. After a short round of fisticuffs
with a certain marmot that was standing right next to Aengus' original
hiding place and mocking me, I found an even better hiding place than
the first. Actually the stamp is better now and so is the container and
the logbook.

I guess it's possible that the marmot was only trying to help me
realize more of this boxes potential. But I'll still never forgive him
for eating the visitor's stamps in that logbook.

After it was hidden I found a few fossils among the rocks strewn about.
And then once again thanked myself for getting out and doing a little
letterboxing.

Cheers,
