From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2005-02-08 21:14:56 UTC

What an exciting morning we had spotting ancient doorways and
columns in the distance. The Arboc is a personal favorite. It was a
hilarious morning of slip sliding through patches of snow, ice, and
mud to find the MOST awesome box, Alien Gothic. This is my new most-
favoritest box and stamp, and tooooo much fun. And to top it off, we
couldn't stop laughing as an airplane flew over, echoing through the
towers, as we were stamping in. I know "they" were watching us.

Kat
