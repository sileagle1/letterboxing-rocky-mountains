Swedish Fish

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2005-01-07 00:01:52 UTC

Swedish fish are just one of those things that just happened. While
letterboxing with my sister-in-law in MA last summer, we were
starving and had a choice, more boxes or stop for lunch. Of course,
the boxes won out and we stopped quickly at a little market that was
dark and dirty but had lots of candy. We had swedish fish (chewy,
red, strawberry-ish flavored, easily carried up remote canyons and
probably has a shelf-life of 200 years) and ever since then, we keep
a baggie of fish among the other tools in our pack! So, it's more of
a comfort-happy memory food than anything else and is used often to
bribe 4 year-old hikers that just don't want to hike just one more
mile! LOL

I didn't get those boxes planted yet, Es, so if you come to town,
I'll just have to provide some pie with merangue, how about lemon?

Kat
