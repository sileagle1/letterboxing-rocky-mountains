H-A's letterbox updates

From: gonefromnj (gonefromnj@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-03-06 21:46:02 UTC

First, had to slightly relocate "Bite My Shiny..." as the box had a
tendency to wander (we had trouble finding it and we knew where it
was supposed to be). It is still nearby, but hopefully everybody is
happier with this spot. Hope no one printed out the clue and headed
out there only to have it moved in the meantime. If so, our
apologies; hope you had a good walk, at least. Tie-Dye Gal and
Soccer Chick were the last ones to find it before we moved it.

Second, the new Lemony Letterbox is almost ready and we are hoping
to replant it next weekend (weather permitting). It will be in the
same vicinity, but hopefully we'll do a better job of finding a safe
location. If you stamped in before, you may want to stamp into the
new journal, but the stamp you will be putting in your own will be
the same as it was.

Thanks,
The H-A's
