WD'A Abandoned Boxes in Idaho

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-12-19 20:25:25 UTC
Currently the boxes that were listed as "abandoned" under WD'A have
been placed under me on LBNA. WD'A does great carvings and is very
creative with her boxes. She dropped out of letterboxing a few years
ago. Capperpillar and I have pulled some of the abandoned boxes
(that were still alive) and are hoping that WD'A will come to the
Gathering in January (we have been in contact with her daughter to
see if we can spark the fire again) so we can help her update, re-
carve and re-plant her boxes. Some of the boxes we were able to
confirm were indeed MIA and those I have updated with an MIA next to
the box name on LBNA. On Atlas Quest I have been updating the web-
master with status changes as we have confirmed the status of some of
her boxes although the boxes are still listed under her trail name on
Atlas Quest.

Until we meet up with WD'A I cannot guarantee that any of her boxes
are currently alive and/or what condition they are in. However, if
you do manage to check on any of them yourself an update would be
great.

These are the boxes that were abandoned:

Boy-O-Boy (Caldwell, Idaho)
Treasure Shop Hop (Meridian, Idaho)
Hit the Trail Series (Hidden Springs, Idaho)
Friends (Garden City, Idaho)
Huntmaster Series (Eagle, Idaho)
Caribou (Eagle, Idaho)
Apple Blossom (Boise, Idaho)
Sweet Violet (Boise, Idaho)
Amaryllis All Winter (Boise, Idaho)
Tulip (Boise, Idaho)
Princess Thumper (Boise, Idaho)
Mother's Day (Boise, Idaho)
Santa Mystery (This one is definitely MIA)

One way or another we will try to get these great boxes back in
action for everyone to enjoy this spring. More details to come in
2009.
