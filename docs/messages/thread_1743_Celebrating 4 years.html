Celebrating 4 years

From: frontrangehkr (frhiker@comcast.net) | Date: 2009-09-06 02:08:18 UTC
It has been four years since I discovered this addiction (oops, hobby)and found my first box. I've met some wonderful people and was introduced to places in my own backyard that I never knew existed.

I found my 1500th box, Clinton Lake, today in Kansas. It was a little damp, but I took the time to read through the logbook. To my surprise, Peach and Cappy were first finders on the box on 4/14/04. After all these years, Colorado boxers still are beating me to letterboxes ;-)

My first box was "King of All He Surveys" by Treasure Keepers on the Dakota Ridge trail in Golden. Just wondering - what was everyone's first find?

FRH
