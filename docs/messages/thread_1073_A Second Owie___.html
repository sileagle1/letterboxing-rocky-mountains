A Second Owie...

From: gonefromnj (gonefromnj@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-15 15:27:33 UTC
As if it wasn't bad enough that someone made off with Super Uno Guy
and just left the empty box lying around, we got a report that
Dilbox was not where it was supposed to be and, when we went to
verify, found that it was indeed missing. For those keeping score
at home, that's two missing boxes within a month and two missing
Dilboxes within a year.

Checked around the area for any sign of Dilbox, but there was
nothing. The hiding place barely appeared to be disturbed at all,
so it seems strange that someone accidentally stumbled across it. I
had also included a note taped inside the lid appealing to anyone
who accidentally found it to please put it back and not spoil the
fun for others.

We will, of course, re-carve and replace in a better spot nearby
since we really like that area, but we'll probaby wait until summer
so we don't end up hiding it in the midst of dense foliage that is
only accessible in winter.

Finally, does anyone have text that they use in or on their boxes to
appeal to non-LBers to put the box back and not take it with?

Okay, Aljan, work your voodoo...
