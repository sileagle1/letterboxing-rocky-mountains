In an attempt to bring more of you up this way, I am seriously considering a huge venture. I am seriously considering placing a copy of each of the Stanley Manor Murder Mystery boxes in locations around Northern Colorado. I have also thought about starting a whole new mystery, but then again how do you out-do the original? This would be an additional 12 boxes in Northern Colorado.....with a little mystery involved as well. Possibly even a gathering to announce it's release? Let me know your opinions and thoughts. If you are not familiar with the Stanley Manor Murder Mystery, it is a group of clues that together solve the mystery. I released it to the postal letterboxing community nearly a year ago and had brought 2 clues to the Moab gathering, which many of you attended.

I hope you have all had a great holiday season!
