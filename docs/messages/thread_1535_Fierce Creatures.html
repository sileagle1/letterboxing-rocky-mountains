Fierce Creatures

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-06-02 17:22:03 UTC
Hello all-

FRH and preboxed came down to SW Colorado for some serious letterbox
hunting. The day I spent with them was all fun and games except for
when FRH almost got struck by a diamondback. (Yikes!!) The picture is
in a new folder called Fierce Creatures and I invite anyone to put
pictures of other wild encounters in that folder. (I know there are a
few wildlife pictures already in those folders.)

I have to admit that I have been a little cavalier about watching out
for wild animals while letterboxing. I spend a LOT of time poking
around in both the mountains and the desert and have never had anything
bad happen. But in this case it was a REALLY close call. He was
camoflaged so well that you couldn't see him until you were right on
top of him. If it weren't for his warning rattle it could have turned
out a LOT worse on that day.

Thankfully, that encounter only served to add a little adrenaline to
what became a dawn-to-dusk FUN FEST.

y-nought
