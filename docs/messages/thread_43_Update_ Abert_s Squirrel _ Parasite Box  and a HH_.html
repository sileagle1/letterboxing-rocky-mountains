From: tlrewok (teri@adminpowerinc.com) | Date: 2003-08-07 16:02:53 UTC
Hi guys!! Trinity Jah, Ariana and I met up with Fiona and Autumn
yesterday, Aug. 6, to find the above boxes. A beautifull spot!! We
were not the first finders as Dixie and her Teammate made it up there
last weekend, but she did leave us a HH. It was Pandy's Bear's
Angel. We all stamped it and let Fiona take it with her. This spot
in Bailey is goregous!! I will update the placers, and yes, mention
the correction on FR550. We thoguht that maybe the info that was
posted here was from someone who was just looking at a map, but low
and behold it was Miss Dixie who had just gotten this box!! Thanks
for the info. I know I said I thought the same, but when I got there
I went off of the clues. We figured it out pretty quickly and got on
the right track.

Thanks to the placers and thanks to Dixie for leaving us a record of
some of the more famous LBers!! (I never thought that I would see
some of those stamps!!)

Ewok & Trinity Jah (on behalf of Fiona & Autumn also)
