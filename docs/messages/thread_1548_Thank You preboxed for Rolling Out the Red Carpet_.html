This box is planted as a thank you for preboxed who hosting a Mini

you for rolling out the red carpet for us!!!
Prebox will receive the clues at the Meet & Greet. The clues will be
posted for everyone Saturday evening.
Location: Clear Creek Path, Golden, Colorado

Directions: Park at the Golden Library, 1019 Tenth St. (Cheyenne
Street and 10th Street.)

Additional information if you love to get wet: Cool off at Clear
Creek Whitewater Park located in Golden, Colorado at 10th & Maple.
The whitewater park is a popular place to get wet riverboarding,
kayaking, tubing, or wading in Summer. Peak flows occur early in
Summer (late May - early June) with 1,000+ cfs and Class II+ rapids.
July and August are better months to tube and wade as the flows are
much lower (200+ cfs) and water temps higher (60 degrees if you're
lucky). Clear Creek whitewater park extends from the start of the
foot path (approx 1/2 mile west of the campground) to Vanouver park
on Ford Street north of Coors - 1 1/2 miles in total length. The most
popular stretches are from 10th & Maple (Lions Park) to the Library
Hole.The park was developed by reinforcing the sides of Clear Creek
with boulders and cement to create whitewater features along Clear
Creek from Maple Street to Washington street south of 10th Street. It
is open to the public (enter at your own risk - PFD and helmet are
recommended) with the most parking near the Golden Rec Center and
Lions Park at 10th& Maple. Weekends are crowded in late Summer and a
weekly rodeo event is held on Wednesday evenings during the month of
June.

Clues: Park at the library and head to the Clear Creek Path that
follows the river and is located behind the Library. Follow the path
to your left. Immediately you will come upon a small rose garden on
your left that is situated in front of the D'Deli eatery at D'Creek.
Continue on the path until you come to an information sign
titled "Down by the Old Mill Stream" also on your left. The Mythical
Meow you are seeking is behind the giant round stone under a small
log and rocks. Please re-hide carefully as this area has heavy foot
traffic and the patio seating to the eatery is very close by.
