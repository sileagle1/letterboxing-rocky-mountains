Lion's Den is gone

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-08-28 16:25:32 UTC
I'm glad to see that those missing boxes are being replaced. I haven't
had a box go missing for a while, until now. I'll be working on
replacing the Lion's Den this Fall. The clues to this letterbox have
been pulled from another local letterbox for the time being. There are
no published clues.

To lose this one is a little bit tough for me. It was first found in
June of 2004 by the young SplendidBuccaneers, Nightcrawler and
Pixiejunction. One of the very first local boxes ever found, it
reminds me of how excited I was when I realized that people would
actually visit these things.

I'll poke around for another hiding spot, but the new lion will
probably live in the old den.

Happy Trails,

y-nought
