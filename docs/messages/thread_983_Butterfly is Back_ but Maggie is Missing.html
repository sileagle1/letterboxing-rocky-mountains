Butterfly is Back, but Maggie is Missing

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2006-01-14 22:11:53 UTC
Hi folks, today I replaced SoC#2: Hairstreak Butterfly in Ute Valley
Park, fairly near its original location. While there, I also checked
on Maggie and found the box relocated from its original place, the
stamp and log book missing and the box full of, you guessed
it, ...stuff...

So, careless geocachers destroy another letterbox.

While looking on the geocaching web site in hopes of finding the
person who took the stamp so I could try to get it back, I discovered
there is another geocache right near where I re-hid the Butterfly. So
I don't know how long it will last before those chuckleheads ruin that
one too. If you find any JeLyBean boxes with geocaching ...stuff...
in them, please do me a favor and remove it.

I have marked Maggie as missing and have no plans to replace it.

'Box Happy,
JeLyBean

Yep, right on the lid. On the web page for the geocache,
other 'cachers had even noted it in the log, they said things
like "found another box first, but it said on it, 'This is not a
geocache' so I kept looking," or "found the other box first but it
didn't seem right so I kept looking." But I suppose if you let your
kids raid the box, they might not read it, or be able to read it.
That's what I'm guessing happened.

Aaaanyhoo, I guess the good news is, I checked on another box,
Pulpit Rock, for which I'd gotten a missing report, and that was
thankfully still there. Plus the Butterfly has been replaced so
people can still find a letterbox in this wonderful little park. At
least for now, anyway.
