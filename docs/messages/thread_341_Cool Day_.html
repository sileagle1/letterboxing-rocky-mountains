Cool Day!

From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2004-09-12 22:00:40 UTC
We just had a fantabulous day! Samurai and I went out to get the
Rattlesnake (looks good by the way - thanks Debbie!)
and then on a whim, we decided to go and do some maintenance on the
Alchemist's Lizard, as we haven't been there since we put the box
out. Lo and behold, as we are sitting there cleaning up the box a
little, a brand new boxer - Katie - and her mother show up hunting
our lizard! This was their 3rd box - Beltane and Ostara being their
first couple. They read about the hobby in Time Magazine very
recently and had decided to check it out. They were thrilled to sit
and talk with us for a bit about the in's and out's of our little
hobby. We exchanged stamps, and headed on our respective ways.

We just found it extremely cool to have been in the same spot, at the
same time. We invited them to come check out the RM Letterboxing
group, so hopefully we will see them here soon.

~RavenWolf~
