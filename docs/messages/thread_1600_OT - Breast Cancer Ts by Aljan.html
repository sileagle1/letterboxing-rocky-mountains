From: Aljan (aljansundance@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-09-17 02:57:49 UTC
Hey gang! I hope that no one minds a little off topicness for a great
cause!

My design has been chosen by the oh so cool company, 911 Cares, for
their 2008/09 breast cancer supprt t-shirts! This company supports 911
dispatchers who are sick or injured, among other really cool stuff.

I have put an image in the files section under 'breast cancer Ts by
Aljan'. The design with the headset and ribbon (someone else's, but
still cool) will be on the front and the butterfly (MINE!!) will be
nice and big on the back. The shirts should be available by the end of
the week on 911 Cares website - www.911cares.com

I would be psyched to see these shirts fly off of 911 Cares' shelves.

Blessed be to all.

Aljan
