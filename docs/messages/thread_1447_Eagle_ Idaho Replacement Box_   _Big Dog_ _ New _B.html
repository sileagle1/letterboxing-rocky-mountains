Once you find the location of your starting point park and follow the
red path to a large yellow circle. Continue on the red path to a
smaller yellow circle. Leave the circle and the red path to the south
heading towards a flowing body of water. Go through the fence posts
and in front of you there is a rusty bridge. Located below "inside"
the "ribs" of the bridge on the left you will find the black
magnetic "Big Dog Letterbox" hiding. Make sure to reach carefully
inside for the box and watch out for screws.

Continue over the bridge and once you reach the Greenbelt take the
dirt path to your left. Note the round green wooden posts on your
left. Start counting from the first round green wooden post until you
reach the 45th post. Stop. Turn and face the river. Standing at the
edge of the path take 4 careful steps down the hill towards the water
or two cautious paces. "Big Dog Barks" is hidden under the rocks at
your feet behind a triangle shaped rock that is hidden under a larger
rock that acts as a ledge. The box is directly across from the 45th
post so align yourself from there. The box is not as far down as the
tree that is to your right if you are now facing up hill as you
search the rocks with your back to the river. Please watch your
footing as some of these rocks are loose.

Should you note the box missing or its contents in bad repair please
