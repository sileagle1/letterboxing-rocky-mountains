Coming to a box near you........

From: Ramdelt (scott_katytalbot@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-02-20 22:01:21 UTC
Some of you may remember the Stanley Manor Murder Mystery series that
I took "postal" a few years ago. Since I am no longer very active
with the postal community, I have decided to plant this exciting
series for my Rocky Mountain friends. You may recall that there are
12 different boxes in this series with a final sleuth prize stamp. I
have been plotting and planning how this will work for quite some time
and have come up with a few ideas thus far. Some boxes will be tough
(somewhat Bardish), others will be direct. I am hoping to get all
boxes placed this spring/summer with clues included in each box to the
final sleuth prize. Obviously the larger number of boxes will be
planted in my general area but I plan to have them in multiple
counties (possibly the eastern slope and southern Colorado with some
assistance). I am open to any suggestions that anyone may have and
hope that this will be as much fun as it was in the postal community.
Please let me know if you have any suggestions or comments. Thank you!

Ramdelt
