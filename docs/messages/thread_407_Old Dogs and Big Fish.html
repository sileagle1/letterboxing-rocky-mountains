Old Dogs and Big Fish

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-27 19:47:11 UTC

The drizzling rain held off long enough today for me to head down to
the river to check on Big Fish. I pulled him out of the now
leafless Nightshade bush and found him a perfect home further down
the trail. While there I found the sweetest little old dog, Sadie
Dog HH, waiting patiently for a ride. I was going to leave Sadie
there for the next person, but people come along so seldom down
there and she looked at me with such sweet, wistful eyes that I
relented and brought her home. She's curled up in front of the fire
sleeping and dreaming of a road trip next week where I'll find her a
new home and she can wait for her next family to take her in for a
spell.

It was a lovely day.

Esmerelda
