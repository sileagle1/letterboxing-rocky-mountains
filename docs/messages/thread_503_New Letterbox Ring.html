Hey I know that there is a site for Letter Box rings and I have already
posted there. I was just thinking that maybe some of you snowbound may
like a letterbox in the mail. My husband and I are starting this ring.
We have a few more spots available and the more the merrier. If you
have never participated in a postal letter box now may be the time.
We've done a few and have found it to be almost as good as a birthday
party. Happy Boxing!

PS Esmeralda, I am sure that there just has to be a pirate comic book
out there somewhere. I'd love to see one of your log books in this
ring. :-)

Look in the mailbox

Its a bird

Its a plane,

No its a New Comic Book PLB Ring

The Flying Javalinas have decided to start a new PLB Ring. We love
comic books and feel that there must be a few others on the list who do
too. This is not limited to comicbook geeks. We would love to share
some fun books with newbies too.

Easy Rules:
#1 Each person would create a box with a logbook and stamp with a comic
book theme.

#2 Each box would contain a copy of your favorite comicbook. Please
dont send an original Action Comics #1 :-) These will be read and
passed on for all to share and then returned to you. Please keep them
PG rated.

#3 The box must not cost more than $3.85 + delivery confermation to
send. The USPS flat rate envelopes are a perfect size.

#4 You may include special items or information as long as you keep it
within mailing costs. You can allow others to stamp in them.

#6 You must keep the turn around time to under 3 days. Really, how
long does it take to read a comic book?

#7 To be eligible you must be one of the first 15 to send us
notification of what comic book you will be mailing out. (We dont
want everybody to send the same Spiderman book.)

#8 The first round of boxes will be sent out by February 9th.

This will be more fun than the San Diego Comic Book Convention!
