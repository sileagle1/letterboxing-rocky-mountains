Good morning! Cadenza is right: if you search from the main
yahoogroups homepage our list shouldn't show up because of that
setting. It's easy to change it:

-go to the home page at
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/RMLetterboxing

-click "management" in the left column, then
-click "Description & Appearance" under "Group Settings" on the right
-click "edit" under the "Directory" setting, and you can choose what
category to put us under. Most of the letterboxing lists are in
Outdoors>Letterboxing.

I think the new file Randy put up on the big list will help people
find us as well, and there's no reason we couldn't drop a little
reminder to the list every now and then.

Happy day, it's "Friday" for us and we're looking forward to a long
weekend, Jay in CT
