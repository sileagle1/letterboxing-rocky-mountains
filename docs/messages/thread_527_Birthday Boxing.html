Birthday Boxing

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2005-02-10 03:27:40 UTC

So, last Thursday was my birthday, and I just have to tell you what
kind of friends I have. They are the best. Kennamtn carved four
stamps of different kinds of places and things that I love in life
and cut them out in puzzle pieces that fit together. With each stamp
she wrote one of her awesome poems that had clues to the next stamp.
So it started at work with one that I had to figure out to find the
next and so forth, until the last one told me to go to another
friend's house, where everyone planned a surprise party for me. She
hid them all around Moab and I had to drive up to a half hour to
find one up a canyon north of town. Wow, I was just blown away by
the time that was spent planning everything! So much fun!!!

Kat (not feeling any older or wiser yet)
