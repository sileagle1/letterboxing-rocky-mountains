Thank you, thank you, thank you....

From: meadesl (BoxTOPS@comcast.net) | Date: 2008-09-02 05:21:03 UTC
Wow, we are just blown away after the last frantic four days of
boxing. You guys are absolutely incredible...the stamps are
amazing, the logs are very creative and we are inspired to put more
into our boxes for others. We have been to the most fantastic
places in Colorado...this activity has taken our family to places we
would never have encountered had it not been for your creativity and
desire to lure others. We continually shake our heads in amazement
of how enriched and blessed we are as a family. Thank you all for
taking the time to create the stamps, for putting together the logs
and boxes and for finding such interesting locations to bring us
to. We are planning on return trips to vacation in several
locations that we've seen... I'm beginning to think that this is
not really a hobby or activity, it's a way of life. A commitment to
art and entertainment for someone you may never meet...and
opportunity to share your talent for an unheard accolade. The time
that you all put into even finding these locations and then planting
and maintenance is inspiring.

FYI - we had several high notes this weekend...our 200th box, solved
a Jely mystery, completed Lariat Loop and started on Talking Iron
(phew - I'm whomped).

Couple of questions: 1) Why do pirates have such a pension for high
places on land? 2) Shurtugal wants to know if sitting on a cactus
while opening a box is enough to earn him a letterboxing purple
heart?

I need ADVIL...
BoxTOPS, who's exhausted and needs a vacation from her vacation....

P.S. We have a new family trailname: sporaddicts (Boxtops, Celtic
Sojourner, Dolphin Rider and Shurtugal)
