Pirates and Pies

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-15 23:21:17 UTC
Sooo... Dread Y and I finally settled on a date for a letterboxin'
parlay, consumption of fine pies and quality time with ye old
renaissance folks for any of ye what be interested!

Batten down the hatches and hide yer valuables on the weekend of
July 23 and 24 for pirates and kilted rascals will be about!

Aside from the dates of that weekend, we haven't set a schedule in
stone yet. If everyone wants to go to the faire, then there you
go. If some of you want to come but not enter the faire (be ye
mad?!), there is a park nearby where we just might have a picnic or
some such civilized thing. I know the Nought's have some letterboxes
on their wanted list and I sense much stamping and fun.

I know the dates aren't going to work for everyone and I apologize
for that. That's just the only time we could get all the moons and
stars and work schedules aligned for travel from our corners of the
state.

If you're interested in coming, shout it out and the plans will
begin to form!

Esmerelda, dusting off her best ruffled blouse, foofing the feathers
on her hat and putting a fine edge on her cutlass
