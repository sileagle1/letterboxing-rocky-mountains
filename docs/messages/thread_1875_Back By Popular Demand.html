The consensus I received from letterboxing friends far and wide was "please replant". To honor the enthusiasm of all my friends I replanted 4 boxes today in Boise (yes, I braved the 90- degree heat as a true labor of love). I also checked on two boxes this weekend while I was out and about. Clues for these 6 boxes are on AQ with an F-5 restriction.

Re-Plant:

Visions of Angels (2 boxes) Boise, Idaho

Original stamps re-planted. Boxes moved from Horseshoe Bend to Boise. First finders re-plant gifts included in both boxes. Replant gifts do not have anything to do with the actual boxes. Just a little something from me to make the find extra fun.

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=119284

Golf Course Fishing-Boise/Eagle, Idaho

I have combined two separate plants into one series of two boxes. This series contains the original carvings of both "Easy Catch" and "No Fishing." Both boxes are now located on the trail that "Easy Catch" lived on alone before it was vandalized. One re-plant gift has nothing to do with the box it is in and the other has everything to do with the box.

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=115340

It's ultra-important to watch for updates on the AQ Boards as I am moving boxes around, changing box names etc. So you don't end up re-visiting old finds (unless you intend to). In the case of "No Fishing" I am leaving the original posting as retired so I do not mess up find counts for anyone who originally found the box.
