From: HER (fauxsum@hotmail.com) | Date: 2008-06-02 16:51:41 UTC
I'd love to meet up with you for exchanges in Golden when you're in
the Denver area!

Does Saturday, June 21st work - maybe in the afternoon? How about
you local-area 'boxers (aka "the usual suspects"? Does that date
work? If so, I'm happy to take it from there to organize.

What we've done in the past for summer meet ups is to gather at the
park where the Coors box is and take it from there, weather
permitting, do exchanges at a table there, or head out for a cafe or
restaurant if it's not so nice. Then perhaps do some boxing with all
or part of the group.

As for fairly easy boxes in the Golden/Morrison area:

With limited time, you probably won't be able to do them all, but you
could try some of the Lariat Loop series that FRH (Front Range Hiker)
and I put together - especially if you proceed in a clockwise
direction from the first box (for less hiking/driving.)

Here are some other ideas for the area:

Of course, you gotta get Bill Cody by Es

Drivebys or indoor boxes:

Got An Eye on You (altchar) This one offers an indoor alternative for
a meeting spot if the group prefers.
Gold Miner (The Carving Clan)
Triceratops (FRH)
Town of Morrison (Dixie Peach)
The Fort (me)
I'm quite fond of my own Dream House box..

Those are just a few ideas off the top of my head.

It wasn't clear whether you'll be doing any boxing in Denver itself.
If so, I'd be happy to recommend some. Most (not all) are my boxes,
but in any case - most Denver boxes are also pretty easy
snags....though some have some clue solving first. Let me know and
I'd be happy to point ya in the right direction for some Mile High
City exploring.

Let me know what everyone thinks about my suggestion for a meet time
and place(s) and I'll take it from there by putting something up on
AQ.
