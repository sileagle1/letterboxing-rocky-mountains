The story on the treasure finder:

The guy just had a hunch that it would be on an island, the clues
didn't really help him much at all. And the island is small enough
that he just searched every possible nook and cranny.

This is the earliest the Silver Bullet has ever been found. In fact
they usually don't give clues that mean very much until Thursday
because they want people to listen to the radio all week. The guy
that writes the clues was a little dissapointed because the good
clues were never given.

The finder called the radio station with a deep pirate voice and
said, "You had better throw in two tickets to next year's Snowdown
Follies or you'll never see your sweet Silver Bullet again!" and hung
up. They used caller ID and went to where he worked and made him
confess to his rogue ways....Fun.
