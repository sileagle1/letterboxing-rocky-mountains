From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-02-06 16:55:58 UTC

While I was hunting around for evidence that "We Are Not Alone" I
came upon some other interesting treasures. I found Desert Bighorn,
Paddle Hard, Searching....., and Colorado River letterboxes
yesterday, all along the River Road between Moab and Cisco. There are
a total of seven very nice letterboxes accessed on this road now by
five different letterboxers. It's also one of the most spectacular
landscapes in the world. A person could spend most of an
unforgettable day finding them all. I think the first person to find
them all in one day should have the honor of naming the tour.
Something like "Grand Tour de CiscoMoMo", or hopefully something
better.

y-nought
