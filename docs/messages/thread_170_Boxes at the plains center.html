From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2004-03-21 22:30:03 UTC-07:00
I went when they had a "free day" ... it was nice, if different. It was a bit orchestrated, and the boxes were not difficult, but it was educational, and the prairie was nice. I was happy to be the first "non-staff" letterboxer to do them all. I saw a small herd of white tails, some burrowing owls, and things. The hardest one was the Sod Village, I thought - and the longest "walk" was the Coyote Canter. ..
There is NO SHADE at all - so don't wait for the middle of summer to do this. I did it right after the fourth of July last year, and was burnt to a crispy critter! :)

You might check to see if they're planning a free day, or find a day when they're doing some other interesting event, so your $5 goes a bit further.

Oh ... and I ALWAYS feel the thrill of "I found one" ... so this was no exception.

Best wishes,
Autumn
