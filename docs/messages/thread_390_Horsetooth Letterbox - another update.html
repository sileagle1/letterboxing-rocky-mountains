Horsetooth Letterbox - another update

From: Cattail (MLK) (marcikoski@earthlink.net) | Date: 2004-10-14 19:41:09 UTC

Howdy folks -

Good news this time - it seems that Horsetooth Mountain Park will
eventually allow letterboxes under a "special event" permit. The
policy is currently being revised; apparently they had some bad
experiences with geocachers, and their current policy is based on
that (at this time, NOTHING is allowed to be placed in the park, so
please don't plant anything without contacting the park first!).
But, in time, I think the Horsetooth Letterbox will be legally
replaced in its hidey-hole by Horsetooth Falls. I'll keep you
posted as I find out more, and will let you know later if the new
policy is just for Horsetooth Mountain Park or pertains to all of
Larimer County Open Space or Parks or whatever.

Yippee!!

Cattail :)
