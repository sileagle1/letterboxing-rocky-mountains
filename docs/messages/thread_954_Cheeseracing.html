I hadn't thought about the fact that many of you weren't letterboxing during
the whole cheeseracing controversy. That was a funny time on the big list.
It was back when I thoroughly enjoyed the list still. It was more of a
community for me. Don't get me wrong, we still had some doozies (sp?) of
flame wars. Anyway, you should search the list for cheeseracing. It's
fun. I love the cheeseracing website. I thought it was the funniest thing
I'd seen in a while.

I guess I didn't tell you about Texas ties. I lived there for a year and a
half (in the Houston area) while I did some work for my church. I have an
uncle who has been in the Dallas area for years, and two years ago my father
moved out to the Dallas area as well. When he did that, my grandparents
decided it would be better to be close to two children as they were
approaching their 90s so they also moved to Dallas.

It's like there is this big Texas magnet pulling us. I'm fighting it pretty
hard though. I enjoyed my time there, but I'm really not a TX gal. I need
mountains around me. During that 1.5 years in Houston, I hadn't come home.
I still remember flying back to SLC, and as we approached Colorado and I
finally began to see mountains out of the plane window, I literally began to
cry. The person next to me must have thought I was losing it. Who cries
when they see mountains out of a plane window? It just hit so hard that I
finally knew I was coming home. :)

So that's the Texas story. If Silver Eagle still reads the list, I'm
saluting in his direction.
