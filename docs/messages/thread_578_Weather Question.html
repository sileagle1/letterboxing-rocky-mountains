Weather in Moab in early April is always an iffy thing. There can be
as much as 60 degree changes from early morning to night. It is
usually pretty darn nice that time of year, although last year at
that time we did get some snow! Layers are always good, but
definitely bring some shorts! It is stormy and the rims are covered
in snow right now, but day before yesterday was shorts and t-shirt
weather.

Annnd remember, it's not how many miles you travel, but how many
boxes you can find on the way!!!!!!!
