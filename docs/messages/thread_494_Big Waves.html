From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2005-01-17 03:51:47 UTC

Ohhhhh, BIG sadness! One of my boxes with one of my best stamps in it
has been washed away in the California storms last week. I was so
sure it was planted up high enough, but rainwater flowing down from
the street above washed away the hiding rocks, and then it looked
like big waves also hit! Sooooo bummmmmed. It was in a good box, so
maybe it will wash up on some beach somewhere and someone will find
it. On the happy side, however, there are four new boxes planted in
this area, so I'll ease my pain by going on the hunt! Hope everyone's
having a good week!

Kat (thinking of easing the pain with some pie)
