From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2004-07-28 14:48:46 UTC
For those of us headed to Yellowstone -

Looks like the East entrance is OPEN! Whoohooo! I leave this evening,
and will update on everyone as to boxes we find. We are so excited!
There are so few boxes in the Rocky Mountain Region (comparably) so
it's always fun to go a smidge further to get some new ones!

Thank you to everyone with news on the Cu box. It has been repaired,
but we did not bring a pen and the message inside it is still pretty
much illegible. When we return from vacation, we will re-write it.
There is a stamp from SCOTLAND in it!! Too cool!

See you all on the other side.

~RavenWolf~
