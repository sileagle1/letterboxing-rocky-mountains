11/17/2007 - Box Replanted

This box is placed in honor of the "Going Fishing for LetterboxeRs in
Idaho" Gathering July 10, 2005. This is one non traditional Fishy
Box. Hope you have fun finding it!

Starting Point: Merrill Park 637 E. Shore Dr., Eagle, ID

Clues: From the large shelter walk past the playground (on your
right ) and small shelter (on your right) on your way to the river
(south). You will cross a bridge and come to a sign carved into a
large boulder that says "Eagle Pathway System City of Eagle 2004."

Take the paved path to your right and follow past mile marker NW 9.9.
continue down the paved path and you will eventually come to a path
on your left about 18 steps or 9 paces BEFORE mile marker NW10.0. If
you come to the mile marker first just backtrack the paces or steps.
The path is located east of the mile marker. Follow the trail towards
the river (south) approximately 66 steps or 33 paces until you reach
a "Y" in the path. We noticed some hay bails and twigs in the way.
Just go around and continue your hunt once you reach the path. Take
the path to your right (west) another 74 steps or 37 paces.

You will now be at two logs on the ground along the path on your
left. Starting at the beginning of the largest log take 10 steps or 5
paces and you will be at a break in the large log.

Hidden at the bottom of the crack under some bark and rocks you will
find the "GoldFish" Letterbox hiding.

Enjoy your visit to the Eagle Pathway System and Merrill Park. Please
re-hide the box carefully as this is an active path. Should you note
the box missing, it's contents in bad repair or if you just want to
say "hi" please contace me at Blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com. Happy hunting!
