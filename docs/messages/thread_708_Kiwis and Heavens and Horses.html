Thanks to a heads up from Ramdelt, I was able to do some box
maintence this weekend while we're here visiting relatives.

The little Kiwi was indeed gone.. my apologies to anyone who paid
the entry fee into the park only to come away empty handed.
HOWEVER! I zoomed back to my sister's house, cloned the stamp, used
an extra journal I had and even as we speak the Kiwi is once again
camping in his home away from home. With some very large rocks
wedging him in and even further out of sight.

New logbook in Harry's Heaven. The highlight of the day was reading
the old one which was very full. One of my favorite entries was
from an English gentleman who heard about the box while on holiday
in South Africa and made a point to seek it out when in Colorado!!
Too fun!

There are a few pages left in Twenty Mile, I'll replace it in a few
weeks when I return!

Hope you're all having a wonderful weekend!

Es, going upstairs for pie with the family
