April is Coming - And that means it is almost time for Rocky Mountain
Plant-A-Box month!

http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gEventId=1129

Get out your carving tools, your tupperware (or lock 'n locks) and dust off
your hiking boots! Sharpen your clue-writing wits, and join those of us who
(so far) have committed to planting at least one box during the month of
April.

And we may be holding a little gathering (at least for those of us in the
Colorado/Denver area) just to celebrate - so for you Rocky Mountaineers from
points further out - I encourage you to celebrate the month with your local
pie fanatics, too! :)
Cheers!
