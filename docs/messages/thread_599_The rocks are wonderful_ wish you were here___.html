Hello--
I just got back from a backpacking trip near Moab--it was
awesome! We got a little snow each night but it was gone before
breakfast was over. You can just feel the weather starting to settle
over there. There was green grass, a babbling brook, some waterfalls,
and some of the most colorful sandstone walls that I've ever seen.
(And I've seen a lot!) I just missed the weather window because it
was a bluebird day when we hiked back out today.

Esmerelda. It won't be the same without you. You can bet we'll
be doing some flag waving, saluting, and pie eating in your honor to
try to make up for it. And the famous missmoon coming out west? That
sounds like an excellent excuse for another gathering.

We get a traveling event stamp too? Suhweet!

I've got to say, I look forward to reading the posts here. And
after a few days away it was neat to come back and catch up. There
sure is a lot going on with Channel Seven Mitch, Jeffco's new rules,
Box on the Rocks, Carving Classes, PLB's. What struck me is how
everyone is fostering letterboxing with such care, helping it along
and treating it like the precious and precarious thing that it is.
Just something I noticed and am thankful for.
