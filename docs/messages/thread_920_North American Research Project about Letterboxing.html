North American Research Project about Letterboxing

From: marisa1of9 (marisafink@bsu.edu) | Date: 2005-11-04 14:55:16 UTC
You are invited to participate in a web-based survey looking at the
profiles and behaviors of individuals who participate in letterboxing.
This research is being conducted as part of a doctoral dissertation
about the activity of letterboxing. Any visitor 18 years of age and
older may complete the survey, and all responses are anonymous. To
voluntarily participate, go to
http://portal.bsu.edu/inqsit/inqsit.cgi/fink and select
`letterboxing'and 'continue'. Use your trail name or a pseudonym to
sign in. For more information about this research project, please
contact the Primary Investigator - Marisa Fink, Ball State University,
AC 414, 2000 W. University Avenue, Muncie, IN 47306, 765.285.1962,
marisafink@bsu.edu. Thank you for your participation.

RE: [RMLetterboxing] Re: North American Research Project about Letterboxing

From: Fink, Marisa Fuoti (marisafink@bsu.edu) | Date: 2005-11-05 03:49:23 UTC-05:00
JelyBean - I was far from offended. You made my day! It does sound bizarre to some people when I tell them I am doing doctoral research on letterboxing. After they realize that I am not talking about a video format, they become curious about the hobby/activity/sport/obsession. Ever since I got started in 2000 I have been fascinated by why people participate. In the summer of 2004 I spent a week in Dartmoor where it all began - it is so different in the UK. In typical fashion, Americans have made major changes in letterboxing. But it is all good. And I want to write this and lots more looking at why and how people participate in this great culture! Happy trails!

Marisa
