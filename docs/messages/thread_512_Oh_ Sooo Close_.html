Oh! Sooo Close!

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-01-29 23:30:17 UTC

I went into town, picked up Elros from a birthday party, and was on
Second Avenue by the Leland House when they announced all the clues
and added the next one:

25. The west is best.

26. Near the Summit the Dreamweaver will Rendezvous with Serenity.

28. Men stand close to enjoy the show, women remain seated.

So we hightailed it a block down to Main Avenue where The Summit,
Dreamweaver, Les Rendesvous, and Serenity are. In front of Magpies
were some portable toilets set up for the Superhero Quick Change
Contest. There were about eight people there looking things over. I
went up to the Johns and inspected them too, looking behind the
posters and up on top etc. After that it was kinda fun watching as
group after group of people came by and did the same thing. We
realized that we were too late, checked the bathrooms over at the
Strater Hotel and at Farquahrts just to make sure and came home. Much
to my surprise it was announced that it hadn't been found. The DJ
that was broadcasting live from Magpies at the contest said "A lot of
people are VERY WARM, they just need to step up and reach down deep
and pull". So then somebody stood on the flower bed by the Bob's
John, reached into the vent and pulled a blue string with the Bullet
Wrapped in a T-shirt so it wouldn't bang around when we shook the
John.

"West" referred to the west side of Main. Santa Rita, aka Gateway, is
right next to the sewer plant. Some other references I don't get, but
they will be posted some time next week.

The clues were much better this year and this is by far the closest
I've ever been. I'm a little ashamed that I wasn't thorough enough to
reach up there. If I were looking for a letterbox and I was alone, I
think I would have. (There are a lot of other people that are wishing
the same thing).

This was also the most fun I've had at this because my letterboxing
buddies were helping me and cheering me on. Thanks again to all of
you. We'll get it next year and there will be Mastercarve and pie for
everybody!

y-nought
