Sorry about that! Ya'll have been keeping us so entertained with
stories of new boxes and tales of ... Pie???

I had the opportunity to go letterboxing over my birthday. Was
visiting the Central California coast - Monterey Peninsula - and
found two boxes. Can I just say that it is so beautiful there?

Anyway - bitten by the boxing bug again, and am looking forward to a
free weekend to go out and get some of the great local boxes I've
been meaning to find, perhaps a trek to Ft. Collins for a boxing day.

I've been logging my finds at Atlas Quest - if ya'll haven't been
there yet, it's wicked cool. There's a link in the links section.
Anyway - I've missed you all, and hope we can coordinate some sort of
gathering in the late spring/early summer.

Best,
Autumn

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2005-02-16 01:53:37 UTC

*grins* I found the "Monterey Letterbox" just across the way from
the pier, and the "Point Pinos Lighthouse" which took forever to find
since we weren't in the right turnout. Finding that one was a treat,
as many folks noted in the logbook that they'd made two or three
attempts to find it. It converted my friend to letterboxing forever,
and now he's carving his own stamp! Whoop!

We were going to look for a few in PG, but ran out of time. Sorry
yours were picked up. That would have been awesome to find some of
your boxes out there.

You have to admit, Carmel is breathtaking --- spent some time on
Pebble Beach - groves of cypress to die for -- while I love CO, I
could easily move there (if it wasn't so damned spendy).

Autumn
