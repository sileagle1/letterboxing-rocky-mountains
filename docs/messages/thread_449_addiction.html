From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-12-23 18:51:08 UTC

Well, I've heard that there ARE no rules... so my opinion is to go
for it!! How fun for the placer to get back and find a surprise
waiting... that would be a hoot! The only time I went snowshoeing I
thought my heart was gonna explode outta my chest. Which is never
good. Anyway, if I went to put a box back and found a get well note
I'd think it was great.

I hate not finding the box, too. The worst one was when I drove to
Moab and hiked back in to find one of Kat's... nowhere to be seen,
although I found the rocks that had covered it pulled back. After I
got all the way back home I found out that someone from Lake Tahoe
had been sitting nearby...doing a little art.. having a little
lunch.. enjoying an afternoon in the sun by the water... with the
box at her side!!! DOH! It was back in it's hidey hole safe and
sound the next day. I wasn't a happy hiker but I got over it.
Sorta. LOL

Es, who isn't wearing anything on her feet more adventurous than
slippers on the 24th
