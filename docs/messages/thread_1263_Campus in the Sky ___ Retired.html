Campus in the Sky ... Retired

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-02-03 22:23:22 UTC
Hello-

I recieved a note last week that the maps were no longer under the
deer hoof for Campus in the Sky. I went and checked and found out
that whoever took the maps also used them to take the letterbox.

This letterbox was into it's fourth year and contained some very dear
and valuable stamps to me. I don't really have the heart to start all
over on this particular box, so I'm going to retire it. The clues
took a long time to walk through and the stamp was nothing very
special so I think it is better just as a memory. Lion's Den is still
there and unless the theives figure out what cougar the bonus clue
sheet is talking about, it will live on with it's own published clue.

I'm not as dejected as I sound, and I have an idea or two for some
new boxes around here. I just thought I should let you folks know in
case you have printed copies of that clue.

Box On!

y-nought
