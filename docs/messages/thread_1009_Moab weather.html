Re: Moab weather

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2006-02-09 22:06:14 UTC
Interesting that you mention a gathering! Y and I were just
discussing that a few nights ago. Box on the Rocks II.

Nobody should ever, and I mean NEVER EVER come to Moab the entire
week before and including Easter day. It is just to congested in
town with the annual Jeep Safari. During the day, everyone
disappears into the backcountry and several choice sites where it is
common to sit and watch the drunk drivers roll and smash their
jeeps, but in the late afternoon and evening town is in total
gridlock. Added to it this year is the fact that they are doing a
yearlong project ripping up main street to replace all the old pipes
and level out the road better. It is like a war zone here that week
because even if you can get away from the crazies, the Utah highway
patrol has a fleet of helicopters on constant patrol of the trails
all around the area. They zoom all around and it echos up and down
the canyons!

The weather at the end of March can be anything from hot, 80 degree
sunny days to snowing out! You never know from day to day. It is
common to have 50 to 60 degree differences from early morning to
evening. If you come then, bring lots of layers, including shorts.
The weather should be more predictable after Easter break, but if
you come before the break, the "season" is still quiet and you
should have the trails pretty much to yourself.

How exciting!
Kat
