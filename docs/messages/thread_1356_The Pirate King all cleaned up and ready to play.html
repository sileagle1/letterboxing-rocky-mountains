The Pirate King all cleaned up and ready to play

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-04 01:04:13 UTC
Skeetch and I visited the first box in our word of mouth series, 'The
Pirate King' today. To say it needed attention is an understatement!!
It was like being in the Haunted Mansion, only instead of cool fake
scary things created by highly paid imagineers, they were all real and
ooky. But now the outer box has been replaced with a nice shiny new
one, the clues have been refilled, everything is polished and wrapped
and clean and ooky-less and ready for more boxers in search of an
adventure!

So! If you would care to take a gander at the clues just drop me a
note. Even if you're a lurker. Arrrr. Pirates will play with anyone
who has a yearin' forrrrr excitement!

Es, looking with admiration on the three groups who braved the untended
box in the last couple of weeks and didn't make a peep of complaint!
