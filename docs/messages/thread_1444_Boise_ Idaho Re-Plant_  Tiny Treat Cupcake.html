Location: 664 South River Shore Lane, Suite 164, Eagle. Off of

The cutest little cupcake shop is in Eagle, Idaho. Welcome to Lilly
Jane's Cupcakes. Each cupcake is baked from scratch daily, using
only the finest ingredients such as sweetcream butter, rich
Madagascar Bourbon vanilla and a variety of specialty chocolates.

Park in the parking lot in front of Lilly Jane's Cupcakes. Walk in-
between Cold Stone Creamery and Windermere Real Estate towards the
fountain in the center of the complex. Take the short path to your
right. On your left you will see four large evergreen trees.
Your "Tiny Treat" is hidden behind the back of the last tree (closest
to Eagle Road) under leaves and three river rocks. Please re-hide
carefully as this is a busy complex. Make sure to visit Lilly Jane's
and have a tiny treat!
