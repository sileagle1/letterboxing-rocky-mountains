Declassified KGB Booklet

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2009-02-01 23:14:02 UTC
Hello people, JeLyBean here.

In the spring of last year, I came across a little curiosity on eBay.
It was a little booklet with a red leather cover and the KGB shield on
it. It looked to be a kind of declassified mission booklet for KGB
spies or something. I showed it to a few friends last year who thought
it was interesting. Since I know how much letterboxers like secrets
and maps and such, I thought it would be fun to share it with a wider
audience. So I slapped in on the scanner and put the pictures up
online here: http://home.comcast.net/~the.jefe/letterboxing/mink I
hope you get a kick out of it.

J
