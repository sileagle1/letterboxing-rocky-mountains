I just flew back from the Inkuisition, and boy, are my arms tortured.

It was so great to meet up with everybody who made it to the realm on
Saturday! Elros, Dark Side of the Moon, and I were trying to push and
shove our way onto a table when Dred Peach and Cappy showed up. Soon
after, Ravenwolf, Sparkle Fairy, Little Raven, Samurai Stamper, and
Brother Raven showed up in full renaissance dress. With their help we
got that table and took no prisoners. By that time the letterboxing
stories started flying and news of new boxes, plans of new boxes and
the upcoming Grand Lake Gathering were the hot topics. We paused from
time to time to curse Mapsurfer and his evil Bard. Later, Tie Dye Girl
found Peach, Cappy and I daydreaming of Pirates and lost treasure and
it was great to meet up with her again after her trip through Europe.

Unavoidably, I must join my real life now (already in progress).

Look for pictures posted by Dixie and I later this week!
