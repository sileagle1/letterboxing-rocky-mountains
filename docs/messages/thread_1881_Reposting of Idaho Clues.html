Reposting of Idaho Clues

From: Kelly (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2012-07-01 02:45:59 UTC
With the unveiling and discovery of the Idaho vandal and the returning of most of my stolen stamps I decided that it was probably time to re-list all of my Idaho clues on Atlas Quest and LBNA. I have spent about 5 hours on this task so far and I think that all of my active Idaho boxes are listed once again on Atlas Quest. I am still making updates on LBNA to sync the two sites but I am almost finished. If anyone notes any that I have missed along the way please let me know. One or two are bound to slip through the cracks. I started getting a little fuzzy around midnight last night working on this time consuming project. I forgot how much work it is to post clues for so many boxes but I am extremely thankful that we can finally post them once again for everyone to enjoy.
