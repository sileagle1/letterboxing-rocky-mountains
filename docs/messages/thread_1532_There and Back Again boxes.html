I know a few people are still carving, but I don't know what the
subjects of their stamps are yet and will be happily surprised when I
see them! I'll keep the list updated as they arrive.

At this point this will be 36 stamps on the mountain, plus 8 one day
stamps. Some of the boxes on the mountain will be in clusters.
Characters who go together may be very close to each other. Lnd_crzr
and I discussed it and felt it would give everyone a better chance of
getting all of the boxes, plus, people working their way up the
mountain in groups won't have to sit and wait while everyone stamps
in to one box at a time.
