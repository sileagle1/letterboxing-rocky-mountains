Jeffco Open Space - New Info!

From: Debbi Scott (dscott5377@earthlink.net) | Date: 2005-08-18 09:28:45 UTC-06:00
Jeffco Open Space Ranger, Drew, just called me - according to him, they are
no longer - at this time - going to require permits for letterboxes. nor are
they limiting how many boxes per park or per boxer.. HOWEVER, they would
still like to be notified when we place a box and they would still like us
to follow the rules of placing them on or very close to the trails, etc..



So - looks like my 2 boxes in Lair O Bear were NOT taken by Jeffco, but most
likely "found" by a non-boxer. Not sure if I will be replacing them or
not..
