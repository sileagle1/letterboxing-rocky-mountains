We Catamount Carvers love history! It's a favorite subject!

If you've got a little time to burn, just search for the word PIE in the
archives. It will provide a complete history of pie in the world of
Rocky Mountain Letterboxing!

I will tell you that the first message with reference to pie turns up on
August 17, 2004 and it was Esmerelda. No surprise is it?

In reference to the Onion I, she says ...

"I even tried bribing him with pie and a complimentary beverage.
nada. evil. evil I tell you.

Esmerelda, weeping giant onion-induced tears"

On October 25, 2004, Esmerelda replies to Aljan's mention of getting
Dragonfruit at a Trader's Joe and says,

"I haven't tasted it. I'm waiting for a report. But next time I get
to the Trader's I'll pick some up and let y'all know if pirates like
Dragonfruit. I know they like pie."

It just builds and gets more and more out of control from there. Now you
could argue that pie came up with Eoghan's RC Cola and Moon Pie boxes,
but let's face it ... does any one really consider that pie? I think we
can squarely place blame for this RMLetterboxing obsession with our
favorite pirate ... Esmerelda!

~Silent Wings of the Catamount Carvers
