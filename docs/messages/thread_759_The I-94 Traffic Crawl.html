Hey Rocky Mtn. LBers. We Great Lakes LBers are starting a series
called "The I-94 Traffic Crawl". Many of us have taken long,
frustrating drives through Chicago, Detroit, Minneapolis or other
areas along I-94 and are anxious to create some diversion. I've
driven I-94 from start to finish and know the problems in ND and MT
are quite different from Chicagoland. Would anyone be interested in
putting a box or two somewhere between Billings and Fargo?

Here's the info I gave to the Great Lakes board:

Let's call the thing "The I-94 Traffic Crawl" and prefix the boxes
accordingly (e.g. "The I-94 Traffic Crawl: Gary, IN". THEME: Just
play off the word "crawl" and think "traffic" - we've all been there,
right? Try and tie it in to your location somehow. Keep things a
mile or two off the freeway and send us somewhere interesting. We
need stimulation after staring at brake lights (or endless hills in
eastern MT).

Submit ideas and locations to me off-list and I'll make sure we avoid
duplicates.

Let's also try to avoid the Oases (in Chicago) since a few people are
already headed in that direction. Get us off the freeway!

It would be great to have all the major cities represented as well as
the start and end points of the freeway. (We're particularly looking
for one near the Billings West End of I-94.)

This should be interesting and make our driving fun. I'm starting to
look forward to my next MI or Minneapolis trip. When you travel this
route, think about adding a box at a rest stop or park.

Looking forward to your emails and ideas,
M Clan
