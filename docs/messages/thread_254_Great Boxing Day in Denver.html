Great Boxing Day in Denver

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2004-07-08 01:46:22 UTC
Went out today and was able to snag six boxes! Here's the status
report for the placers!

Harry's Heaven ... found - in great shape
Twenty Mile ... found - also in great shape
Cool locations both of those, and beautiful stamps!!!

Nocturnal Scorpion - found - box was soaking and full of mud, but
contents were fine in their ziplock bags ...
The Sea Goat - found - great location
Dual Mindedness - found - fantastic clues. contents were not in a
ziplock and the entire inside was wet. Most of the stamps in this
box were waterlogged. I added a ziplock to the microbox, and
hopefully that will protect the logbook going forward.
All three of these boxes in Debbi's series have amazing stamps .. :)

Thanks for noticin' me ... gound - great location.

Great boxing day,

Autumn
P10 F49 X22
