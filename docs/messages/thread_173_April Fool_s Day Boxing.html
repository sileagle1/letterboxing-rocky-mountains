April Fool's Day Boxing

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2004-04-02 17:54:07 UTC
Had a weekday off, so thought I would go boxing and take advantage of
that small patch of good weather.

I was able to find "T-I-DoubleGuh-Rrrr", "Oh Bother", and "Blue
Heron", before I had to call it a day due to time and other
committments.

It was fun to see the stamp signatures of folks I recognize in
several of the boxes ... Eoghan, Pumpkin Princess, Dixie, TyeDye Gal,
and many others! :)

Great new boxes - thanks to the Treasure Seekers for planting
them! ;)
