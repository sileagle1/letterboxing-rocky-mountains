The Pirate's Code

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-22 15:30:25 UTC
While my navigator, Elros, and I were sailing through the Western
Azores we became shipwrecked on the reef. The starboard oar was rent
and we found ourselved marooned. Such a fate often befalls this
Captain, who is usually thinking of letterboxes and oblivious to
important practical considerations.

But our fortune changed when the good buccaneer Esmerelda sailed into
view. We boarded her cruiser and went to port procuring much needed
supplies and an iron of the tire. But Esmerelda's help went beyond the
fetching of essentials, and her greatest deed was the lifting of our
spirits; turning our travails into joyous adventure.

So even though the Pirate's Code is to leave behind those who fall
behind, Esmerelda noted that the Code is really more of a guideline
and used her discretion to help the poor seafarers.

Thanks Es! You are, and will always be, the Greatest Buccaneer Ever!

y-nought
