Rumor has it that there is a new mystery box recently planted in Colorado
by the Catamount Carvers.


HONEYWINE LETTERBOX CLUES

I dont care for honey wine,
But the view from there is mighty fine.

Down thirty four a treasure lies.
The evening sun may blind your eyes.

Silo one is painted blue.
Silo green is number two.

Go a hundred yards beyond,
Hang a right youll see a pond.

Three ponds lie within the dyke.
Stroll around them if you like.

At middle pond theres something more
Hidden near its eastern shore.

A young trio of cottonwoods stand
Where the water meets the land.

Look beneath and youll discover
A flattened rock that is a cover.

Covers what? You may wonder.
Could it be? Secret plunder?
