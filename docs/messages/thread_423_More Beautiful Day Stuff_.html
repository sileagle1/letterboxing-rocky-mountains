More Beautiful Day Stuff!

From: luvsmoab (klawry@citlink.net) | Date: 2004-11-09 03:51:25 UTC

What an awesome little road trip we took! Just an overnight trip for
fun, so there was some time to do some boxing. Started with a stop
at the Animas Overlook, found the beautiful boxes up there, and
planted the Animas Coyote Box (clues coming soon) as well. If you
haven't been to this spot, it is gorgeous enough by itself, but then
to have a letterbox trail of 5 boxes is just TOO much fun. Then it
was off to continue to Durango, where we found a couple of fun maps
to boxes (clever) and to the beautiful Denver Rio Grande Box. All in
all, I think everyone needs to take a road trip to this area for
some excellence in boxing fun!!!

Kat (home just in time for a hard rainstorm) from Moab

Woot! I LOVE getting visitors and a new box to boot! This is getting

I also took a road trip and squeezed in a little boxing. I caught a
Wild West Show, Picked up Jodie's "..It Will Give You Wings" HH,
talked with a little bird, visited a King, ran into a very rude
robot, and a few others before I contemplated the meaning of life.

amazed at how many great trails there are out there, and how many
neat stamps you can find on them.
