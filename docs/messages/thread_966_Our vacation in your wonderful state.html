Our vacation in your wonderful state

From: callik4lb (gilbe@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-12-28 21:51:55 UTC
Hello all - I'm back home now and wanted to tell you what a wonderful
time I had in Colorado. I was able to leave you a box but haven't had
time to post the clues yet - it will be called "A Gift From Oregon"
and lies outside of Coaldale a ways on the Rainbow Trail. We weren't
able to find any boxes actually placed by you Colorado boxers,
although we looked for JelyBean's Turret Ghost Town box - ran out of
daylight and thought it would be too dangerous to "go for it" even
though we did find the location before we had to leave the area. We
also found a box called Rainbow Trail placed by Edna, who lives in
Missouri, and it sure could use some attention. I e-mailed her and
encouraged her to try to get some help from local boxers - its lid is
broken, log book is damp, and stamp is missing, although there is one
in the box left by a letterboxer. It's such a huge box that I
couldn't hide it any better than it was when I came across it - really
out in plain sight, and it has morphed into a kind of hiker log book -
tons of entries by hikers passing by and checking it out. Since it is
damp and broken, I didn't hide it any better than I found it so people
will continue to empty water out if it gets in. Anyway, we thoroughly
enjoyed our time together and loved the Cabin on Shelf and Red Canyon
Park boxes, also all the gorgeous places we were able to see. You
truly have a wonderful state that rivals my own, and I don't say that
lightly, because Oregon is known for its scenic attractions. I am
especially impressed with your parks and roadside recreation areas,
nice bathrooms, no fees, so well maintained. Can't wait to come back.
Hope you all enjoy the little present I left for you, although if
your unseasonably warm weather changes no one will see it until
spring/summer most likely. Your boxing friend, Calli-K aka Kathy Gilbert
