Re: Atlas Quest question

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-06-30 14:29:15 UTC
There are indeed clues that only appear if you have the premium
membership on AQ. Ryan doesn't have it set up to search *only* for
those clues, though.. what happens is that they'll pop up in a
search. So, if there were Premium Members Only clues in Parker and
you were searching for boxes in Parker, they'd appear. You can tell
that they're premium because the little Robin Hood hat appears at the
top of the clue.

There is also a board that is only accessable by premium members but
it's mostly just chatting. :)

I will have some clues posted here in the next week or so that will be
limited. They may end up just being word of mouth, but if I do decide
to post them they'll only be in the premium area of Atlas Quest.

Es, rambling
