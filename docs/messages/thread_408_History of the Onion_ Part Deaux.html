History of the Onion, Part Deaux

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-27 20:17:18 UTC

The Onion is no more. In a comedy (tragedy) of errors during the last
couple of weeks my 87 year-old father lost messages, which lead to
the Nice Open Space Ranger leaving for the weekend and the Evil Space
Ranger throwing away the logbook and stamp. This really stinks. I was
SO very happy at the prospect of keeping a copy of your stamps in
that journal, and I understand it was nearly full.

The only thing I can do to make it feel better is to plant another
Onion. This crop will be near the old onion patch. I hope to get up
in that area in the next couple of weeks. When it is in the ground I
will post revised clues.

Viva la Letterbox!
y-nought
