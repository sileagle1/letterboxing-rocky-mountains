Lunchboxing/Addiction

From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2004-12-17 04:15:31 UTC

Oh yes, we have all been there, and thank you y-nought for the great
new term I am sure I will use on many future occasions.

I was in Maryland this week on business:

Says JeLyBean's colleagues: "Hey JeLyBean, we're all going out to
this place for lunch, want to go? - they're supposed to have better
crab cakes than God can get in Heaven."

Says JeLyBean, "Umm, I have a, uh, thing I need to do. Raincheck?"

Says JeLyBean's colleagues, "Your loss..."

One hour later...

Says JeLyBean's colleagues, "Wow, you really missed it J-Man, those
crab cakes were so good I almost passed out."

Says JeLyBean around his mouthful of quarter pounder with
cheese, "Bummer..."

Thinks JeLyBean, ...but then again, none of you suckers added 2 to
your F count! Looooosers!
