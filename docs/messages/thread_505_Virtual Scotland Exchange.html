From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2005-01-27 18:04:20 UTC

As you may know, goddess, Princess Tupil and I are heading to
Scotland in a couple of months. We've been in touch with a former
Colorado letterboxer who lives there now and think we've identified
a few letterboxes to find along our route. We're also planning on
leaving one with a prototype of goddess' signature stamp so in a way
she can stay forever in Scotland.

I was thinking, it might be fun to have some of you fine Rocky
Mountain people's stamps in the log book we leave, so in a small way
it's like you've travelled along with us. If you're interested,
please email me off list at jelybean "at" adelphia "dot" net and
I'll tell you how to get an image to me. I can offer in return to
send an image of the stamp we're leaving, it's a nude goddess icon
thingy, if you've seen goddess' signature stamp in a letterbox near
you it's very similar, a little smaller.

I also thought it might be fun to have some hitchhikers "cross the
pond" so if you have a cool hitchhiker you'd like to donate, email
me off list. I can probably only take a couple.

Happy 'Boxing
JeLyBean
