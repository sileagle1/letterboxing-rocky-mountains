Here is the cross-post from AtlasQuest. This event is limited to 20 people! Please sign-up on AtlasQuest so I know who all is coming.

>Hey everyone!

Let's all get together and talk shop. Transfer and carving techniques, logbook creation, pouch making, etc, etc.

The always spectacular Tai Chi and Chai Tea have been generous enough to find an indoor venue for us to meet in.

Couple of notes:

1. I am NOT going to carve an event stamp for this. I'm sure there will be some new folks, and feel free to exchange if there is time!

2. This won't be your typical meet-and-greet. I had gotten several requests to share how I do what I do, and my plan is to do this in a seminar or workshop type setting. As a result, I am limiting this to ages 12 and older. I love all of your wolflings! Please don't get me wrong, I don't want to offend. But I am gearing this toward an older audience. Let's see how this goes, and maybe we can talk about a kid-specific workshop in the future.

I'm still working on all the details. Look for a materials list to be posted within the next two weeks. Don't panic! I won't be making you bring any specialty items. Everything can be bought at any of the local craft stores, or brought from your own stashes.

Looking forward to seeing you all!
