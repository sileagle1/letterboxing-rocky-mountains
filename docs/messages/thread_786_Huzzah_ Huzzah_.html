Although we didn't go inside and have eclairs, we did visit Larkspur
today and plant another letterbox at the Renaissance faire. I know, I
know.. the faire is only open two more weekends but, there you go. My
favorite son, Revman, went with us for the drive and picked right up
on the whole cover up technique. The new spot isn't as visible as the
old one, but it is still just a few steps off the path and we had
quite a few people walk by during the semi-covert operation. Rev was
pretty amused that pretending to talk on a cell phone while sitting in
the dirt next to a giant fence kept people from being interested in
what hijinx we were up to. He pulled his phone out and we double
teamed it while Skeetch hummed a pirate ditty and kicked pine cones
around.

This is a whole new box.. new clues, new stamp, new journal. We'll
just retire the first one with a moment of silence and a salute with a
fine eclair. Arrrrrrrr.

Es, heading home to foof up the feathers on her splendid pirate hat
