Loverly weekend!

From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2005-01-11 19:41:47 UTC

Hello all!

Samurai and I got out this weekend (finally!) and found an Indian
Chief and a Stegosaurus.

Really lovely stamp, Es. I love your little journal too! And as we
were leaving, a whole herd of elk were out near the fence, not 10
feet from where we were! Very cool.

And we really enjoyed the hike up to the Stegosaurus, JelyBean. It
was great to get out. Nothing like a hike up the side of a mountain
to let you know how out of shape you are after the holiday. =3

We wanted to get an Onion, but we ran out of light... Such is winter
LBing!

Thanks to all!

~RavenWolf~
