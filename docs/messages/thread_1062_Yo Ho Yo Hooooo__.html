Yo Ho Yo Hooooo!!

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-04-08 14:25:12 UTC
After I did the snappy It's My Day Off pirate jig this morning, I
signed into my email and almost fell out of me chair when the 'your
box has been found' reports flooded the screen! ARRRR!!! It whar
like Christmas mornin' on the poop deck!!

Between the birthday boxer PRE and the visitin' Art trekker the local
boxes have seen the sun more times than they have since y'all were
here!

They both report box number one of 'there were two guys' missing, so
I'll be sailin' down thar today to check it out and get started on a
new stamp if it's walked the plank. But that means that neither of
them know the first part of the joke! I'll be tellin' them the first
part.. for a DOLLAR!! BWAR HAR HAR!!

It was sure fun to see all the notes this morning. Can't wait to
hear trip reports! I hear that PRE has a most excellent story to
share on one of the Zebediah Gump boxes. WOOT!

Es, who wonders if the east coast boxers do a zippy jig when two
boxers in one week visit their boxes
