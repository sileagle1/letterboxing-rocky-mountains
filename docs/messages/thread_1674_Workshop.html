Okay all - I know, I know. It's almost April. I have no idea where the time has gone. I lost track of Feb entirely. =3

But I am more than happy to throw together a workshop for anyone who is interested. Topics would include carving (gouge and xacto), transfer methods, logbooks, and anything else we all want to share info on. Does anyone have any nifty ideas? Maybe a church, or community room or clubhouse something that we could all borrow?

Lemme know! As soon as I get a spot, I can start working on a date.
