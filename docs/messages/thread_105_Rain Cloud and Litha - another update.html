Okay - braving the rush hour traffic and fading evening light, I
hiked up Dakota Ridge last night and checked on both boxes.

Litha is exactly where it was supposed to be. Several vistors -
including the infamous Wanda (of Pete & Wanda) who made this box her

just hidden well.

sending the full book along to Funhog this week. This area needs
another box, and had there been another hour or so of light to work
with, I might have hidden another one up there.
