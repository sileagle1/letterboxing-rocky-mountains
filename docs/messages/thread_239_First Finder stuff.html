Hey Carrie,
A First Finder certificate is basically just a "prize" that would go to the first finder of your box. FF items can range from a special image of the stamp - to a little paper certificate that you might design on your computer to "congratulate" the first person to find the box. It is a special treat to add to your log book. I found one box that had a little ziplock back with an old coin inside (the coin is now in my logbook) ...

It's not necessary to include a FF prize or certificate, but it certainly is spiffy to find one.
