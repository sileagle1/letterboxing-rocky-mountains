From: (cadenza74@earthlink.net) | Date: 2003-12-12 00:45:16 UTC-07:00
I can't believe you remembered that Aryn! Yes, I saw a moose this fall,
well, I've seen several, but I saw this one in Yellowstone while I was
making a loop trip through ID, WY, and a bit of MT looking for letterboxes.
I took a roommate and her friend along. It was the first time for them to
go to Yellowstone and it was the maiden letterboxing voyage for the friend.
To make the trip even more cool, it was a bull moose. I love it when I see
moose in the wild. The first one I ever encountered was when I was a girls'
camp counselor in UT. I was taking my girls for a hike and we ran into a
mother moose and it's calf. So cool!! The only challenge was keeping a
group of thirteen year-olds away from her so they didn't get charged.

Well, I guess I don't need to account more adventures in the wild. I'm
happy to see you joining us Aryn. This list is fun. It isn't nearly as
busy as the main list and has a decidedly nicer tone, although I still love
reading both. Don't worry about not being up against the Rockies. There
are some here on the list that don't really live at all near the Rockies,
but they have planted boxes in them and keep up with questions through the
list. Even though I live closer to to what most would consider the Rockies
(although I guess the Sawtooths would probably still fit in that range), I
think you've still planted more boxes than many of us here. We're glad to
have you!

Till later,
Cadenza
