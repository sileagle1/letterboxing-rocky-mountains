Mmmm, sweet onion and a patch...

From: Aljan Sundance (aljansundance@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-07-26 07:04:56 UTC-07:00
Heading home from RenaiPalooza I got a sudden taste for onions and stopped by to stamp in. That box felt like a real achievement! I didn't have the clues with me so I had fun doing my best to remember!

After spending about an hour in construction delays I headed home planning to leave a note for Silver Eagle hoping that we could meet up for an exchange. After following a car for a bit that looked a bit lost, thinking I had scored the right people, I gave up and headed to one of my boxes. Passed a nice looking family close to the box with a couple of kids who seemed to be having fun pegging each other with pine cones and a Dad who was awfully smiley about the whole affair... I turned around to take a second look, wondering if his pack could be holding a journal and stamps and there was the LBNA patch that gave him away... then I wasn't so freaked out to ask a total stranger, hey! Are you Silver Eagle? It was perfect timing. We had a nice exchange and chatted for a bit. What a nice guy!

A fine ending to a highly enjoyable weekend!

Aljan

Well, I just got back from the Palooza, and, boy, is my kilt tired.

was over and we had to leave the magical kingdom full of great
letterboxers behind. It was only my second gathering, but in some
