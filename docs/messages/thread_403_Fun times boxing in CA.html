Fun times boxing in CA

From: JODIE KERN (jodiemkern@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-10-21 19:54:24 UTC-07:00
Just came back from a short trip to CA... talked my Aunt into doing some boxing (letterwhat? What is it that you want to do? Explain it to me one more time...). Hit a fun Halloween series hidden in places around LA where famous murders or suicides happened. Kinda morbid but really cool! Wouldn't be surprised if somebody called the police... I was out at the highest span on "the suicide bridge" looking for the box in the pouring down rain (don't fret, I safely toted the box back to the dry confines of the car for stamping in and then returned the box), soaked to the bone, with no shoes - who walks off without packing sneakers when they know they're going boxing leaving themselves nothing but stupid heels? and certainly looking quite lost as I peered around the rails of the bridge searching for the box. I am sure that more than one passing motorist thought I was going for the big jump!

A good time and my Aunt has already put in a request for her stamp design. Took a hitcher to plant but couldn't find a box big enough. Bum.

And I brought back snacks for Es! Yummies from Trader Joes!!
