-------------- Original message --------------
Will Stubby the Toothslayer keep going? We were a pretty nutty bunch to
begin with, but when you get us sleep deprived from a combination of
driving, staying up carving on top of that, running around on trails all
day, and just plain full of letterboxing excitement, we must just look like
a psychotic bunch! We'll have to try it again with more sleep . . . Oh, who
am I kidding?
We did get back to both Searching and Ticaboo, well it was my 1st time at
Searching. I loved the spot and absolutely loved the clue. I will admit to
frustration initially as we passed many of what I considered to be boulder
fields and house size rocks (Blackvelvetrav asked "what value home? 1 Mill?
70K? :) But when we got to the one the clue referred to, I thought "this has
GOT to be it! Big and hard to get around." I'm a LOTR fan too, so the clue
was just fun. I wrote the poem down in my logbook to remember. It was nice
to see that it was from someone who did the books, not just the movie. Not
everyone knows about things like entwives. I also emptied my film at Tikaboo
with some fun scenery. I love that country!
Ramdelt, I think we just missed you at searching. We got there with
Boxerlover too and kidnapped her to go find more boxes together. CW
Sunseeker and I spent a lovely afternoon hitting more boxes on 128 with her.
I'm still just tickled with finally being able to put names with faces and
sharing the time with all of you. I've never met another boxer on the trail
in my life and this weekend I bumped into five or six on the trail and about
the same number in restaurants. It was a kick! I've always been so jealous
of those eastern types talking about seeing others out hunting on the same
trail! I can't tell you how fun it was for me to pull up to Moab Man for a
quick find before the gathering after 8-9 hours of driving, and to spot
A-Bear and find out CW Sunseeker, Blackvelvetrav and Lizman were in the car
along with Ramdelt and Stubby, who they had bumped into earlier. It was such
a surreal experience for me! Now I need more of those.
till later,
Cadenza
-who isn't doing the best job of getting to work.
