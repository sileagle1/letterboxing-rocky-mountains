Went to snag this box this morning for the fourth time and low &
behold there were a bunch of people there again, even at 9am. I
guess there was a class or something. I still managed to walk around
and look for the box, but did not find it. Anyone find this
recently? I will try and get back there and see if I can find it. I
already knew where I thought the box should be, but it was not there,
so I checked a couple of other places and still did not find it. If
anyone who has found it is in the area, can you double check and see
if it is still alive? I know that the placer does not live here, so
any help would be great!!

From: Autumn (Autumn@shadowslight.com) | Date: 2003-10-01 10:30:00 UTC-06:00
We found this box on the 14th. The place was packed, lots of cars and busses. So ... here's what we did. We sat on the big rock (the one listed in the clues) like we were waiting for someone. It was easy with two of us so one could lookout, and one could snag the box. (remember its a microbox)

then we pocketed it and took it to the car to stamp in. We waited awhile, and once again, used the same method to replace the box. Worked out okay. We watched the area for awhile afterward to make sure that no one saw us and went to the hiding spot.

Terry, email me offlist if you want more help with the location of the box.
Autumn
