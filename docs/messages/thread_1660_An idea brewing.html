An idea brewing

From: Kat (Autumn) (autumnkat@gmail.com) | Date: 2009-02-02 07:40:31 UTC-07:00
Hey all ...
I know that AQ has an annual "plant a letterbox day", but I thought it might
be fun to set up an event for the Rocky Mtn gang - Plant a Box MONTH. There
are some of our wonderful local area boxers who plant like a VILLION boxes,
and I thought this might be a fun way to encourage others to plant one (or a
few?) so that later this spring and summer we would all have a variety of
new boxes to find! Of course, you'd be able to plant as many as your heart
desires, or just get out there and do some maintenance to reactivate old
boxes, etc. etc.

I know ... I know ... we shouldn't need an event to encourage us to plant.
Actually, I dont believe we do. In fact, I know boxers who have 10+ boxes
sitting at home waiting for the perfect sunny unencumbered weekend to go on
a planting spree! I just thought it would be a fun thing, and perhaps some
of us (ahem ... that'd be me) might be encouraged to get carving and get out
on the trails sooner and get some of their creations hidden. Personally, I
tend to go in wild spurts. I planted a bunch in 2003, then I was a bonehead
for a few years and either didn't box at all (gasp!) or boxed a little but
didn't plant. I admit, sometimes I need a little encouragement, so this is
as much to encourage myself as others.

Any thoughts? I thought I would shoot for March or April (march tends to
be a very snowy month).

AutumnsFolly
(who actually does have boxes made and ready to plant, believe it or not!)
