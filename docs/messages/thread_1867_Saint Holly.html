Saint Holly

From: rvn_wolf (treikya@gmail.com) | Date: 2012-04-01 22:38:20 UTC
Hello my fellow Rocky Mountain letterboxers!

Long time no chat? How are we all doing? How is the warm spring weather treating you?

Today, as some of us know, is Holly's birthday. Our famous PreBoxed loved this holiday, and tried to incorporate her wit into the day every year.

At her memorial, I had said that Aljan and I were going to put together a memory stamp/book for her, and that has been completed. (Not kidding - promise. :))

I have spent a lot of time trying to decide what to do with it, and how to handle it. Holly knew a LOT of people all over the country, and I would like to send the book out for the people who knew her to sign and stamp, but I want to give those of us from her home region first crack at it.

I would like it to be a memorial book in the true sense of the word. No just stamp and go. I want people to put in pictures, LTC's personal stories about how they knew Holly, or what they knew of her. I want people to take few pages and express themselves.

I was really hoping to have a gathering put together by today, but illness kept me busy the entire month of March.

How many of you would be interested in getting Aljan's fabulous stamp, and contributing? It's going to take some time, and it's not exactly a traditional gathering. If I can get a bit of a head count, I can start arranging a time/place.

Miss you guys! We need to get together for some spring fun.

~RavenWolf
