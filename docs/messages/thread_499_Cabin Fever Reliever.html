From: jelybean_lb (jelybean@adelphia.net) | Date: 2005-01-25 18:22:43 UTC

Coupla' thoughts y-nought,

Clue #2, a famous incident of someone cheering on his grandson
happened last year when convicted felon Charles Keating showed up at
a swim meet to cheer on his grandson. Keating was convicted for his
role in the Lincoln Savings & Loan scandal of the 80s and spent 5
years in prison. I would investigate: 1) is there a Licoln Savings
in Durango, 2) any other connection with Lincoln, 3) there was a
Colorado (Denver?) man who was involved in Keating's prosecution,
don't know his name, but any connection with Durango?

Clue #3, the song is Land Down Under, which refers to Australia.
Vegemite is a uniquely Australian food. I would investigate, 1) is
there a restaurant in Durango that serves Australian food, 2) is
there any other link between Australia and Durango, 3) what other
connotations of "down under" might have significance.

Clue #4, Inside/Outside, get a copy of the current issue and
investigate for connections to the clues.

Clue #5, the Snowdown poster this year shows Snowman and Weather
Woman battling Dr. Drought. Minus the medical personnel, this would
just be "drought." Investigate 1) areas of Durango affected by
drought, 2) organizations working against the drought, maybe the
local water company?

Good luck!

JeLyBean
