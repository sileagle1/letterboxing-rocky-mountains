Adventure on Mt. Doom

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2008-07-23 23:10:59 UTC
Early this morning with my two trusty helpers Sasha and Elwing, I
packed up the Pearl with three large bags full of most excellent
letterboxes and headed up to Mt. Doom.

Bring mosquito spray.

Carefully we worked our way across and up the great mountain,
planting and writing clues, leaving beacons and discussing routes.
Higher, nearly to the bleeding troll we climbed. When, without
warning, the skies opened up and a deluge poured forth!! Scrambling
into a nearby hobbit hole we hunkered down, telling stories and
hooting when lightening split the sky! Sasha made a raincoat out of
one of the plastic bags. But I'm never supposed to tell anyone
that.

Eventually the skies cleared and the sun broke through and we headed
up to finish our task. Nearly to the top we discovered that King
Theoden had escaped from the bag and was nowhere to be found! Oh,
no! Elwing said, 'uh.. who's going to bring him back up here if we
find him?' She's a trouper.

The view from the Nazgul and Witch King took our breath away. I wish
I had my camera with me.

After much rock scrambling and careful climbing all of the new boxes
were resting safely in their homes, waiting for their much
anticipated visitors. We crept down the mountain, searching for the
elusive Theoden all along the way. He was hiding in the trunk, the
rascal. He'll go back up when the last few boxes arrive.

We drove home with the top down, the sun drying our clothes.

Bring mosquito spray.

Es, who has to take Sasha and Elwing to the movies tomorrow to pay
them back
