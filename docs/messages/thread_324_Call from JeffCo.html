Call from JeffCo

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-02 01:13:42 UTC
Hi all and a special welcome to Jodie--
I just got off the phone with an Open Spaceman. He called me in
Durango from his cellphone as he was searching for the Onion
Letterbox. After I clarified the clues he walked over to it and
basically told me over the phone that it was approved. Before he
found the exact location he was concerned about keeping people away
from a gully that was prone to erosion.

I just wanted to pass this on and say that these folks were
great to deal with from beginning to end. Since I don't live up there
I won't be part of an ongoing arrangement of approval, but I am
optimistic that some of the ideas discussed here would be well
recieved by JeffCo. As always we need to do our part to make sure our
boxes don't inadvertently harm these areas. When they see this is the
case I think approval will be quick and painless.


y-nought

P.S. I've had those letterboxes of Jodies on my wish list for a
while. Can't wait to check them out.
