While we anxiously await the next installment of Dread's sweet
story, I have a request.

If anyone is going to be in the area of Rocky Mountain Clydesdales
and would check on it for me, I'll hoist a tankard of ale and down a
slab of pie in your honor! And be eternally grateful!

A family was unable to find it last week, but it was their first box
so they weren't sure if they were in the right spot. Plus, anyone
who's been there knows that the bush has about a bajillion leaves
under it that the box likes to migrate into. So.. I hate to call it
missing until I know for sure! Actually, I hate to call it missing
even if I DO know for sure! Blimey, as Dread would say!

On another note, new journals went into Grizzly Creek and Buffalo
Bill today. An all new Little Raven was hidden in a new spot and a
mystery box dedicated to the evil Bean is snuggled into it's home.
Clues will go up when I get some more days off and can hunker down
and do them right.

Is this a fun hobby or what?

Es, who had a blast reading full journals, climbing around mountains
and peeking under a hundred different rocks today
