Part 2 of Peach's Fireside Stories

From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2006-09-12 13:23:29 UTC
It was a bright and sunny dayyes, sunny! Cappy and Peach grabbed
some eats and went off into the daylight to hunt for boxes. This
after planting one of their own at the base of Winter Park the day
before. The SKI TRAIN letterbox holds a special fondness for them,
wellfind the box and you'll know why! They needed to photocopy the
clues, which were hand written on five pages of hotel note pad
paper, for the others meeting at the Grand, Grand gathering and
stopped in town at the copy store. The store was busy with activity
but a girl asked if she could help them. They asked to use a copier
and she gave them the key for one of the two in the shop. A
strange man was using one copier immediately next to the one they
were to use. He appeared to be filling an order for another
customer. As Cappy and Peach approached the available one, he
reached out and asked for the key and pushed his assistance on
them. When he asked them for the paper that needed copying, he
grabbed at the clues and actually tried to start reading them!
Cappy quickly, but politely, took them back and placed them face
down on the printer. They took one copy and discovered that the
pages weren't quite in order. Peach asked them to rearrange two of
the pages. The man, AGAIN, tried to read them as he took over moving
the pages. Peach wasn't happy and the DREAD started rising to the
surface! ARRRRRR! She wanted to pull out her cutlass and tie a
scarf around his peeping eyes and his hands behind his head! NO
PEEPING!!! Needless to say, Cappy quickly finished up the copying
so they could get out of there, knowing Irish Peach could get pushed
past the Dread point! Peach snatched her precious clues from the
man and they paid for their goods and got out of the shop, feeling
his peeping eyes following them as they left!

They cursed the man and wondered why he was so brazen as to attempt
to read their personal papers! After a bit of discussion, a light
went off in their head! Could it be? Was it one of the men that
were after Kyle Fincher??? Oh my gosh! If you don't know who Kyle
is, you MUST find the "RED" letterbox to learn his tale! As they
drove out of town, they kept their eyes peeled for a green Bronco!

That was strange! But, not as strange as THE STRANGEST BIRD
letterbox they stopped for on the way. You learn something new
every day, even with bad guys on your tail! When they made one last
stop, at GRAND LAKE letterbox, their hearts went cold when the tree
that it was hidden near was chopped to the ground.the men after
Fincher, again? Will they stop at nothing! Then, as they looked
out onto the Lake they shivered as the spirits of, SPIRIT LAKE, THE
COMMISSIONER, AND MOMMY DEAREST took form and seemed to reach out to
them. Cappy and Peach had signed into their logbooks sometime ago
and felt themselves being pulled towards the other side of the lake
and the gathering! As they headed towards the gathering, they felt
the presence of...a munching moose! DREAD!

Stay tune for Part Three: THE GATHERING and is that a DOG, Little
Wolf???
