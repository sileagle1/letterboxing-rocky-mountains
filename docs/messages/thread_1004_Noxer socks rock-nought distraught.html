Noxer socks rock-nought distraught

From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-02 20:22:42 UTC
Well folks....we got close again this year...but no Bullet for y.

One of the guys that was searching the frisbee golf course since
yesterday afternoon was checking a random tree for woodpecker holes.
It seems he got discouraged and kicked a bread-loaf size rock that
flew a few feet because it was a fake rock made of styrofoam and
sand. He looked inside and found our beloved Bullet.

With two more days of clues to go he admitted it was pure luck that
he kicked that particular rock out of the thousands out there.

In the radio interview he said that he started searching the course
because he knew Cobra and Shark were frisbee golf disk companies.

And JeLyBean, #13 was Hit the Polling Place, which refers to the
fact that we don't have actual frisbee golf goals, they are just
Poles put up and decorated with streamers that the golfers have to
Hit.

I thought the clues were really good this year; they even were
making a play on the theme Disco Do It and Disk Golf. I had a great
time, mostly because I got to share this search with the TEAM.
Thanks everybody.....we'll get it next year,

y-nought, who can finally breathe deep and start thinking about some
letterbox clues he's been working on.
