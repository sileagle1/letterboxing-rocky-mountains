So, I've been getting a LOT of emails this week about one of my New
York boxes, the Eternal Flame, near Buffalo. At first, I thought, wow,
a lot of people went to find my box this week. But as more and more of
these rolled in, with more and more details, I finally pieced together
that there was a gathering near that box, with the Eternal Flame as
sort of a centerpiece for the gathering. Today I got an email from
none other than Green Tortuga himself, a veritable letterboxing
celebrity. How strange to have one of my boxes picked out as a focal
point for a gethering. This ever happen to anyone?

BTW, Eternal Flame is cool; not the box, but the incredible natural
phenomenon. Flaming waterfall. No joke. If you're ever near Buffalo,
be sure to check it out.
