From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-05-23 13:32:56 UTC
This is for all you lurkers out there, too! Don't be shy! If you
letterbox you are on the invite list! As well as any non-boxing
friends and family you would care to bring along.

I was just wondering how many of you all would be interested in meeting
at the Ren Faire on Sunday, June 10 for a little stamping, exchanging,
piping and pie? Anyone? Anyone?

You do not have to wear a costume (but it's more fun than you think),
no one will talk funny at you, you will not be embarrassed in public.
But you'll have a lovely day, get some boxes, do some exchanges, you
can eat a turkey leg and/or a lovely cream puffy thingie and watch
knights knock each other senseless, bow or curtsy to the king and queen
and see exactly what a pirate wears.

Es, looking for a show of hands
