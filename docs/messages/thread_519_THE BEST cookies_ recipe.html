BUCKEYES

1 Cup Butter
2 Cups Peanut Butter
5 Cups powdered sugar
1 8 oz pkg Chocolate Chips
Bar Parafin

Combine first 3 ingredients and roll into balls. Heat Choc Chips in
double boiler ( I just use regular pan carefully) until melted add
paraffin. Dip balls in chocolate enough to make an eye. Cool in fridge.
