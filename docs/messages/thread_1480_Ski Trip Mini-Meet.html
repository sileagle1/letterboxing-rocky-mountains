Ski Trip Mini-Meet

From: Wisconsin Hiker (kotlarek@wi.rr.com) | Date: 2008-03-09 14:26:38 UTC
We just wanted to say THANK YOU! to the CO letterboxers that came out
to meet us last night. It was a bit snowy, but the atmosphere inside
the quaint restaurant was lively. We had fun doing exchanges (we got
our 500th exchange last night!), stamping in the fantastic event stamp
that Front Range Hiker created, pasing around various cooties and
sharing lots of boxing stories.

The food was great and it was nice to make some new friends in this
part of the country. If any of you come to the Milwaukee area of
Wisconsin, please let us know, so we can get together again!

If you'd like to see a group photo from the mini-meet, I posted one in
the Photos section of this message board. Here's a direct link to it:
