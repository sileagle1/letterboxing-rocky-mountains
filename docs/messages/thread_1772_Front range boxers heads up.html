Okay so there was a reporter. I talked to her, because I knew she was going to do the article anyway. The article is not online, in the RHweekly for here in Loveland. I hope you all can take action to protect your boxes.
Again I am sorry, the only thing she sent me for proof reading was the actual article and not the pictures.
