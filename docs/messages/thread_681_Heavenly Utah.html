From: dixssps (Dixieh7@msn.com) | Date: 2005-05-20 03:41:57 UTC
Hi Ed!

Morning Glory Bridge is totally awesome, and we saw it in the
morning! I have a few more pics I can email you if you like, I
didn't want to upload too many to this site.

Kat, I have many from Moab that you may like too, do you want me to
email them to you? I have a few great ones from the Fiery Furnace!
Plus, I got some wild flower pics. They were so awesome last
weekend, all in bloom! We loved them! What a beautiful place to
live!

Ed, we did find Rock Crawler but I have to tell you that we knew
where it was without the directions because we ran into A-Bear and
crew while they were placing it! It was too funny! We wondered if
there is a special designation for finding a box WHILE it's being
planted! We did have our friends stamp in and it's still there, but
we led them to it. Did you get the other two at Dead Horse Point?
We walked along the opposite trail a way and I got a fantastic
picture of a tree growing out of the rock ledge. We also stopped at
one on the way out of the park at a rest stop and we couldn't
believe how many people walked over to where we were trying to
discreetly stamp and see what we were up to. I mean, we're in the
middle of next to nowhere and people went out of there way to walk
nearly on top of us! GADS! But, we were careful and waited them
out! Too funny! People have to see if they're missing something!

Now Ed, when are you coming to this side of the mountains to box?
Oh yes, is there still plans for get together at the Ren Fest?

Hugs, Dixie Peach
