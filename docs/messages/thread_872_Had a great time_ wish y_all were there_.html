Had a great time, wish y'all were there!

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2005-09-20 04:01:09 UTC
I don't even know where to begin! That was DANG fun! From the moment
Silent Wings arrived with Peach and Cappy on Thursday until the last
goodbye to the last letterboxer, Boxer Lover, on Sunday night it was a
non-stop smile-a-thon for me. Y'all are seriously the coolest grownups
I have ever met. Anyone who will pick up a hitchhiker sitting on top
of a roll of TP is my friend forever. I won't soon forget the sight of
people wandering around the yard looking under flamingos and in coffins
for letterboxes. Suh.. WEET! And I also will never forget the sight
of Skeetch coming out of the bushes carrying that coffin in the state
park where Peach and Cappy had hidden it! WOOT! Arrrrr!!

Chocolate silk pies... peach cobbler... mmmmmm!!

If anyone ever needs the perfect helper just give a shout to Silent
Wings. She just bustles around doing everything that needs doing with
a smile. I never would have been ready without all her help. Plus,
she's so sweet about the merciless teasing we gave her about her
perfect stamping techniques! And all the time we were teasing her we
were lining up to have her ink the stamp for OUR books because she does
it so well. Of course, we all owe her for life, but, man those stamps
look good! Good enough. hehehehehe.

I was so tired on the last flight home tonight then I remembered
sitting around the old school stamping into Napoleon Dynamite and all
of us giggling and having so much fun that a passing stranger asked us
if we were rolling a joint. Which made me chuckle out loud in the dark
plane and the long day wasn't so long anymore.

The pictures are making me start to smile all over again. Thanks for
making it such a great weekend. I honestly can't wait until the next
time!

Kat, we missed you and there were lots of sad sounds when people found
out you weren't coming. And more sad sounds when they found out how
sick you were! Get well soon and come and play on Mount Doom!

Es, wandering off to bed with a goofy grin on her face
