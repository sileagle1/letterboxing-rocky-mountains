Aljan hit the points I would mention - Red Rocks park and ampitheater and
Lookout Mountain / Buffalo Bill's - there are some other cool things to see
- Might hit the Brown Palace in downtown for some historic factor, or the
Molly Brown house.

If you are the museum sort, the Denver Museum of Nature and Science is
awesome - and has one of the very best views of Denver from their second
floor (city park with downtown and the mountains beyond) - that gave ME a
"wow" and I live here! :)

When you know your agenda, please let us know - we might be able to do a
little mini-gather to meet you along your way?

RE: [RMLetterboxing] Vsiting Denver

From: mlanaghen (mlanaghen@comcast.net) | Date: 2010-02-05 10:05:22 UTC-07:00
I would make a trip up to Boulder if you get a chance in fact, a great day
jaunt would be Boulder, Estes Park (go to the top of Trail Ridge Road), and
then you could come back the same way, or go around the loop and come back
through Winter Park Assuming Trail Ridge is open by then.



Beautiful drive, great views the Boulder Mall on Pearl Street is always
fun, and there are great boxes all along the way if you were so inclined.



If youre not inclined to do a day jaunt (the one described above would be a
FULL day), you could still get up to Boulder and back for a day of
adventure stop at the Davidson Mesa overlook (on hwy 36 about 1 mile past
McCaslin exit on the right side of the road its not well marked, but its
a beautiful view of the whole front range Im hoping to plant a box there,
this will motivate me to get it done before May!).



And definitely let us know if youre up for a mini-meet.

Chica (Martha)
