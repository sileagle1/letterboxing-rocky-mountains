Announcing The Grand Grand Lake Gathering!

From: Aljan Sundance (aljansundance@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-02-11 19:09:07 UTC-08:00
I know that it is early but I also know how rediculously busy everyone's lives get, particularly in the summertime!

So...

I announce that the Catamounts and I are conspiring to hold "The Grand Grand Lake Gathering" here in... uhhhh... Grand Lake on the third weekend in August! My house will be open with a nice hot fire for roasting marshmallows, cold drinks for those of you who are parched from driving, the hot tub nice and hot for those of you that care to participate, ground aplenty that is ripe for the pitching of tents and a bit of space inside for anyone that may care to crash on Friday night. No extra beds though!

And THEN on Saturday morning, also here at my place, will be a nice hot BBQ, again, cold drinks and other such libations to share to bolster up your strength for some boxing. Oh and then there would be the exchanging of stamps, chatter and a special event stamp!

I will send out an email in the future that will have some recomendations for different types and price ranges of places to stay in the area.

Let's box people!

Aljan
