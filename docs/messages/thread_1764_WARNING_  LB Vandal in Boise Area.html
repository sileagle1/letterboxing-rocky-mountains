WARNING: LB Vandal in Boise Area

From: Kelly (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-12-28 04:00:20 UTC
I received an urgent e-mail from Capperpillar this weekend warning me of a recent vandalism of several of my boxes. The perpetrator appears to be taking the stamps out of my boxes and is leaving their signature on the cover of my logbooks that says "Firecracker was here." They are leaving behind the logbook and the box but the stamps are now gone from my "The Buzz: Starbucks" box and my "No Fishing" box both located in Eagle, Idaho.

I will be pulling these boxes and checking on others in the area this week to see how many of them have been attacked. I am considering the options I have to secure my boxes and will update my boxes as soon as I decide the best course of action. It may come to pass that I remove all clues and only provide them to established boxers that I know or have knowledge of. I am asking anyone who finds one of my boxes in this condition to please pull the box and contact me. I will update the box status and will find a way to hook up to retrieve my box. I was hoping this type of theft would not happen in Idaho after hearing about the vandalism back east but it looks like we are no longer a safe and friendly boxing town that we once were. It only takes one vandal to ruin it for all of us.

Thanks for your help. Keep your eagle eyes open for whoever this thief is. You can bet the Pei's of Adventure will not take this lying down! Blackvelvetrav
