Kathy -

I am the placer of the Alchemist's Lizard. In fact, I just got an
email yesterday saying someone found it this weekend, and it was just
fine.

Maybe here is what happened: The original Lizard was taken and we re-
carved the stamp and re-placed the box. Now it is in a different
place. Maybe that's why you couldn't find it? But as far as I am
aware, it is happy in its little grotto where it now sits. Please let
me know as you get more info on it. I wouldn't want it to be in a
sensitive area. If you don't like where it is placed, we would be
happy to move it to a better location.
