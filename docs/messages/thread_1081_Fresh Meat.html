I'm starting a series called "West Elk Loop." I've prepared most of them; when I'm finished up, I hope to begin placing them in May. It's a series that starts in Carbondale, then takes you down to Redstone, Paonia, Black Mesa, up to Crested Butte, and back up to Carbondale. There will be lots and lots!!

I went ahead and placed the first one in the series - a hitch hiker hostel! I just LOVE those! It's chock full of HH's waiting to be wisked away. You'll find the clues to it on LBNA in Garfield county.

Hope some of you can visit it soon and get some HH's exchanged!

Cameo
