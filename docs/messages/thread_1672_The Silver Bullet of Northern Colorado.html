I was wondering if you all may want to help me with our local search
which is a lot like Y's in Durango. We are looking for a rock hidden
somewhere in Northern Colorado. This is a larger black rock with little
green specks on it. I am missing one clue from today and do not have the
last clue word for word, however I will post them tomorrow if the stone
has not been found. Any help would be GREAT! Thanks! Ramdelt

The clues so far have been:

1. Some advice to start out week five.
You have to LOOK CLOSE when you seek.
Think of where we haven't been,
That's where you'll find the blarney this week.

2. The Original King of Late Night times three,
Then just a few paces to the northwest.
These boxes aren't really necessary here,
For this is where the water flows best.

3. High in the sky the watch over the blarney.
During the day, but especially at night.
Most with just one, but some with two.
Blue is what you need to have in your sight.

4. Fred, Barney, Wilma, and Betty.
This foursome nearby watches and checks.
The town, the trees and the path below,
Look for the one with little green specks.

5. All around to the east of the blarney,
Warnings tell you what not to do.
A letter, a line and a post for good measure,
Will prevent financial ruin from coming to you.

6. Some play with, some play without.
But we all have one above our head.
Three up, three down, they scream from the pavilions.
As does the leprechaun resting in his bed.

7. A famous song can tell it all,
Especially when two colors become one.
But a certain lyric will lead the way,
To where the search for the Blarney will be done.

8. Finding the blarney and winning the green
Will surely make your day.
East of Woody and also Toby,
And baby you're on your way.

9. You'd think the beach was to the south,
For what you see up in the sky.
Not a ball, not a Frisbee, not a seagull.
But the place where you hear the waves crash by.

10. Take a big yellow taxi to the scene
The far west side of paradise is peaceful if you know what I mean.

11. (Something like) The artist of Sunflowers who cut off his ear's
last name goes to bed here
Once you find it, everything will become clear.
