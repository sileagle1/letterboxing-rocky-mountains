Having just bought some new blades and finding all of them dull and
uneven I was forced into sharpening them.
It went better than I expected.
I used a very fine grit sandpaper, the black kind, not sure the number
rating but as fine as it gets.
I mostly sharpened the outside but I did the inside too by curling up
the sandpaper and running it along the inside towards the sharp part.
The good news was that they were sharper than when I started but I
still feel bummed to buy new blades that are duller than the ones that
I am trying to replace.
I have seen ads for blades made by MasterCarve. Has anyone tried
those? I have not seen them in any of the stores I go to.
Good Luck,
Nisha
