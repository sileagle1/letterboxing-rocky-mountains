and yet another great day of you know what

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2004-09-08 05:20:17 UTC
I drove home from Denver today and took the long way. Was it
gorgeous today or what?!

Along my journey I finally collared that pesky Onion. Then I sailed
up to Bailey and snuck into the Dragon's Lair. On around the scenic
route until I pulled up to the dock in Breckenridge and discovered
Bears Gone Fishin'.

During the long sea journey I managed to check up on Harry's Heaven,
Twenty Mile and Grizzly Creek, all of which were doing well.
Grizzly Creek got new stamp pads... don't tell the other boxes.

Every single box I looked at today was extremely well re-hidden.
That was really great to see! A pretty large group with kids had
done one of them and you could hardly tell there was a box there.
The sticks and pine needles and rocks were so artfully placed that
it looked totally natural.

It took me over 8 hours to make a 4 hour drive... and it was worth
every minute.

Good times!

Esmerelda, unpacking the pirate ship
