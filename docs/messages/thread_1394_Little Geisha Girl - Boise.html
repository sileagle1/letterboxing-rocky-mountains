Little Geisha Girl - Boise

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-08-25 18:58:55 UTC
Little Geisha Girl was confirmed as pillaged today. Raisin and I ran
by and pulled the box permanently. It was the oddest thing. The
stamp was gone and the logbook was missing but whoever took them left
the box in tact and left behind the logbook pages that had been
stamped in along with the zip lock bags. I have never had that
happen before.

Since the box has seen very little action since being planted
December 4, 2004 I have pulled the box permanently from it's Boise
hiding spot and I have re-carved this stamp and I am moving it to New
York.

Here is the history of the logbook pages that were left behind while
the box was planted in Boise:

Planted: December 4, 2004
Found: December 4, 2004 A-Bear
Found: December 28, 2004 WD'A
Found: August 23, 2005 CW SunSeeker & Jasmine
Found: September 14, 2005 Pete & Wanda
Found: November 26, 2005 Eva & Emma
Found: September 3, 2006 Team Ennis
Found: March 17, 2007 The Sub Club
Moved: August 2007
