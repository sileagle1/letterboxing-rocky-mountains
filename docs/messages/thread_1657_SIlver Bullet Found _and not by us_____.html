From: edwebbe (edwebbe@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-01-29 15:07:11 UTC
Oh, Brother!

Last night, right at dark, the Bullet was found in a fake rock behind
a man-made waterfall. The waterfall is part of the entrance to a
subdivision by our new hospital.

Get this.......the woman who found it said that she thought it would
be cool if the Bullet was hidden in her subdivision so she just
started looking around her neighborhood. Where the rocks are stacked
for the waterfall it looks like a v within a V so she got the idea to
look behind it. Tom Little said that what he meant by v within a V
was actually "W" which stands for Wilson Gulch....where the
subdivision is.

So, even though the clues so far were really very little help at all,
the Bullet was found in almost record time. Which proves how hard it
is to write clues that will make a treasure hunt last for a week.

Personally, I think you guys had better ideas at hiding spots for
this year's Bullet (in the HORN of a Dodge Viper?....brilliant!) than
Tom did.......but you guys are letterboxers and are real pro's at
this.

Thanks for all the FUN!

y-nough
