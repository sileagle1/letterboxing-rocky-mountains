Cascade, Idaho - Box Updates

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2009-06-17 17:13:34 UTC
This past weekend we were camping in Cascade, Idaho and I stopped to check on some of my existing boxes. I am still finding a very disturbing trend of my boxes being left completely out in the open. Many that I checked on were sitting next to their hiding spots without being covered and one naturally did not survive. All I could find was the lid. I am not sure who is being so careless and many of the finders are not officially logging in their finds on Letterboxing.org or Atlasquest.com so I am left to wonder who can't take a matter of minutes to carefully re-hide these boxes. UGH! Here are my updates:

Give a Dog A Bone - Cascade, Idaho http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=42760
6/13/2009 - Box was left uncovered but survived and is alive and well.

Summer Sensation Smiths Ferry
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=8093
6/14/2009 Box was still partially hidden and is alive and well.

Ella the Elf Cascade, Idaho
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=77272
6/12/2009 Box was still hidden and is alive and well.

Cascade Critters Cascade, Idaho (5 Boxes Originally)
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=6506
6/13/2009 ALL OF THESE BOXES WERE FOUND OUT IN THE OPEN. Out of the original five only three are still alive (gee, wonder why).
"Furry Friend" Box # 1 ALIVE
"Prickly Friend" Box # 2 ALIVE
"Tricky Friend" Box # 3 ALIVE
"White Tail Friend" Box # 4 MIA (Only Found Lid) :-(
"Paw Print Friend" Box # 5 MIA

Jay the Fishing Pei Cascade, Idaho
http://www.atlasquest.com/showinfo.html?gBoxId=77277
6/14/2009 Box was still hidden and is alive and well.
