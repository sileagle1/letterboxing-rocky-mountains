From: Silent Doug (silentdoug@letterboxing.info) | Date: 2005-09-14 09:03:44 UTC-04:00
Seems like all the regional LB groups have been getting patches
lately -- Great Lakes and Texas just had some made, and Mid-Atlantic
and Northeast put out theirs earlier this year. They're usually less
than $2 each, and they look great on my pack along with the LbNA patch.

Has anyone given any thought to making a cool logo and patch for this
group? Since I've done a bit of boxing in the area, I'd certainly be
interested in a couple of them.

Let me know if anyone wants to take on the project -- I can direct
you to the other boxers who have had patches made so you can quickly
research providers.
