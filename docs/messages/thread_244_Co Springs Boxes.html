From: RavenWolf (treikya@aol.com) | Date: 2004-06-28 14:58:15 UTC
Hello all -

Me and my family, along with a few friends, had a very WET weekend
LetterBoxing up in the Colorado Springs area.

Esmerelda - we must have just missed you! We saw your stamp at both
Fox Run, and Little Feet on the 26th, and at Bear Creek on the 27th!

We found the Fox Run LB in the same place as Esmerelda and also
stamped in and the stamp is missing. The stamps are also missing at
the Bear Creek LB.

However, Little Feet, The Chantress, & The Cog Railroad all look
good!

Thank you to everyone who planted boxes down in the Springs for us to
find - hopefully we will have better weather next time we are up
there. We attempted to get the Quail Lake, Cutthroat Trout, Pulpit
Rock, and Cascade Falls LB's - we even drove right to them - but the
weather got icky and we had to move on.

~RavenWolf~
