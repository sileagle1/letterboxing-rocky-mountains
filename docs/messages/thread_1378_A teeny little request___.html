A teeny little request...

From: gonefromnj (gonefromnj@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-07-31 14:00:19 UTC
...and I hope you won't think I'm a pain for asking.

When you make minor changes to your clues, can you please make a note
at the top that the clues have been changed as of such-and-such a date?

Let me explain my reasoning: The H-A's just returned from our first
trip to Moab. We started printing out clues way back when so we could
get a jump on where the boxes were and which ones could be done
together, etc. After months of planning, we had about as much figured
out as we could remotely. Right before we left, we took a look online
at all of the clues we had printed out to see if any were noted as
changed or missing. We adjusted our plans as required and printed out
the clues that had changed. HOWEVER, it would have taken forever to
try to figure out if each individual box's clues had changed without
it being noted, so we assumed that any clue not noted hadn't changed.
Unfortunately, in at least one case, we realized after we returned
that the clue had been updated, which was why we never found the box.

If it sounds like my post has "a tone," it isn't meant to. As I said,
it is just a teeny little request and I welcome other's viewpoints on
it.

Also, if you have boxes in Moab and you haven't heard an update from
me, it is just because I haven't gotten to updating everyone yet. We
found many, many boxes while we were there when we weren't distracted
by the amazing scenery. Hopefully we were the last ones who'd never
been to Moab, but if there are any more of you, by all means get out
there!
