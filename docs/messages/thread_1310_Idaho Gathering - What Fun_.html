Idaho Gathering - What Fun!

From: blackvelvetrav (blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com) | Date: 2007-04-09 15:44:33 UTC
What fun the gathering was on Saturday night! I only posted one group
picture on the Atlas Quest site but I do have the entire album on
Kodak Gallery if anyone would like the link sent to them just send me
your actual e-mail address to my regular e-mail account at
blackvelvetrav@yahoo.com I like to use Kodak Gallery because you can
order prints and/or save pictures off of the site.

I am still working on replacement boxes as I continue my Spring
cleaning efforts here in Idaho. Then I have boxes in other states
that need some TLC too. We are looking forward to our next get
together in July that Dragon Rider is hosting. But I am sure I will
see some of you on the trial before then!

Happy Hunting!
Blackvelvetrav



Re: Idaho Gathering - What Fun!

From: Judy (jowagnon@comcast.net) | Date: 2007-04-10 02:54:45 UTC
Indeed it was fun! Dragonrider and I 'boxed all day and then met the
rest of the group at the restaurant. It was great to see old friends
and to meet new 'boxers of the Idaho persuasion - super nice folks!
And, of course, Silent Doug, Letterboxer Extraordinaire, who is not
silent at all. There were exchanges and HHs and personal travelers
and Mongolian food. Blackvelvetrav unleashed a mess of cooties, one
of which infected me via WD'A, but I sent it back East with S.D.,
only to get another one from A-Bear! (We need to give A-Bear stealth
lessons.8~P) She was forgiven, however, for the delicious breakfast
on Sunday morning. On the way home, I dropped off Don and Gwen's
Honor Liberty HH at A-Bear's Evel Knievel letterbox. The HH has had
an amazing history; let's keep it moving. Huge thanks to A-Bear for
putting together the gather! See you all at Dragonrider's gather in
Twin Falls in July!

Boxer Lover
