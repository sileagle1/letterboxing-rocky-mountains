The H-A's have taken another hit: Lemony Letterbox was reported
missing (again). For those of you who found the second incarnation,
the log it was under, which looked as though it hadn't been touched
in years before we came along, is now in the drainage area nearby,
along with just about every other piece of wood from the area. No
sign of any part of the letterbox, though.

I'm taking this better than I expected, in part because I'm getting
used to it, but also because we found another place to hide a
replacement and because I'm feeling like I can carve a better stamp
now than I did back when I did that one. Also, we received good
news on "How's Your Aspen?" and two of our hitchhikers, one of which
is in Massachusetts.

I've got stamps piling up, so I need to get out and hide some new
boxes, including two (soon three) replacements, an Elitch Gardens
box and one featuring a skeleton pirate known as Captain Scapula.
Of course, I will post here whenever anything new goes out.
