Weekend Lbing

From: Lisa Cook (lisadcook@comcast.net) | Date: 2005-09-12 17:28:24 UTC
Hi all,
I found a bunch of boxes this weekend and during the week. Oh and
Saturday got me over the F100 mark...woohoo! I know, I know I still
havent placed any-but I have 6 log books ready to go and 6 stamps I
have drawn/just havent carved them yet.
Found King of all he Surveys and Waterbearer. (Ive still only found
about 1/2 of that series so far). Went to Buffalo Bill-WOW-Im still in
Awe of that stamp!!! Also Ive lived here 20 years and never bill to BB
Grave before. What a view! Also went to Jennys to find Yes. You all
need to go to this one. What a great story and my type of
cafe/restaurant. They had the best cream of mushroom soup Ive ever
had. I had the burger (per Aljans suggestion) and my friend had the
vege enchiladas...both excellent. There are only a few pages left in
the log book (maybe 4 or 5). The waiter wasnt too familiar with lbing
and asked us about it (he said the other times people had come in they
were too busy in the rest for him to ask about the sport (addiction).)
So maybe we will have a new lber in the area! He seemed pretty
interested.
On Sunday I went to Coors Beer-my first Silver Eagle box. Very cute.
also on Sunday went to Enginerd. Nice hiding place-watch out for those
cyclists!

On Wednesday I also found Smiley in Highlands Ranch and the 3 Daniels
Park boxes--nice walk and great views!

Im looking forward to seeing everyone on Saturday and finding some
boxes on the way over the mtns.

Now Im off to buy the ingredients for PIE.

TDG
