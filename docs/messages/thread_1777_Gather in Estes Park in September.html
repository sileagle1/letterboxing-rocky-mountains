Gather in Estes Park in September

From: mlanaghen (mlanaghen@comcast.net) | Date: 2010-02-28 22:11:23 UTC-07:00
I've been thinking about a Rocky Mountain gather of some kind. I know some
of you are already working on something, but wanted to throw this out there
as an idea.



I was thinking a day-long event (maybe 10am to 4 pm) at the YMCA of the
Rockies in Estes Park. Probably a Saturday in September. If people wanted
to, they could come up before hand and stay at the Y (facilities range from
wonderful private cabins, to dormitory-style rooms), or camp in nearby Rocky
Mountain National Park or at any of a dozen or so RV parks in Estes.



I thought people could send boxes in advance of the event, and we could
plant all over the valley, as well as doing a series at the Y itself. And
then on that full-day, we could have a few classes (basic carving, advanced
carving, custom ink pads, codex logbooks, Postal Letterboxing 101, LTC 101 -
just a few ideas, and, of course, we'd have to find folks willing to teach
all the classes!).



I'm sure we'd get some event boxes shipped in, and could create some, and
also have time for meet-and-greet and exchanges, etc.



Plus, events/activities for kids during certain times of the day as well.
And depending on interest, child-care so moms and dads can go boxing.



Then folks could also stay that night at the Y, or drive home, depending on
their preferences.



Anyone interested? I'm thinking Saturday, the 18th of September. (Labor Day
is the 6th - so this would be two weekends later.) Weigh-in on timing too.



Thanks for your feedback,

Chica (Martha)
