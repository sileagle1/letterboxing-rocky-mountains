Now- my yearly plea for help:

For those of you that joined this past year, Snowdown is a Cabin-
Fever-Reliever in Durango, Colorado. There are LOTS of events
including a parade and, my favorite, The Search For The Silver
Bullet! And this year the theme is Jolly Roger Snowdown!!!

http://www.snowdown.org/poster07.htm
(Yarrrr!)

The search is a week-long treasure hunt where they announce five
clues on the radio each day, starting Monday, and usually ending on
Saturday when someone finds the prize. The clues are really
hard...until the very end where they basically tell you where it is.
So...if nobody minds, I'll post the clues each day when I get the
time, and you folks can use your noggins to help me find the loot. It
means $250 for me, and pride and prestige for the letterboxers who
help me win this year! Plus, I'll post a picture of myself in my
ridiculous pirate hat!

Last year I was looking around on the disc golf course a half hour
before the silver bullet was found under a fake rock that I must have
walked right by. And the year before I was maybe two feet from the
out house that the bullet was hidden in. All because of the help I
got from RMLetterboxers. So, like JeLy says, this could be THE YEAR!

If this is a little bit too much off topic for some of you, please e-
mail me privately and I will find another venue to beg for help.
Really, I don't want to bug anybody.

Shiver me timbers!

y-nought
