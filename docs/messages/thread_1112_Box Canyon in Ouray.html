Box Canyon in Ouray

From: splendidbuccaneer (splendidbuccaneer@yahoo.com) | Date: 2006-06-05 19:47:23 UTC
Skeetch and I just got home from a short but lovely camping trip in
Ouray. We went to the falls this morning and wandered over to check up
on the box. Sadly, we found only the container with the lid removed
under the tree. A baggie with inky paper towel was tossed higher up
the hill. I wandered all over the hill hoping to find the stamp and
journal with no luck. We're guessing someone was either seen while
getting/rehiding it or didn't cover it from view and it was found by a
hiker on the trail. How sad. It's been there for four years! I was
more upset with finding this box missing than any of mine so far!

Es, who did a little ranting on the trail when she found the empty box
