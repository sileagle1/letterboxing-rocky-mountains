Re: Idaho boxes on hikes?

From: sileagle1 (sileagle@windstream.net) | Date: 2012-06-20 23:50:19 UTC
Another great thing to do while in Idaho is bike the Route Of The Hiawatha. Not sure if there are letterboxes involved but it is the most scenic ride I have ever done. It is on a Rail to Trail and goes over several high trestles and a tunnel over 1.5 miles long. It is near Lookout Pass Ski Area near Montana border:

http://www.ridethehiawatha.com/

Have fun!

Silver Eagle
