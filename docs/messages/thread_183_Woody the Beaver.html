very well hidden and made it very hard to find with so many people
near the area! Bending down to tie your shoe always works!

Debbi, keep your boxes coming - we have enjoyed them! Thank you!

Thank you so much for your compliments on our carving! =] My daughter
actually carved that stamp (she is 13). She has really taken to this...
she loves to carve them. And, I have to say, I am very impressed with
what she has done. =] Now, keep in mind, we have pictures that we use..
we do not design from scratch =]

I had the same thought about the location. But, like I said, I couldn't
find anywhere to put it. When we originally had the thought for the
stamp was when we went there to get Alchemeists Lizard... on the map we
saw beaver pond. Thinking it would be a more wooded area, we carved the
stamp, boxed it up and went back to hid it... without having ever
actually been to that part of the park. I may have to go back and move
it at some point, but after the run in with the snakes that day... i
think I may have to wait a while!

Debbi
